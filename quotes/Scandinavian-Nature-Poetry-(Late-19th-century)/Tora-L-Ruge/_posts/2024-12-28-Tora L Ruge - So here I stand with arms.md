So here I stand, with arms outstretched,
Embracing all, my heart bewitched,
In nature's folds, I find my home,
Among the wild, I roam and roam.

The world alive, a breath, a sigh,
In every moment, I learn to fly,
For in this dance with earth and sky,
I find the meaning, and I know why.


- Tora L Ruge
