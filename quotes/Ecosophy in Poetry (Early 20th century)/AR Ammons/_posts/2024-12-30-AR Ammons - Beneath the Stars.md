Beneath the Stars

Lying on earth,
a canvas of night,
watching the stars
fall into silence,
each one a wish, a hope
punctuating the dark,
reminding us,
each heartbeat,
each breath,
we are part of this,
a universe vast and intimate.

- AR Ammons
