The meadow blooms with golden lines,
Through grasses lush and green,
As bees hum sweet among the vines,
On this serene, sylvan scene.

With every petal kissed by dew,
And sunlight's warm embrace,
The heart of hearts, in nature true,
Finds peace in sacred space.

- Thomas Hardy
