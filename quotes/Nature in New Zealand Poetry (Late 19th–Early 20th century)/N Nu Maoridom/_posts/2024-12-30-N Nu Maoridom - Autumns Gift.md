Autumn's Gift

When leaves of gold lay soft and still,
And whispers of the cool winds thrill,
In autumn's grasp, I find anew,
A palette rich, of red and blue.

The harvest moon, in gentle glow,
Invites the night to dance with flow,
In every scent of fallen leaf,
A bittersweet, yet sweet relief.

- N Nu Maoridom
