Bird-Worthy

     As if less would hold,
        the other's feature—
  dignity, richer soft
glides like a power
  of nature’s boon, entwined
  ivy; note how we
  fasten ourselves to
  cadence and the long
  thoughts of soft ambiance.
   ----

- Marianne Moore
