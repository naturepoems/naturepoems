The wind is impatient,  
waiting on summer,  
bright grains of truth,
breaking open flowers,
the gentle chaos of green,
ataxas axons firing  
like lightning  
in the forest.
The seasons begin  
straining for laughter, the breadth of a smile,
catching color on soft winds.

— Diane di Prima
