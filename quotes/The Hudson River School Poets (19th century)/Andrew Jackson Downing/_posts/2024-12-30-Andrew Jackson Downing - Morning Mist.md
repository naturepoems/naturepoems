Morning Mist
A veil of mist enfolds the dawn,
Like a breath held in delight,
As the waking world is softly drawn
Into the warmth of light.
The trees stand tall with whispered dreams,
While birds begin to sing,
Celebrating nature's gentle schemes,
Her beauty in the spring.

- Andrew Jackson Downing
