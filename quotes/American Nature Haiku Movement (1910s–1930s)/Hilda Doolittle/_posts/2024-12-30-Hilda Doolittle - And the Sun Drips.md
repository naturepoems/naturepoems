And the sun drips  
       down the shadowy walls
       in jeweled drops  
       of rich colors.
       Follow the quick
       feet of shadows.
       Hear the hidden whispers
       in the twilight.
       The stars are eating green,
       the brown earth waits.

- Hilda Doolittle
