A Home in the Trees
I found my home beneath the arches of trees,
branches weaving a lullaby,
roots digging deep into the earth's warmth.
The whispers of the leaves caressed my ears,
a sacred trust unfurled,
a ceremony of the wild.
In this sanctuary, I was whole.

- Robert Bly
