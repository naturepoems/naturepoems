The Earth Speaks
If you listen
with your heart,
the earth will speak to you.
In her soil, lies her wisdom,
a truth resting on the leaves,
floors of moss, over granite stones,
a chorus waiting for your ears to hear,
a dance between life and death.

- Robert Bly
