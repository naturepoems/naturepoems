The Rain

   I felt the rain.
   I walked with it, 
   I was embroidered all
   with the mist and wet,

   Sea-grass and breezes,  
   Salt wind and the zahming  
   And the drop and the leaf, 
   All rocks and mist  
   And the glitter of sea and sky
   Who could understand this light!


- HD
