The lilac's perfume in spring,
A tender touch from the earth,
Each bloom is a hymn of life,
Each petal a memory reborn.

Butterflies dance over blossoms,
Nature's art in motion, pure,
A reminder of fleeting moments,
Yet eternity blooms in color's glow.

- Walt Whitman
