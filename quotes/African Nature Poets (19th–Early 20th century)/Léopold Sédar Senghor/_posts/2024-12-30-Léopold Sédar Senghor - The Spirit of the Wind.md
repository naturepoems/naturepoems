The Spirit of the Wind
Oh, gentle wind,
You carry the voices,
Of lands far and wide,
In your breath, the choices.

Through fields and forests,
Your whispers reach the soul,
In the language of nature,
You make the spirit whole.


- Léopold Sédar Senghor
