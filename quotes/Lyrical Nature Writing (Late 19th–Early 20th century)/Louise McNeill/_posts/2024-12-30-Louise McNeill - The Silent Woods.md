The Silent Woods

In the silent woods,
   Where the shadows are grand,
   I walk in the stillness,
   And I lose my command.

For the trees hold their stories,
   In the hush of their leaves,
   And the whispers of nature,
   Fill the air with reprieves.


- Louise McNeill
