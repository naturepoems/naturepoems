Behold the mountains, vast and grand,
With craggy peaks that pierce the sky;
Within this wild and lush expanse,
Our souls find peace to fly.
The rivers winding, reflecting light,
With laughter in their flow,
In Nature’s canvas painted bright,
Where beauty's heart does glow.

- William Gilpin
