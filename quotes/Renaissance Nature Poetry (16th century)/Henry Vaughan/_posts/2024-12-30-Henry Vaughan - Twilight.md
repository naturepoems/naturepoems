In twilight’s glow, the world turns slow,  
The evening star shines pure and bright;  
The rhythm of the day does flow,  
Echoing secrets of the night.

In nature's arms, I lose my way,  
With gently sighing leaves in tune;  
Each moment crafty like a play,  
Beneath the watching, silver moon.

--- "Twilight"

- Henry Vaughan
