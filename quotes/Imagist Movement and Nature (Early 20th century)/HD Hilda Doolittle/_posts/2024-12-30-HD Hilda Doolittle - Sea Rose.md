Sea Rose

O wilding rose,
    leaf, and thorn!
It seems you sprout
        on your float,
    o’er the green and mud—
    in light’s hold:
Let them come,
    those who have cried
        for colors,
        as they leave darkness,
ready to gasp,
        vibrant with leaves.

- HD Hilda Doolittle
