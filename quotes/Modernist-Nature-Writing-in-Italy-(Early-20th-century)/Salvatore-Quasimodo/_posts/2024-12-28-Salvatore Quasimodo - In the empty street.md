In the empty street  
The trees smile in ivory,  
Glancing at the golden outlines  
Of homes abandoned in the dusk.
Nature,  
As my witness, takes the hand,  
Restoring the light of the fields  
Where the warm air softens the night.

- Salvatore Quasimodo
