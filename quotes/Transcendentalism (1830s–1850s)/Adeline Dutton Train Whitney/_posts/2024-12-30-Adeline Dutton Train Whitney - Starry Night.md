Starry Night
A starry night so calm and still,
The world sleeps under nature’s will,
The moonlight dances on the ground,
In silver hues that wrap around.
Each star a wish, a silent prayer,
A piece of heaven’s gentle care;
Oh, to gaze at the beauty above,
And find in starlight the warmth of love.

— Adeline Dutton Train Whitney
