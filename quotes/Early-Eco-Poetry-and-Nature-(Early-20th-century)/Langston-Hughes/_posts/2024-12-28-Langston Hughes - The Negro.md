The Negro Mother

I’m the Negro Mother.
   I’m the one who said,
“Let there be light!”
   But the light fell on blind eyes.

I’m the one who stood alone when men
   Were mighty and dead.
I’m the one who fought to keep
   The risky hope alive.

- Langston Hughes
