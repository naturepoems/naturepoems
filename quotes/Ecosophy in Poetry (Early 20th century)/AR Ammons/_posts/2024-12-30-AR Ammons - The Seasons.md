The Seasons

Four movements,
like a symphony,
each sound brushing softly,
a stroke of colors,
a patchwork quilt of life.
Birds return,
leaving whispers
a tapestry unfurling,
early blooms
expand like laughter.
Each shift awakens
new dreams.

- AR Ammons
