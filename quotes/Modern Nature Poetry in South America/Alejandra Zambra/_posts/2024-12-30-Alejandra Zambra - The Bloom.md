A single flower blooms,
a splash of color against
the dullness of the day.

Its petals unfurl
like secrets revealed,
fragile yet defiant,
a reminder of resilience.

Nature doesn’t hurry,
but waits in patience
for spring’s golden embrace.

- Alejandra Zambra
