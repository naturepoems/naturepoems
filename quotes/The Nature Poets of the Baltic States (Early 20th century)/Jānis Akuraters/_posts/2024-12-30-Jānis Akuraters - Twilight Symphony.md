The cicadas’ hum is heavy,
A symphony of late,
As evening wraps soft shadows,
In twilight’s warm estate.

The stars begin their twinkling,
A sprinkle of pure light,
A moment of pure magic,
In the cloak of night.

- Jānis Akuraters
