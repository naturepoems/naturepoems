Dream Variations

To fling my arms wide
   In the face of the sun,
   Dance! Whirl!
   Whirl! Whirl!
   Till the quick day is done.

Rest at pale evening...
   A tall tree...
   Night coming tenderly
   Black like me.

- Langston Hughes
