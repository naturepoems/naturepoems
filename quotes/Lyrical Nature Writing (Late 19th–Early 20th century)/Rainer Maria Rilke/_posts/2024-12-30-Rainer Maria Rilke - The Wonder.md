The Wonder

Oh, wonder of our being,
    in every drop of dew,
    every explosive bloom,
    in every sigh and heartbeat —

    life spills into our hands,
    yet never holds them long,
    can we not die in these shadows
    where the world holds us close?

—Rainer Maria Rilke
