Wild Grapes

What is the taste of the grape  
    from a wild vine?
Its elements are bitter,
    charming, compelling,
    useful in perfume,  
    choking in color.  

Is it the perfect moment
    of splendor with a sense
    of breach?  
    Can a single swallow  
    maintain this?
