The petals of the Evening —  
Are lavishly afloat —  
Old shadows of the Robin —  
All that we Left To Fate —  

And there — like fingers touching --  
Could it be true, we care?  
The gazing lakes, delighted —  
With beauty everywhere!  


- Emily Dickinson
