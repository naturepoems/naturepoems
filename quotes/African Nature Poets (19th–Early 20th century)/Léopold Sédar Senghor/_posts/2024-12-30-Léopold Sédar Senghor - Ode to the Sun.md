Ode to the Sun
Golden sun, you rise,
Over oceans and mountains,
In your light, the spirits dance,
Blindly chasing shadows,

In the song of the earth,
You wander through the skies,
The pulse of life,
In your warm embrace.


- Léopold Sédar Senghor
