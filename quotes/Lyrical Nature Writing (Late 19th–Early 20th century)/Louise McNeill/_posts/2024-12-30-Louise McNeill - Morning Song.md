Morning Song

In the hour of dawn,
   When the earth wakes anew,
   And the mist drapes the mountains,
   In soft silvery hue.

I sing to the rivers,
   I sing to the trees,
   To the birds and the blossoms,
   And the whispering breeze.


- Louise McNeill
