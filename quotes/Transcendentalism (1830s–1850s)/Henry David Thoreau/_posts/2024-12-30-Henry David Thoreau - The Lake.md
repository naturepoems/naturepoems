The lake, a mirror to the sky,
Reflects a world of blue and gold,
Where every ripple sings a sigh,
And tales of ages yet untold.

There, daffodils dance in the breeze,
As nature points her verdant brush,
Each flower and leaf, crafted with ease,
Invite us to linger, and hush.

- Henry David Thoreau
