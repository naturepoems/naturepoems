The mountains loomed,  
a sentinel of ages,  
their crags etched in time,  
a testament to resilience,  
while valleys cradle life  
in a tender embrace.  

Here, the air whispers  
a tale of the ancients,  
each echo a footprint  
a path where the wild things play.

- Nadia Słobodzian
