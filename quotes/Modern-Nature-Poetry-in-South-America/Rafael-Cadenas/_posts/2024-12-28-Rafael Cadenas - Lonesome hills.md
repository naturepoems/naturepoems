Lonesome hills 
 cradle the sunsets,
 laying the warmth of day 
 to rest,

where every flame fades,
 giving space 
to the soft embrace of the night.
The stars witness
 our forgotten dreams,
 their shimmer a calling
to the depths of the soul.


- Rafael Cadenas
