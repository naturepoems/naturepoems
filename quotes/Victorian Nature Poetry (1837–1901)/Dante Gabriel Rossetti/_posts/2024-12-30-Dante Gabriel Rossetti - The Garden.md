The Garden
In the inner cool of shades, 
Where the silent bluebird sings, 
The lark her merriest parade, 
In the garden of sacred things.

Blooming silence with shadows thick, 
Dew drops glisten like stars, 
In haze where thought is stirred, 
A world untouched by wars.

- Dante Gabriel Rossetti
