The rain dances,
    A gentle melody,
On the rooftops,
   Each drop a note,
A hymn for the earth,
      Reviving the soil,
With life and vigor,
   As puddles form,
Like mirrors,
   In which the world
   Comes alive.

- Sylvia Plath
