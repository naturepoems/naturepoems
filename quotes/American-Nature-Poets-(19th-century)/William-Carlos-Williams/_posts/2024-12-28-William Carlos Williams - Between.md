Between walls

Between the walls
of what I know,

I hear the sound of
birdsong in the trees.

My heart beats faster
with the rustling
afternoon breeze,

and I slip into the
skin of the day,

alive, aware
of what has been

and what shall come.

- William Carlos Williams
