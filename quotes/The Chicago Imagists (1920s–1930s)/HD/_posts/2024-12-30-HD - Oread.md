Oread

Whirl up, sea—
whirl your pointed pines,
plash your great pines
on our rocks,
hurl your green over us,
cover us with your pools of fir.  

We can dare,
we can dare to be free,
dare we feed; 
we can feed your hot eels,
your hot sap,
your pines on the hill.  


- HD
