A wild rose blooms by the lake,  
Creating fragrance wide and deep,  
In the morning's soft awake,  
Where the peace of beauty sweeps.  
All around, the echoes swell,  
To the thoughts long kept in sighs,  
Nature's voice, a timeless bell,  
Through the lands where love never dies.

- James Hogg
