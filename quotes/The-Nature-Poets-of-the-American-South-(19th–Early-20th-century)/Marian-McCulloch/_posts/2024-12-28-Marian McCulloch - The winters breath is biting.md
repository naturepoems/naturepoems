The winter’s breath is biting,  
  The world is wrapped in gray,  
  Yet beneath the earth lies dreaming,  
  The bloom of spring to play.

  The branches bare and aching,  
  Will burst with joy anew,  
  For every season carries,  
  A promise born in view.

- Marian McCulloch
