The dawn awakes the sleeping fields,
With dew-kissed crowns of amber grass,
In nature’s pulse, a beauty yields,
A moment cherished, never passed.

As colors bloom and shadows wane,
The earth rejoices in the light,
Each heartbeat echoes love’s refrain,
A dance of day, a sweet delight.

- John Rea
