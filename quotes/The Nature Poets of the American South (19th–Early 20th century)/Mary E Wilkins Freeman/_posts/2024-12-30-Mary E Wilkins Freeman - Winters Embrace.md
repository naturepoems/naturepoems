When winter's chill has cast its veil,
And snowflakes softly drift,
The world wears white, so calm, so pale,
A peaceful, lovely gift.

The pines stand tall, their branches dressed,
In purity and grace,
In nature's arms, I am at rest,
In this, my sacred place.
