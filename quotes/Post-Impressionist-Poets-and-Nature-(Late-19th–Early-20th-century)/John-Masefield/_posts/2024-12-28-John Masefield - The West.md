The West Wind

I will go to the West Wind;
There it wafts to the river strip;
I will wade to the ocean beyond,
In the stream where sailors dip,
'Til the waves throw foam against the sky,
'Til the dawn comes up from the sea, like a sigh.

- John Masefield
