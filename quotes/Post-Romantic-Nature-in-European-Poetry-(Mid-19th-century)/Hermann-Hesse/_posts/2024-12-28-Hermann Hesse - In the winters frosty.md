In the winter's frosty air,
The world dons a cloak of white,
Each flake that falls, unique, so rare,
A wondrous artwork, pure and bright.

The crunch beneath my foot, a song,
The quiet speaks of deep repose,
In nature's embrace, I belong,
In every breath, the stillness grows.

- Hermann Hesse
