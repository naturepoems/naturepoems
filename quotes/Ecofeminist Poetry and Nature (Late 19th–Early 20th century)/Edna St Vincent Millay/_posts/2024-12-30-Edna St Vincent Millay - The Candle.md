THE CANDLE

If I had but a candle-spire,
   To match this roughened sky,
And lift above the damper anne,
   As dusk gives way to cry.
   
My flicker would seek out the stars,
   Like threads upon a vine,
That stitches each to tethered point,
   and light unto the line.

- Edna St Vincent Millay
