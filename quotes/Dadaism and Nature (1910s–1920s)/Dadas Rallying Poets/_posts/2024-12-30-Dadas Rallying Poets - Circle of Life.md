Circle of Life
Nature spins in cycles vast,
Where beginnings meet with ends,
In every life a whisper lingers,
As each moment transcends.

Beneath the stars, stories gather,
In the soil, dreams take flight,
Nurtured by the hands of time,
In the circle of day and night.


- Dadas Rallying Poets
