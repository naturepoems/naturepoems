In Bloom

There among thorns
the roses burst,
a riot of colors,
a celebration of what lives,
a promise of freedom
in fragrant hues!
With each petal,
a heartbeat may occur,
a blush of life;
a delicate strength,
every glance drawn deeper,
into the rhythm of soaring blooms,
a dance of tender joy.

Silvina Ocampo

- Silvina Ocampo
