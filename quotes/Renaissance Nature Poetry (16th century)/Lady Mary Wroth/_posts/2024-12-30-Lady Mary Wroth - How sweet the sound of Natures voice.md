How sweet the sound of Nature's voice,
That speaks in every breeze,
In sunlight’s kiss or rain’s rejoice,
In rustling of the trees.

From mountain high to valley low,
Her beauty we embrace,
Each creature great, and each one small,
Reflects her gentle grace.

- Lady Mary Wroth
