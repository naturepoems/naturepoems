When clouds drift in the heavens,
   As the moon takes her way,
The heart, in sweet reckonings,
   Finds its solace at the bay.

Oh! the waves, they softly gather,
   And the earth is wide and free,
In the peace that time would rather,
   Keep alive eternally.

- Matthew Arnold
