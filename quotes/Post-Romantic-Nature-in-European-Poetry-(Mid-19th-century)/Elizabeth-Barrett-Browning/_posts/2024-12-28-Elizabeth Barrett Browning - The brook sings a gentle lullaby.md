The brook sings a gentle lullaby, 
As it dances over stones, bright and clear; 
With every splash, it weaves a tale, 
Of the mountains high and the valleys dear. 
O, let my heart be as wild and free, 
As this water that sparkles under the trees.


- Elizabeth Barrett Browning
