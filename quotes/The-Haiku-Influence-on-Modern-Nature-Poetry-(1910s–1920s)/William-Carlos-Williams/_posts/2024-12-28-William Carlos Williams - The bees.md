The bees are
the only ones

that truly love

the lilies. They dance

around in a flurry,

settling down, spreading

the fragrance through the air,

taking care of nature’s

secrets, stitching

with each moment

a piece of life, born.

As necessary as

the afternoon sun.

- William Carlos Williams
