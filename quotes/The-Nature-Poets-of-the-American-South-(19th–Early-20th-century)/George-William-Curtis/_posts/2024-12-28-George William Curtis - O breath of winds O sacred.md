O breath of winds, O sacred skies,
O tender gaze of night's delight,
With every star my spirit flies,
And tastes the kiss of morning light.

For nature sings in rhythms fair,
In every moment, small or grand;
And in her arms, we shed our care,
Embracing the grace of her hand.

– George William Curtis
