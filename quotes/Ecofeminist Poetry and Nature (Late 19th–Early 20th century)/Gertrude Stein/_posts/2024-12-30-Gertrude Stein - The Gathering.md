What is the good of all the gathering
If the fruit has no tasted sin,
If the fruit has no singing bliss?

Take all your life as a gathering,
Take all your life as a sea,
And the simple things are all there waiting,
Waiting for the sound of summer glee.

- Gertrude Stein
