Spring, a time of revival,
a time for the birds to sing,
for the buds to bloom,
for new beginnings.

The sweet scent of flowers,
always reminds me
of the life we find in nature.

The bright colors,
the lively sounds,
they echo once again
the warmth of the sun.

- William Carlos Williams
