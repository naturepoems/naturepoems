The fields, a patchwork quilt,
Stitched by the sun and rain,
Golden grains swaying,
Like a sea under a gentle breeze,
Each stalk a dream,
Waiting to be harvested,
Yet in its place,
A moment, a breath, a hymn.
— Frederick Philip Grove

- Frederick Philip Grove
