And it's true that the sky had eyes,  
The trees with their green hair  
And the winds protect the singer!  

O Nature!  
Thou returnest, like the moon's image  
Across the murmuring water;  
Beauty is not yet dead,  
It has just gone to sleep  
Under the carmine leaves.

- Salvatore Quasimodo
