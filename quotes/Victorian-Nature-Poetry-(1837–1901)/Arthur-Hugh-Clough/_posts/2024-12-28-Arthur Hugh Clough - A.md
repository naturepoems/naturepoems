A River

It flows beneath the arching trees,
   The river, murmuring low;
With every sigh it seems to cease,
   Yet deeper currents flow.

The sunlight dapples on its breast,
   The shadows draw and part,
In every ripple’s silver crest,
   It bears a tranquil heart.


- Arthur Hugh Clough
