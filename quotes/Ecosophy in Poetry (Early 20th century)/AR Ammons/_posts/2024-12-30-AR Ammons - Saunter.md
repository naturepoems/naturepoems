Saunter

To saunter, to move
amongst creeks,
letting water slip
over stones,
unhurried, no destination.
Green mantles of grass,
pure air whispering,
each moment held
like a fragile bird,
easy to scare.
Time unfurls,
a leaf in the wind.

- AR Ammons
