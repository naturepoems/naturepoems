‘Tis in the leafy wood abode,
  Where spirits whisper low,
  That changeful visions sweetly rode,
  Those morns in twilight glow.

From tower to tower or stream so sweet,
  My heart to thee shall flow.
  O let me wander where hearts meet,
  In meadows soft as snow!

- William Hazlitt
