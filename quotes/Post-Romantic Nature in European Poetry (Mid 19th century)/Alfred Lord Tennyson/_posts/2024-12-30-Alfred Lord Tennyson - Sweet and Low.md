Sweet and low, sweet and low,
Wind of the Western Sea!
Low, low, breathe and blow,
Wind of the Western Sea!

Over the rolling sea,
And a lullaby is heard everywhere.
All from the golden sand to the silver waves,
Whispering of secrets, lost moments, regret.

- Alfred Lord Tennyson
