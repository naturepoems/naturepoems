In golden fields of amber,  
  Where gentle breezes sweep,  
  The sunflowers stand like sentinels,  
  Their promise strong and deep.

  They gaze upon the horizon,  
  With faces turned to light,  
  A reminder in their glory,  
  Of nature’s pure delight.

- Marian McCulloch
