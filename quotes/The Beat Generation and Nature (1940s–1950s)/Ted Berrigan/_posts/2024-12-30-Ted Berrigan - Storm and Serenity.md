Underneath the veil
of the shifting sky,
a storm brews,
a wild beauty on the edge.

Thunder rolls,
a symphony of chaos,
brought to life with
each clap,
a reminder that power
is often unseen.

And when the rain comes,
as it washes the earth,
i find myself in
each drop—alive.

- Ted Berrigan
