In the woods I find my peace, 
Where the sunbeams pierce the leaves,  
And the rustling leaves release,  
The thoughts that calm my heart.  
The babble of the brook a song,  
Takes my cares away;  
In nature’s choir, I belong,  
As I greet each new day.

- John Burroughs
