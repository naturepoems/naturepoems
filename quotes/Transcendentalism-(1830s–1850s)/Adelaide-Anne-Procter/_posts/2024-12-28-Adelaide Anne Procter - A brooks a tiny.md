A brook’s a tiny river,
   Which sings a song so sweet;
It laughs and whispers ever,
   It dances and it leaps.

It sparkles like a diamond,
   In sunlight's golden rays;
I love the brook's soft gaiety,
   Throughout the summer days.

- Adelaide Anne Procter
