An village of little houses,  
if I could give them voice,  
they’d speak a multitude of truths  
about their wistful beauty.  

Just beyond the silent hills,  
the rivers whisper softly,  
laced with meadows unfurling  
under the kiss of gentle sun.

- Rosalía de Castro
