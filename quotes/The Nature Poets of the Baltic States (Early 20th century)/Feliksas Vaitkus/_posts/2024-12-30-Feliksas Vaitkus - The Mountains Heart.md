Where mountains rise with stoic pride,
And valleys cradle secrets wide,
In every peak, a story flows,
Of time forgotten, where wildness grows.

The rocks are ancient, the paths are old,
The echoes of nature's heart unfold,
Each step I take on the rugged ground,
Is nature's wisdom all around.


- Feliksas Vaitkus
