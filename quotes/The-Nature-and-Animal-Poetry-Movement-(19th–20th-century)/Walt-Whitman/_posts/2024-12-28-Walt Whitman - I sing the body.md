I sing the body electric,
The pulse of life through every vein,
In the trees, in the rivers,
All witness to the joy and pain.

Let the stars be my audience,
The moon as my silver guide,
In the depth of night, I wander,
In nature's arms, I confide.

- Walt Whitman
