Amidst the fields of wildflowers,  
Where the sun-kissed daisies bloom,  
I wander in their beauty,  
And forget the world’s gloom.  

With colors bright and fragrant,  
They dance in playful glee,  
A canvas of pure laughter,  
In nature's jubilee.

- Selma Lagerlöf
