The brook is singing everywhere,
Its waters sparkling in the sun;
From banks adorned with wildflowers rare,
It dances forth, as if to run.
A lullaby of nature's heart,
In every ripple, soft and sweet,
The melodies know no depart,
For in its flow, pure joys repeat.

- Hamlin Garland
