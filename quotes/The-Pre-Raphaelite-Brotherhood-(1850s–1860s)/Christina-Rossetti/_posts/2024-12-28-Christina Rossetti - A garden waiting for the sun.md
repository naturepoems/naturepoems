A garden waiting for the sun  
To warm it from the cold,  
Shall soon be filled with fragrant bloom  
And tints of green and gold.  
With dewdrops gleaming on the leaves,  
As night gives place to day,  
The flowers unfold their petals wide  
To greet the dawn of May.

- Christina Rossetti
