Oh, the sky at dusk, so painted,  
With hues of gold and rose,  
As day’s soft breath is fainting,  
And evening's charm arose.  

The stars begin their dancing,  
In the deepening purple night,  
Nature’s canvas is enchanting,  
Filled with quiet delight.

- Selma Lagerlöf
