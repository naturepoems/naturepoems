Softly, softly, the night descends,
With silver stars so bright,
The whispering breeze, the twilight blends,
And bathes the world in light.

Nature sleeps, but dreams abide,
In every leaf and tree,
O tender night, with thee beside,
I find my solace free.


- Carl Michael Bellman
