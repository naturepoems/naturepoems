Above the rolling meadows,
Where gentle breezes play,
The wildflowers stretch outward,
In colors bright and gay.

They know no bounds or borders,
They bloom in joyful thrums,
A tapestry of beauty,
As summer’s warmth succumbs.

- Kārlis Skalbe
