GLIMPSE OF THE SEA  
Beyond the horizon,  
The sea shimmers,  
A mirror to the skies,  
As if inviting hearts  
To discover dreams anew.  

The waves speak in tongues,  
Of journeys taken,  
And those that are still waiting,  
For the courage to rise,  
And soar like the gulls.  


- Bessie Head
