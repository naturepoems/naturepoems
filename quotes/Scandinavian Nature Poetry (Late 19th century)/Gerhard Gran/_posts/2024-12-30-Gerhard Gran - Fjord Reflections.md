The fjord glimmers in twilight's glow,
As mountains loom with stoic pride,
Clouds drift like memories,
Along the water's side.

Nature's palette paints the sky,
With hues of gold and deep cerulean,
In this moment, I am alive,
My heart, attuned to that hymn.

- Gerhard Gran
