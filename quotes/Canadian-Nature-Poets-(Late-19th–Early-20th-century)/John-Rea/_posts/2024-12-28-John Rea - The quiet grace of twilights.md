The quiet grace of twilight’s touch,
As day retreats to night’s embrace,
In the dusk, we feel so much,
In nature's hold, we find our space.

Stars emerge with whispers soft,
As crickets serenade the night,
In nature’s arms, we drift aloft,
In dreams where all is pure delight.

- John Rea
