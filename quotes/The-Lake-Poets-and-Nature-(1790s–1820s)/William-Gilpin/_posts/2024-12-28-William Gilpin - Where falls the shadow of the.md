Where falls the shadow of the tree,
Upon the silver pool; 
The soft winds whisper, ‘Come to me,’ 
And enwrap the tranquil cool.
The shady mound with violets strewn,
A peaceful refuge calls; 
Here come the gradual sinks of soon,
Where Nature’s silence falls.

- William Gilpin
