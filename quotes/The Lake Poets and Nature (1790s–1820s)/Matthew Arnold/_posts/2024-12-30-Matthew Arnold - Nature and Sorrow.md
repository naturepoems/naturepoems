Nature and Sorrow

O Nature! Let thy kindly leaves  
    Invade the silence of my pain;  
For when thy beauty smiles on me,
I'll see my sorrow flicker like a flame
Before my eyes-  
That soon shall die away.


- Matthew Arnold
