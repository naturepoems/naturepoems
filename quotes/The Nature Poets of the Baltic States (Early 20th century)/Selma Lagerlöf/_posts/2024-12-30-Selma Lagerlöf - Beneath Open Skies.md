Beneath the wide blue heavens,  
Where the mountains kiss the sky,  
I feel a bond with nature,  
As time drifts slowly by.  

Each moment brings a treasure,  
In the colors rich and bright,  
A symphony of wonders,  
In the hush of fading light.
