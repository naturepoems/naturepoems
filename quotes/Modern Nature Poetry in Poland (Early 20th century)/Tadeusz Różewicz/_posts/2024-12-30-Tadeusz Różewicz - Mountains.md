The mountains rise
in regal silence,
their peaks brush the heavens,
clothed in snow and legend,
a timeless witness to
the stirring winds
and the echo of other worlds.

We are but whispers,
traced in the rock,
a fleeting thought
aided by the breath
of the great expanse.


- Tadeusz Różewicz
