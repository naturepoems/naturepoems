Beneath the willow,
In shadow's drape,
Lingers the soft sigh
Of the silent lake;
Dreams wind like tendrils,
With perfumed lights,
Carrying thoughts like
The swans’ soft paths.
To the heart that’s captive,
Nature sings,
Compellingly soft—
A lover's draw.

— **Virginia Woolf, Beneath the Willow**
