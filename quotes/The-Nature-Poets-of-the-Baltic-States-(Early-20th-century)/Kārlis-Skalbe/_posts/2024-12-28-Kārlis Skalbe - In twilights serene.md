In twilight's serene embrace,
The world begins to fade,
But in the hush of evening,
New wonders are displayed.

A chorus of the nightingales,
Weaves through the dusky air,
And in their sweet refrains,
Nature lays its cares bare.

- Kārlis Skalbe
