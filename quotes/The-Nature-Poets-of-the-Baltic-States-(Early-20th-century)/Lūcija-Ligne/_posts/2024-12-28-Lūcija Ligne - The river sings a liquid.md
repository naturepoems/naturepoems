The river sings, a liquid song,
Through the valleys, wild and free,
Each ripple dances, swift and strong,
A testament to life's decree.

Her banks are lined with willows weeping,
As shadows stretch across the stream,
In its depths, the world is sleeping,
Cradled in a liquid dream.


- Lūcija Ligne
