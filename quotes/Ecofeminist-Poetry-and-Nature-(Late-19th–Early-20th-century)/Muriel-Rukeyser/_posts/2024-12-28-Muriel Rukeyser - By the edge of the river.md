By the edge of the river,   
a handful of stones,   
each a prayer,   
I toss them lightly,   
my thoughts sliding   
like the current.  

The water carries them away   
into an ocean of echoes,   
a song of ancestors saying  
what the earth always knew —  
in the rhythms of our own names.

- Muriel Rukeyser
