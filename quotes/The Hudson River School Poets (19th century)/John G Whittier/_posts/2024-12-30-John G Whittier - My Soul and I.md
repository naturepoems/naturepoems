My Soul and I

I walk alone a rugged road,
To find my way of life,
To trace the passage of the soul,
Amid the din of strife.

The leaves are trembling on the trees,
And yellow is the air;
But peace has come unto my heart,
And joy is everywhere.

- John G Whittier
