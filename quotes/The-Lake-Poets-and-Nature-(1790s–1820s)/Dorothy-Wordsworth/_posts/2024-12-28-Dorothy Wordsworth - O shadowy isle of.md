O shadowy isle of beauty,
In thee is no despair;
The light unlocks the feeling,
And love's enchanted air.
Here nature's dreams unveil,
In sapphire, green, and gold,
Where peace will softly sail,
And serene sweetness unfold.

- Dorothy Wordsworth
