Beneath the vast New Zealand sky,  
Where clouds drift soft and free,  
The land awakens in the dawn,  
A symphony to see.  

With every hue of nature’s brush,  
Life flourishes and thrives,  
In every blade of grass, a tale,  
Of time that gently dives.

- L W Lydia H W Milligan
