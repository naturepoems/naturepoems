I have seen the moon rise  
Awash with deep colors,  
Waves of purple and blue,  
Filling the night with dreams  
That echo and call  
In the silence of stars,  
A soft, sweet melody  
Singing of life  
And joy and love;  
O moon, my heart’s desire,  
Can I reach you?

- HD
