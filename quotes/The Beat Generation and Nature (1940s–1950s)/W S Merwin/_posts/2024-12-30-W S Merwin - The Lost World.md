The Lost World

These trees like shapes
   contoured against the light,
every form pressed
   into silence,
become the ancients
   of the dark.

Each shadow reaching,
yeach color flying
   becomes the dream of us
lost in our many hours
   of the unfathomable.


- W S Merwin
