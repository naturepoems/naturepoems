The rugged coast, where land meets sea,
In crashing wave's duet,
The call of nature, wild and free,
A song we won't forget.

The salty air, it stirs the soul,
As gulls take flight and cry,
In every wave, we find the whole,
The earth and we, entwined.


- Mary S M McCulloch
