Amidst the wildflowers strewn,
Where the meadow meets the glade,
I find peace in nature's tune,
In the cool of the evening shade.

As the sun dips low and shy,
Painting shadows long and deep,
Sighing winds softly sigh,
And the world awakens from sleep.

- Jean Blewett
