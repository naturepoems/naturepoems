Through wildflowers bright and fair,
Where butterflies in silence play,
The essence of the land laid bare,
Invites each heart to stop and stay.

With every step, a path unfolds,
In meadows soft, where nature dreams;
Each blossom whispers tales untold,
In fragrant air, the spirit gleans.

- Marjorie Barnard
