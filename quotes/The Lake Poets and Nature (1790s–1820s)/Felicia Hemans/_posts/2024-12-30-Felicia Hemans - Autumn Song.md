Autumn Song
O world of all delights!  
When the fruit is ripe and gay,  
And the faithful heart must sing,  
In radiant autumn's play.

The leaves upon the trees  
Are a crimson, gold parade,  
While the sunbeams sprinkle soft,  
Like treasures that never fade.

- Felicia Hemans
