Amidst the fields of golden grain,
Where sunflowers stand, a proud refrain,
The rustling whispers in the breeze,
Call forth the heart, set it at ease.

In every grain, a promise sown,
The earth below, a living throne,
In nature's bounty, understand,
The touch of life, a gentle hand.


- Tora L Ruge
