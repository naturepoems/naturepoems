The Moon was but a Chin of Gold  
A Discinct prop – to Slim  
But, at such moments tho when  
The sun had not beckoned in  

The Little Muscles of the feebles –  
And relationships pray for it –  
Can they be Crying?  
Or ever Scoff –  


- Emily Dickinson
