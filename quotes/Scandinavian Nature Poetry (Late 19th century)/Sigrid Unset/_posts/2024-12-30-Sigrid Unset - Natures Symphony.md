The Earth is ours, the sky, the sea,
The smell of pine and wood so free.
Mountains rise in majesty,
Their snowy peaks, a sight to see.

Beneath the trees, where shadows play,
The whispers of the breeze convey
A symphony of life at bay,
In stillness found both night and day.

- Sigrid Unset
