The mountain stands with crowning snow,
Amidst the wild, and grand, and free;
Where rivers form and gales do blow,
And echo to the sea.
Here Nature’s strength in silence speaks,
In forms of rock and glade,
Among the weeds and modest peaks,
Where beauty’s never frayed.

- William Gilpin
