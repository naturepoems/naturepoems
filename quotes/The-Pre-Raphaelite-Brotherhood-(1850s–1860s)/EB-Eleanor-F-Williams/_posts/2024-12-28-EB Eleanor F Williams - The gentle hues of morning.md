The gentle hues of morning light,
Across the dew-kissed glades,
A canvas painted soft and bright,
Where nature's beauty wades.

In every whisper of the breeze,
The secrets of the day,
The rustling leaves, the bending trees,
In harmonious ballet.

- EB Eleanor F Williams
