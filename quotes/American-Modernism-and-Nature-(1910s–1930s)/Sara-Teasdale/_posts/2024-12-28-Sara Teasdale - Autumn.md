Autumn Song

Lady of wholly glow, you hover,
Gently over the dying leaves,
Here, where the season wanes,
And nature lends her mournful weaves.

In the chill of morning’s breath,
I watch the colors fade to gray;
The beauty sings of life and death,
In an autumnal soft decay.

- Sara Teasdale
