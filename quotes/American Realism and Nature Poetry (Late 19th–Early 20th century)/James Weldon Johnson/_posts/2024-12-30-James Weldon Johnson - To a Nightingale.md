To a Nightingale

O nightingale, that through the darkness sings,  
   Your melody brings light on cobalt wings;  
   A hymn of joy so sweet and pure,  
   Refreshes the mind and makes the spirit sure.

As echoes dance on moonlit streams,  
   Nature awakens all our dreams.

- James Weldon Johnson
