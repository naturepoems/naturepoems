Mountains stand tall  
With majesty profound,  
Their peaks kissed by sunlight,  
Their roots in the ground.  

They guard ancient secrets,  
Their stories untold,  
In the heart of the earth,  
Where the wild thrives bold.

- Hjalmar Gullberg
