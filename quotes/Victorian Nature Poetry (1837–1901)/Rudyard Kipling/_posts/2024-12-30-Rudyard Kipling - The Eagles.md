When the stars are waking, and the moon is bright;  
When the night is a whisper, and the world is slight,  
Then the eagles chase the shadows,  
Through the forest and the skies,  
While the wild things watch them soaring,  
Soaring high, serene and wise.

- Rudyard Kipling
