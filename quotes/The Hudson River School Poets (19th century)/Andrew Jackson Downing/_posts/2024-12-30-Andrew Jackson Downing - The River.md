The River
The river winds in a tranquil course,
Beneath the morning's dew,
Whispering secrets of the earth
To the skies that blush in blue.
It mirrors all the beauty,
The verdant hills and glades,
And sings a song eternal
Through the quiet forest shades.

- Andrew Jackson Downing
