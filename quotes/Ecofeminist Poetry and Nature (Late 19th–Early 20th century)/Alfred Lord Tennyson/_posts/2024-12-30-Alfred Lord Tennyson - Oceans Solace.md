Starry night upon the sea,
Where wonders live, where spirits play,
The tides enchant in silent glee,
And dusk enfolds the end of day.
Nature's whispers hush the roar,
As waves caress the ancient shore,
And in the depths, the heart of time,
Finds solace in the ocean's rhyme.

- Alfred Lord Tennyson
