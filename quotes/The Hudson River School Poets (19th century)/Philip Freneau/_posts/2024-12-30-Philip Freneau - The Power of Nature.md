The Power of Nature

I wander through the forest wide,
Beneath the whispering shade,

Where every leaf and flower abide
In harmony displayed.

The murmuring brook, it sings a tune,
The gentle winds caress;

By night, beneath the silver moon,
I feel a sweet distress.


- Philip Freneau
