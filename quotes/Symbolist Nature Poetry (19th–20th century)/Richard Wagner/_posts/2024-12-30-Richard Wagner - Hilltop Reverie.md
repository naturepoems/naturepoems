On the crest of the green hill,
Where wildflowers bloom and sway,
The murmuring brook sings still,
In the warmth of sunlight's play.

Birds weave melodies through trees,
As the wind carries tales untold,
The heart finds its gentle ease,
In the beauty of nature, bold and gold.

- Richard Wagner
