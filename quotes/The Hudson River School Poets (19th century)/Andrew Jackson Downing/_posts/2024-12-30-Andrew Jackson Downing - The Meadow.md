The Meadow
Beneath the wide expanse of blue,
A meadow spreads its emerald cloak,
Each flower a brushstroke, each shade a hue,
A canvas where nature's dreams provoke.
The bees hum a song through the radiant air,
And the whispering grass speaks of peace, everywhere.

- Andrew Jackson Downing
