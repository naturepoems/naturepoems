The golden fields of summer's grace,
Where grain and wildflowers play,
Each swaying stalk, a tender trace,
Of memories, come what may.

Butterflies flit in joyous flight,
Over blooms of vibrant hue,
In the heart of the sunlight,
I find my spirit renew.

- Elīna Zālīte Zalīte
