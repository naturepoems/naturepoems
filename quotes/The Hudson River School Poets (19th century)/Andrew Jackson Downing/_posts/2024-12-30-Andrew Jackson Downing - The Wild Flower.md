The Wild Flower
In the heart of the woodland, unbidden, it grows,
A wild flower, so humble, yet bright,
With petals that dance in the soft summer's breeze,
And colors that gladden the sight.
No gardener prunes it, no hand would adorn,
Yet still, it seeks light and blooms.

- Andrew Jackson Downing
