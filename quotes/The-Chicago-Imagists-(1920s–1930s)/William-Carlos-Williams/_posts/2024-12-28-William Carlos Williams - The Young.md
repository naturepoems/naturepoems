The Young Housewife
She stands
at the corner

with a yellow apron
beside the

window. The brook
is murky and

somewhere a child
is crying –

she turns
her back and

sings softly
to herself –

each note falls
gently – they are
whispers

from the radiant world.


- William Carlos Williams
