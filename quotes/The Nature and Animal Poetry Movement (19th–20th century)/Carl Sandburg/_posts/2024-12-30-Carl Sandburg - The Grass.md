The grass
is a great green sea
lying still,
always still, and knowing.  

The stars, the moon  
like a great gray hand  
spread over the earth,
care loving,  
craftsman old, who knows  
where the bones are buried.  
Her hair grows out in rings,  
a green wave in silence.

- Carl Sandburg
