The hills unfold their verdant folds,  
Under skies of aqua blue,  
Each rustling leaf, a tale retold,  
Of the beauty nestled in the dew.

A tapestry of golden sun,  
Rises gently o’er the stream,  
Where shadows dance and rivers run,  
In the heart of nature’s dream.


- Vivienne Scott
