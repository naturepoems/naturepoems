Through autumn woods, the path leads on,
Where echoes of the past are drawn.
The leaves, they crackle 'neath my tread,
In every step, the seasons bled.

And as the chill of evening falls,
A tapestry of nature calls;
To walk the lines of history,
In harmony with earth and sea.

- Elin Wägner
