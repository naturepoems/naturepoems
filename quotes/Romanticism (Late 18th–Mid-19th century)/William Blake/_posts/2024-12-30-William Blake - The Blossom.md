The Blossom

Merry, merry sparrow!  
Art thou a shilly-shally?  
Lambs are skipping in the meadow,  
Flow’rs unfolding, beauty’s shadow.

In the bower of breezes light,  
Resting through the day’s delight  
Underneath the violet bloom,  
Dearest Lover, sweet perfume.


- William Blake
