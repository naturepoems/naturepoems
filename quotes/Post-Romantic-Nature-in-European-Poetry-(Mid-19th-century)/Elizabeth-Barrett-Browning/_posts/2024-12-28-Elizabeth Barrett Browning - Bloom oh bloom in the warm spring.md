Bloom, oh, bloom in the warm spring sun, 
Let your colors burst forth in joyful delight; 
With every petal that opens, a new story begun, 
In the garden where spirits take flight.
Nature's embrace is a sweet reprieve, 
In the heart of creation, truly believe.


- Elizabeth Barrett Browning
