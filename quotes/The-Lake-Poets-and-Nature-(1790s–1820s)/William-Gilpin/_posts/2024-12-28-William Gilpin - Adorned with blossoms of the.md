Adorned with blossoms of the wood,
The meadow stretches wide and far;
Each blade of grass in gentle mood,
Reflects the silver star.
And all the world beneath us lies,
In hues of azure dreams,
As softly hints the time's reprise,
And if with Nature beams.

- William Gilpin
