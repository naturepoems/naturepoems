When I was small, a little Quinn —  
In a white dress — wound tight —  
I walked the paths of tulips —  
And that height of Summer light!  

I waited for a moment sweet —  
To flutter, dip and spin —  
And he who walks beside the rose —  
Is he, who holds me in!  


- Emily Dickinson
