As the dawn breaks over yonder,
   And the dew covers the grass,
There are thoughts of love and ponder,
   And the moments sweetly pass.

The heart, alive with wonder,
   Finds in nature's endless roam,
Whispers soft, that plunder
   The soul and call it home.

- Matthew Arnold
