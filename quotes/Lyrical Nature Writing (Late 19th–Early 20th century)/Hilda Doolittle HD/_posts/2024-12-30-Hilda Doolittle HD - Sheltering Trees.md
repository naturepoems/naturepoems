Sheltering trees of the sun,  
Your patterned leaves  
Prevent the sun from invading  
The shadows upon shadows  
Dreaming of green roots.  
The flocking birds,  
Flying high above, beneath.  
How far the wind pushes  
The clouds over the trees.

- Hilda Doolittle HD
