Within the mountain’s rocky heart,
Amidst its silent stone,
The echoes of the ages start,
In crevices alone.

Each boulder tells a tale of old,
Of glaciers, winds, and rain,
In every ridge, a story bold,
In nature's vast domain.


- Ragnar Knutson
