A river flows through valleys wide,
Its crystal waters, a gentle guide,
Reflections of the world above,
In its embrace, I find my love.

The mountains rise, a grand parade,
With peaks that sparkle, never fade,
Each stone a story, each path a song,
In nature's symphony, I belong.


- Tora L Ruge
