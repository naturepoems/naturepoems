The Glass Bowl

In the glass

bowl we gather

roots, fragments,

for the wild

and tender grasses.

Let them live here

in the hush

of the space,

the reflecting grain,

the stillness,

while outside,

a message sings

through the branches

that sway,

in life eternal.


- William Carlos Williams
