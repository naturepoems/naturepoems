The Forest
In the forest deep and wide,  
The mysteries abound,  
Each rustling leaf, a secret told  
Within the sacred ground.

Let us wander where the shadows play,  
And dance in patches of light,  
For nature holds a magic sweet,  
In the heart of the forest’s night.
