Sky Larks  
The larks, swinging high in the blue,  
   herald the day with their cries,  
as if the skies themselves bent down  
   to cradle their song,  
so sweet and carefree,  
   stitching clouds to golden suns  
while the earth listens on,  
   heart thrumming along.

- Louise Bogan
