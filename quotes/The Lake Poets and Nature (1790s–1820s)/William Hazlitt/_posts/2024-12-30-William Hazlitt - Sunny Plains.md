I dwell among the sunny plains,
  Where rippling waters glide;
  Beneath the boughs of gilded chains,
  With blossoms I abide.

O whisper'd light that brightens here,
  The vale where shadows weave—
  I gather sweet from every sphere,
  For every heart to cleave.

- William Hazlitt
