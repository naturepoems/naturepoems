Beneath a sky of cloudless blue,
The earth awakens from its dream,
In every blade of grass, a view,
Of life that stirs, a flowing stream.

Birds flit joyfully through the air,
Chasing shadows, flickering light,
In the embrace of springtime's care,
Nature unfolds in pure delight.

- Richard Wagner
