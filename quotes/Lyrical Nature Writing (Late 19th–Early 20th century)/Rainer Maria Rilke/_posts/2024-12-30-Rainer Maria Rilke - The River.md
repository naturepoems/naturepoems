The River

Oh infinite river,
    I become your quickened heart,
    flowing gently toward the embrace
    of the deep, restless sea.

    It is only by the grace
    of your persistent ways
    that I learn to breathe,
    to speak with the silent shores.

—Rainer Maria Rilke
