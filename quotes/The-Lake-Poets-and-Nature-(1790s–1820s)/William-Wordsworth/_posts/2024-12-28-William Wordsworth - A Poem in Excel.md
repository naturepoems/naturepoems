A Poem in Excel  
What’s in a flower,  
but a truth  
I find on the green hills.  
As a wind, its blooming—you see  
A song sent from nature,  
Which soothes my very soul,  
And shaper out of the earth,  
Life sings in me.

- William Wordsworth
