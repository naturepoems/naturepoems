The golden sunset spills its hue
Over mountains, rolling green,
Here the earth unfolds anew,
Where the wild and sweet have been.

Birds are calling, echoing far,
While the night wraps in velvet cloak,
Every twinkle, every star,
Bears a promise, softly spoke.

- Jean Blewett
