Into the Woods
Beneath the canopy,
Where silence reigns,
The woodlands breathe,
In fragrant green,
Sunlight dapples the ground,
With patches of gold,
Inviting hearts to wander,
In the stories untold,
Of roots and dreams.

- R E D Evans
