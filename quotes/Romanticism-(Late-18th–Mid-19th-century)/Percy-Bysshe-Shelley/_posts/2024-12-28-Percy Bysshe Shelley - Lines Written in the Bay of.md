Lines Written in the Bay of Lerici
The sun is warm, the sky is clear,
The waves are dancing fast and bright,
The Journeying Sun sheds light
On every glowing sphere that flies.

O Nature! full of life and light,
Assure to me the beauty bright!






- Percy Bysshe Shelley
