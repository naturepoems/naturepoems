The Call of the Wild
Listen to the wild,
The roar of life untamed,
From the depths of the jungle,
To the heights, unclaimed.

Each rustle ignites,
The spirit of the ancient call,
In the heart of the wild,
We find freedom, after all.


- Léopold Sédar Senghor
