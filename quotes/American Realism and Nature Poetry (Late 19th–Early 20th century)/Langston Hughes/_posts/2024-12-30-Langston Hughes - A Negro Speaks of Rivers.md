A Negro Speaks of Rivers
I’ve known rivers
Ancient dusky rivers
My soul has grown deep like the rivers.

I look back to the Nile
I remember the Mississippi
I can see the Congo

What the rivers have seen
My soul has grown deep like the rivers.

- Langston Hughes
