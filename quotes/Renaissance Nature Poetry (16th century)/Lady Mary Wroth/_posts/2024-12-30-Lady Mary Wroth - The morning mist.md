The morning mist that wraps the ground,
Holds secrets sweet and rare,
Each droplet kissed by light around,
A treasure resting there.

With golden rays, the sun will break,
And lift the veil anew,
While every petal, flower, and flake,
Will shine beneath the blue.

- Lady Mary Wroth
