Oh, the wild magnolia blooms,
In fragrant white embrace,
They cast away the shadowed glooms,
And fill the night with grace.

Beneath the stars, they sway so free,
A beauty hard to find,
In nature's heart, they sing to me,
Of joys that softly bind.


- Mary S M McCulloch
