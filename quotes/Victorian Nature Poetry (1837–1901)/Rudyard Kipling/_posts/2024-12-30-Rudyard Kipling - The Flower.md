In the heart of a flower,  
Where the dew-drops lie,  
There are secrets and stories,  
That the blooms will never die.  
 
With colors that spill from gentle hands,  
While the winds caress the air;  
The blooms are a canvas, the earth turns round,  
And beauty is everywhere.

- Rudyard Kipling
