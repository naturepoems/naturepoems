Evening Light

As day slips away,
wraps itself in shadows,
the sky ignites first lines
of a painter’s touch.

Fingers stretch in violet,
a hush invites the stars,
a soft reminder—
the world can pause,
just breathe in between.

Philip Whalen

- Philip Whalen
