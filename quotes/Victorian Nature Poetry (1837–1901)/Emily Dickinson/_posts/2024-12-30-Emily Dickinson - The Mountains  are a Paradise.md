The Mountains — are a Paradise —
But have a Guess of Hell —
As if, from this The Universe —
They need my sad farewell —

They wear the gray of quiet —
And the deep of Ice and Snow —
Yet when the sun is weary —
They break the Night, with Glow —

- Emily Dickinson
