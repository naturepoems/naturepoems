The mountains rise, with majesty,
Their peaks like dreams, eternally,
A canvas broad of azure sky,
Where eagles soar, where spirits fly.

In solitude, the soul can stir,
Amongst the pines, the winds that purr,
This elevated view I seek,
In nature’s arms, I find my peak.

- Charles Lane
