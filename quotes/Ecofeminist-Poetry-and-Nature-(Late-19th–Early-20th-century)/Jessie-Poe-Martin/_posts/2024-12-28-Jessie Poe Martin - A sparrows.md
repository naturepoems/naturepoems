A sparrow's song,
Turns my window's frame,
To a world no iron,
Nor stone can tame.

Those feathered notes,
A gentle plea,
Whispered softly,
To you and me.

In the dappled light,
Where shadows play,
Nature speaks,
In her own way.


- Jessie Poe Martin
