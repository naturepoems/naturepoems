I saw a man upon a hill,
His face was pale and drawn,
He leaned upon his hand and said,
'Behold the coming dawn!'

With fields of yellow daffodils,
And bluebells stacked in rows,
The reason for his wistful gaze,
Is the beauty nature shows.

- Thomas Hardy
