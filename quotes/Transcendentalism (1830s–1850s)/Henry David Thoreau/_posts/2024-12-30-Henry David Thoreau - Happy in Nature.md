Men are born to be happy,
In fields of clover and light,
With dreams of songs and freedom,
Bathed in the quiet of night.

Each star a whispering promise,
Each brook a cheerful friend,
In nature’s splendid vastness,
We find our hearts can mend.

- Henry David Thoreau
