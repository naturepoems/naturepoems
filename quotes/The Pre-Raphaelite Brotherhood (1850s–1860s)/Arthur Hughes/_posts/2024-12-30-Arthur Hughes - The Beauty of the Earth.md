Whence comes the beauty of the earth,
   The riot of the flowers?
It claims a joy, as life rebirth,
   With promises and showers.
  
Or does the daylight won the night,
   To wear the morning dew,
From dusk till dawn, from dark to light,
   There blooms a world anew.
