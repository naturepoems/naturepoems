Under the stars’ eternal watch,
where night unfurls its velvet shawl,
the whispers of the cosmos beckon,
a quietness that encompasses all.
Each flicker of light, a distant tale,
in the expanse of the boundless night,
a connection that weaves the veil
between the infinite and our sight.

- Alfredo Panzini
