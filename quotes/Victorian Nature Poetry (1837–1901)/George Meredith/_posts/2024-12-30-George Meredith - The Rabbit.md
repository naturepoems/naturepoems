The Rabbit

As I walked the fields one day,  
  By the meadows in spring’s might,  
On human forms and twig I stay  
  And glimpse thee shyer in twilight.  
Thou hop and scurry, turn, and flee,  
  While nature watches, wild and free.

- George Meredith
