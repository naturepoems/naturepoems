The sun is a shining flower
That blooms in the morning sky
Its petals stretch out wide and bright
To catch the dew, to drink the light.

And all around its glowing bloom,
The earth awakens from its gloom,
Each blade of grass, each leaf, each tree,
Rejoices in its symmetry.
