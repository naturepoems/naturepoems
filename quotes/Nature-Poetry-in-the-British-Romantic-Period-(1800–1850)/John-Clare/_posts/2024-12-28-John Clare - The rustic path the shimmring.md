The rustic path, the shimm'ring dew,
A peaceful sight to view,
Where every step, the spirit lifts,
With nature’s endless gifts.

A realm where beauty finds its place,
In every twist and turn,
Each moment here, a soft embrace,
For which my soul does yearn.


- John Clare
