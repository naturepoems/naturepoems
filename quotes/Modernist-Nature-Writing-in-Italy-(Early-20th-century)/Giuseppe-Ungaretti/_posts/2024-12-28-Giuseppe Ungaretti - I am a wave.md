I am a wave 
that breaks
on the shore
of your bosom,
a drop of water
in the vastness
of your heart.
The silence of the sea
in the scattered grains of sand,
dissolves in me.

I feel the sky,
in every tear of rain,
a jewel of the earth,
in the embrace of the wind.

- Giuseppe Ungaretti
