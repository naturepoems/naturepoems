Beneath the canopy so wide,
Where shadows blend with dancing light,
The ancient trees in silence bide,
A witness to the day and night.

A songbird flutters from the branch,
Its melody, a sweet refrain,
The forest joins in nature’s dance,
To celebrate both joy and pain.


- Lūcija Ligne
