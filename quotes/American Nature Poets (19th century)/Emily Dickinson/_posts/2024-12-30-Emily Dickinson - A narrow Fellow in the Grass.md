A narrow Fellow in the Grass
Occasionally rides—
You may have met him,—did you not
His notice sudden is—

The Grass divides as with a Comb—
A spotted Boy, not far—
He likes his Hands full of the soft
And marking passed—Has a scar—

- Emily Dickinson
