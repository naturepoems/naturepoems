The Sorrow of Loneliness

In the continuous breath
    of winds blowing unseen,
    the trees whisper stories
    of lonely, flowering cries.

    Yet each leaf learns to dance,
    basking even in silence,
    while even the rough edges
    find a way to offer peace.

—Rainer Maria Rilke
