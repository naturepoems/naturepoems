The Caged Skylark
As a dare, leave off green grass; aside, just set rocks out,
I call down the one pace luminous of life – the bright sun,
Smooth-topped and saturnine tears show. Make voices echo,
Give joy wholefilled, lest spill and reflect the brightest light.

--  Gerard Manley Hopkins
