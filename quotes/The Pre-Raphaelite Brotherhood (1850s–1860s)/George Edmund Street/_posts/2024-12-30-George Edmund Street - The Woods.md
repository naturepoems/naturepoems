The trees are bending in the wind,
The woods are full of sighs;
The may-flower's scent is like a sigh,
As fragrant as the skies.

O love, where art thou?—Still I feel
Thy power on my heart today—
Though the sunshine be dim and pale,
And the woods are far away.

- George Edmund Street
