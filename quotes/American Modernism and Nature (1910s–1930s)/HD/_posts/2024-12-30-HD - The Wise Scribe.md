The Wise Scribe

   The dry sage’s charm
     weaves through silent oaks,
   their golden pines,
  here, beloved’s wing,
    tucked in the soft earth;
   I falter beneath the moon’s watch
and feel their strength,
     their sharpened leaves,
given to the sky
and to me, folded.
       —H.D.
