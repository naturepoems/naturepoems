The Forest  
Ye sing unto the faery woods,  
Bright beings of the air!  
Ye live in joy, where beauty broods,  
And dreams are everywhere.  
Around you wash the verdant leaves,  
The murmurs of the breeze:  
In heart and eye, this life receives  
The secret joys of trees.

- Leigh Hunt
