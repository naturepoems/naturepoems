In the morning light,  
a butterfly flutters  
like a whisper,  
carried by the breeze,  
imprinted on my soul,  
with delicate wings  
that span the beauty of the day,  
as if to say,  
"Be light, be free,  
this moment is yours."

- Adélia Prado
