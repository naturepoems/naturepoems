Upon the hills where shadows fall,
The sunrise burns the feast;
The autumn yields her fruit to all,
And welcomes her wild beast.

All day the rivers sing the tune,
In mist their laughter lies;
While far away the lofty moon
Still blooms beyond the skies.

- William Morris
