From rivers hand in hand, we walk—
To seek a sweetened dream.
The winding woodlands, love would talk,
On where the stars will gleam.

With every foot amid the birch,
I place each spring dawn fair,
And wrap my heart in the tender search
Of whispers from the air.

- Coventry Patmore
