Within the realm of flowers bright,
   My heart finds joy anew,
In every petal bathed in light,
   In every drop of dew.

For the earth is rich in stories,
   That the blooms so sweetly tell,
And within these fleeting glories,
   I sense a magic spell.

- Matthew Arnold
