Among the pines, upon a hill,
I wandered in a mellow light,
The breeze danced and swayed at will,
Nature's grace, pure and bright.

A hush that fell on every face, 
As silence broke the evening calm,
In the stillness of this grace,
The evening sang a soothing psalm.

- Charles Baudelaire
