The Song of Nature

O Nature! thou art beautiful,
Pure in thy very core;
This mind of men doth pass away,
But thou art evermore.

Thou art a silent witness
Of all the changing years;
Thou art the bard of peace and joy,
And of the world's own tears.

- John G Whittier
