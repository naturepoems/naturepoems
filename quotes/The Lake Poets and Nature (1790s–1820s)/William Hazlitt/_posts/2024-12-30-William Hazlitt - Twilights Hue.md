O visions seen in twilight's hue,
  And fading daylight's die,
  How do the aged hearts renew,
  As evening stars float by!

Yet from the chasm of the past,
  The base-born tumults rise,
  To listen to thy music cast
  From nature's heart to skies.

- William Hazlitt
