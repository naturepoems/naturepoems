In the quietude of the night,
Underneath a velvet sky,
Where silver stars dance in delight,
And shadows whisper a soft sigh.

The moon casts whispers on the lake,
A gentle ripple, a soft embrace,
Nature's cradle, the night's sweet ache,
In the symphony of serene space.


- Richard Wagner
