To the Moon
Thou silver Moon, dost rise so fair,
Among the stars so high,
Thy gentle light doth softly dare,
To twinkle in the sky.
O silent orb, my friend so true,
In night's embrace you glide;
Thy beams like whispers fall on dew,
As the world sleeps side by side.

- Hartley Coleridge
