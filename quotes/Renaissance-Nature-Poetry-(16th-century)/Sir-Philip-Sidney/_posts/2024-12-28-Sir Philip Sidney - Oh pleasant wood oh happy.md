Oh pleasant wood, oh happy shade,
In whose coolness I am laid;
Where birds do sing, and breezes blow,
I find my peace ‘neath the oak boughs low—
Here life’s dark shadows, worries cease,
And by this bounty I find peace.

- Sir Philip Sidney
