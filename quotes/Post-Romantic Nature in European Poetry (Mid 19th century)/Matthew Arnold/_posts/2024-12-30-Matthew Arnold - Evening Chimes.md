When the bells of evening chime,
   By the river’s gentle course,
All life within me feels sublime,
   And I see without remorse.

For the beauty that surrounds me,
   In the nature’s grand display,
Brings forth all that astounds me,
   In the twilight of the day.

- Matthew Arnold
