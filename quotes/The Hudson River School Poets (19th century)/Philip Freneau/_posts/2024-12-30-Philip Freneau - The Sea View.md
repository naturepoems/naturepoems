The Sea View

O ocean vast, thy waves that roll,
So free upon the shore;

Shall quench my thirst, shall fill my soul,
With beauty evermore.

In every crest, in every tide,
Thy brush of power speaks to me;

As moments pass, thy hands abide,
A kindred spirit, wild and free.


- Philip Freneau
