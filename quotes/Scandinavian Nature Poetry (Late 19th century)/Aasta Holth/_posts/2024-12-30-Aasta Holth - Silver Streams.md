Silver Streams
Silver streams weave through the glade,
Shimmer like stars in the night,
Dancing over rocks, unafraid,
Calling the moon with their light.

Life pulses in currents swift,
Whispers of water, wind, and earth,
In their course, the ancients sift,
Reminding us of our worth.

- Aasta Holth
