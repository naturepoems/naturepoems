The dawn breaks cold and clear,
A hush before the day,
The world awakens, drawing near,
To chase the night away.

In fields of frost, a silence reigns,
As light seeps through the trees,
In moments frozen, life remains,
In nature's gentle breeze.


- Ragnar Knutson
