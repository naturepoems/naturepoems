Underneath the blue sky,
A vast sea of wheat undulates,
Golden waves greeting,
A horizon kissed by the sun.

Each blade a whisper,
Of ancient tales,
Stirred by the gentle zephyrs,
Speaking of life and time.


- Giorgio de Chirico
