In Nature's heart there lies a grace,
   A silent wisdom, soft and true;
A quiet love, a warm embrace,
   In every hue, in every dew.

Let me but tread the path she shows,
   And find my peace in every place;
For every petal, every rose,
   Is part of Life's divinest face.

- Adelaide Anne Procter
