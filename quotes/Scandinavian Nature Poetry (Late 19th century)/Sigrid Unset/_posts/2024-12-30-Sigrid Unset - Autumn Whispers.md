The whisper of the autumn breeze,
Carries tales among the trees.
Leaves of gold, like memories,
Drift soft upon the rustling seas.

Each season's turn, a story told,
In nature's grasp, a hand to hold.
The beauty found in shades of gold,
In every breath, a heart consoled.

- Sigrid Unset
