The ocean's breath, a rhythmic sigh,
Caresses shores in a soft caress,
With every wave that rises high,
It sings a tale of wildness, blessed.

Seagulls cry in the salty air,
Salt and foam, the essence of bliss,
In the dance of breezes fair,
Nature’s heart finds peace in this.

- Richard Wagner
