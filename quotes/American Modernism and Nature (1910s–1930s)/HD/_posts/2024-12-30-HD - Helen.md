Helen

   All Greece
      *knows the story*
  Of my life,
  Dreaming in shadows
  And stony face,
  A nomad in the hawthorn—
     I am still! Let me behold,
  Cross o’er the wild,
         The dawn sing
  And time whimper.
         And I am earth.
       —H.D.
