The stream slips by the mossy stones,
Where daisies dance and play,
Each ripple sings a tune that’s known,
In nature’s grand ballet.

The sunbeams flicker, shadows merge,
And all is calm and bright,
In every shade, a lovely urge,
To wander through the light.


- John Clare
