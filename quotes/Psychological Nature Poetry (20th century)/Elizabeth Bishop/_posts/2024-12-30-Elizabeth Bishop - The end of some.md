The end of some

The country is wild
and the sun at last finds you
within each accidental curve,
as it holds you in place.
And you wait in the shelter
from storms,
small black cupboard,
a precious alliance of nature
against the walls of time,
as all the walls of ages threaten
and talk.

- Elizabeth Bishop
