For a Coming Extinction
by W. S. Merwin

We are no longer
an abrupt consequence
but a snowflake
falling forever.
We must go now, as
the raven cloud.
Leaves close,
in shades unseen,
disappearing.
The silence,
at the edge of a lake,
where the beautiful bodies
sink into the past.

- W S Merwin
