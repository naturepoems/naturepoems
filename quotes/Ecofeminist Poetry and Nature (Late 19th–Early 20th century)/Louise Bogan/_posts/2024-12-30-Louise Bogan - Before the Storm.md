Before the Storm  
A time so still without a sound  
   the trees, leaves waiting for  
the wind to strike them,  
   to shiver softly before the rain  
speaks gently to ground  
   where secrets lift like clouds  
and the sky bends down  
   with a sigh like an embrace.

- Louise Bogan
