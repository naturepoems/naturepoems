This Lime-Tree Bower My Prison

Ney, I’ll receive thee back to me:
And henceforth I’ll never wander more,
Mindful of the joy
God has in all the living forms
To fruit and flower.  

I shall come back,
And in this bower—
Once more, a prisoner——to thy lip,
And in the quietude of light,
Like a lost child in forest.
