The Blossoming Tree
Witness the tree,  
In democratic bloom,  
The petals’ laughter scattered,  
As each bud finds its room.

Under the canopy of blue,  
A dance of colors ignites,  
Each blossom sharing dreams of spring,  
In sunlight and softened lights.

- Émile Verhaeren
