Nature, the Gentlest Mother is,
Impatient of no Child,
The feeblest or the waywardest—
Her admonition mild

In the least leaf of Spring.
She has a Syllable—Says,
"You shall not wait, you are of free.
No bounds can reach within."

- Emily Dickinson
