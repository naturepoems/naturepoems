O let us wander in the woods,
  And hear the boughs reply,
  Where all the mocking echo broods,
  In murmurs soft and shy.

The whispers in the rustling leaves,
  A life that blooms anew;
  Beneath the sun's sweet light received,
  Our joys and hearts pursue.

- William Hazlitt
