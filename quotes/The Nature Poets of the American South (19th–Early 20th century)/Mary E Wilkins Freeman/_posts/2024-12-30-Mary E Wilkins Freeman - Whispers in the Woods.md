Among the trees that stand so proud,
The whispering leaves arise,
In nature’s heart, I am avowed,
As shadows softly vie.

Through dappled light, my spirit soars,
In the glades of emerald green,
Where every step, a world restores,
In peace, my soul has been.

- Mary E Wilkins Freeman
