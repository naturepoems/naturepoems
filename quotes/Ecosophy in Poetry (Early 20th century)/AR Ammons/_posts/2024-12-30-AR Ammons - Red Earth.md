Red Earth

The sun scorches,
burnishing the ground
with its heat; red earth sings,
a cradled memory.
Cracks spread,
a symphony of longing,
bound to the pulse of seasons.
Every grain,
every breath holds history,
during the silence of noon.

- AR Ammons
