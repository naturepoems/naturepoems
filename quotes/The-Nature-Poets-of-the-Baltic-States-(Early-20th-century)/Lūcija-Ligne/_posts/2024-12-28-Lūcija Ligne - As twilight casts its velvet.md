As twilight casts its velvet shroud,
And stars peek through the darkened veil,
The night unfolds, serene and proud,
In silence, stories softly sail.

The moon a guardian, wise and bright,
Illuminates the path we tread,
In nature's arms, our souls take flight,
Where every step, a dream is fed.


- Lūcija Ligne
