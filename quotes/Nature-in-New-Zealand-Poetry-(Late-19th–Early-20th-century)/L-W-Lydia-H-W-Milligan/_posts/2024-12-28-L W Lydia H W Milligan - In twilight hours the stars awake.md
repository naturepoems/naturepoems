In twilight hours, the stars awake,  
As dusk paints skies in grey,  
Among the trees, the night unfolds,  
And gently leads the day.  

Fireflies twinkle like whispered thoughts,  
In the dewy evening air,  
Nature’s lullaby echoes soft,  
Her beauty beyond compare.

- L W Lydia H W Milligan
