The meadow sings in joyful tones,
With daisies bright and violets blue,
Each blossom blooms, a heart that owns,
The beauty of a world so true.

Butterflies dance in delicate flight,
As bees hum a sweet serenade,
In nature's glow, all feels right,
In the sunlight's warm cascade.

- Richard Wagner
