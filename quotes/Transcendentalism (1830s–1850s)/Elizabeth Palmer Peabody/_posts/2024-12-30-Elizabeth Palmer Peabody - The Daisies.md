The Daisies

Lo! The daisies bloom
In the bright of noon;
They are white, and pure as snow,
With the gentle glow.

They flutter like little white stars,
In the meadow there,
Oh, blooms so bright,
They fill my heart with care.

- Elizabeth Palmer Peabody
