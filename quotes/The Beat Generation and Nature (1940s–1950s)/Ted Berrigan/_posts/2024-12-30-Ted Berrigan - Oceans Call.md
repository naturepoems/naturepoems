Ocean waves crash,
a timeless rhythm,
a pulse of the earth that
pulls us close,

voices of the sea
calling, singing,
in a cadence of salt
and foam,
a reminder that we are
parts of the whole.

And within the depth
of blue,
as horizons stretch,
we find our own paths,
treasure within
the vastness.
