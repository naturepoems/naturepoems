Falling Leaves

The leaves, they dance in autumn’s breath,
A riot of colors bright,
As every whisper of the wind
Turns day to dusk and light to night.

They twirl and spiral to the ground,
A carpet, soft and gold they lay,
Reminders of the seasons’ change,
And the fleeting nature of our day.


- A R D Fairburn
