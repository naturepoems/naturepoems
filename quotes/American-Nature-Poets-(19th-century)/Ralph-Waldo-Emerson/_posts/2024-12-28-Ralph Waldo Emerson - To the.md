To the Dandelion

Dear common flower, that grow'st beside the way,  
Fringed with brave leaves, and flowers like the sun,  
Emblem of my soul, in the sun's light,  
Let me see myself as light from you,  
Wild but free.

- Ralph Waldo Emerson
