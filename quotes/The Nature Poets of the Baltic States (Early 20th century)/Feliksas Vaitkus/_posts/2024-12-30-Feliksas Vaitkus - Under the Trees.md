Beneath the bough of ancient trees,
I find my heart in the summer breeze,
The leaves, they chatter in playful glee,
A language spoken just for me.

The shady cool, the fragrant earth,
Remind me of my simple worth,
In nature's cradle, I've come to know,
The seeds of peace continue to grow.


- Feliksas Vaitkus
