The Wind and the Stream

Wild is the wind upon the hill,
And long the shadows fall,
And dark and deep the forest glade
Where the hooting owl doth call.

But sure, it holds a softer sound,
What rumor sounds aglow,
Where rivers join, and flows along,
And I shall ever go.

- John G Whittier
