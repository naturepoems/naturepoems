Acres of ferns, untouched and lush,  
Dance in the breeze, so fine,  
Their emerald fronds raise up to greet  
The sun’s warm light divine.  

The call of distant waterfalls,  
Carries secrets from the hills,  
While nature in her gentle grace,  
Forever bends to thrills.

- L W Lydia H W Milligan
