Amidst the woodland's depths I tread,
A carpet of the fern,
Where the brook sings, and the shadows spread,
And each corner seems to yearn.

The dappled sun on mossy stone,
A dance of light and shade,
In every corner, life has grown,
An ancient serenade.

- EB Eleanor F Williams
