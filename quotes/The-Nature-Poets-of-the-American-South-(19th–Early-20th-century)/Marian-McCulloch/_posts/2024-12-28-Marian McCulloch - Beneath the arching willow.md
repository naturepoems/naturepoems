Beneath the arching willow,  
  Where shadows weave and play,  
  The heart of nature whispers,  
  In the softest, kindest way.

  With petals kissed by dewdrops,  
  And scents of earth and rain,  
  In every nook and cranny,  
  Love blossoms once again.

- Marian McCulloch
