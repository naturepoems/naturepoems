The rivers run like liquid glass,  
smoothing the jagged stones  
with patience and strength,  
breezes gust through golden fields,  
carving the future from the past—  
Nature’s dance is eternal,  
a vivid tapestry of dreams  
woven into each starry night.


- Aldo Palazzeschi
