The Pains of Sleep

My wearied soul, relieved from pain,
Turns to the mighty eve.
And over me, the deep, opaque,
Veil where the shadows lie.
...
This profound night, in a moonlit trance,
Lightening from afar, the world unknown!
... (excerpt)

- Samuel Taylor Coleridge
