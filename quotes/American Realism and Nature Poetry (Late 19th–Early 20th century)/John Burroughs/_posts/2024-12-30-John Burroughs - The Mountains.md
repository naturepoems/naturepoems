The mountains are giants, ancient and wise, 
Their faces weathered, their heads in the skies;  
With peaks that pierce the heavens' veil,  
And valleys deep where dreams set sail.  
In their shadow, I humble my soul,  
Finding my place, feeling whole.

- John Burroughs
