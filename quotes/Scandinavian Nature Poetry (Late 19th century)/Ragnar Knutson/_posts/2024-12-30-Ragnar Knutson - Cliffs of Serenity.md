The craggy cliffs against the sky,
Where seabirds nest and soar,
I feel the pulse of earth and sky,
In waves that crash on shore.

In every storm, a release,
In every calm, a grace,
I find my spirit’s sweet increase,
In nature’s wide embrace.


- Ragnar Knutson
