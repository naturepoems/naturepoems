There was a child went forth every day;
And the first object he look’d upon, that object he became,
And that object became a part of him,
For the following day, he look’d upon a tree,
The tree grew in his mind, and flourished in his thoughts.

- Walt Whitman, "There Was a Child Went Forth" (excerpt)
