Among School Children
I give you a golden nugget,
In the silence of the air.
Do you see? How it glistens,
In the quiet as the earth.

The growth beneath our feet,
Carried in the heart of sun,
And like the dreams we tether,
Ever so closely as one.


- Ezra Pound
