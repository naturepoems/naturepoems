Rippling streams and timeless stones,
Softly singing nature's song,
Each pebble tells of journeys,
Where waters danced along.

The mountains wear their crowns of trees,
Guardians of the sky,
In their sturdy silence, they put my
Restless spirit high.

- Elīna Zālīte Zalīte
