Where the wild roses grow, 
Midst the thorns of the quiet dark, 
Is a dream that we may know, 
In shades where brightest sparks.

There my sighs penetrate the soft, 
Calm air drifting lazy along; 
Let me stay here, frozen aloft, 
In night’s sweet sorrowed song.

- Dante Gabriel Rossetti
