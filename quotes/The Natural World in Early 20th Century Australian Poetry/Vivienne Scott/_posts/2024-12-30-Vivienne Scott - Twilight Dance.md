In the twilight’s gentle haze,  
The world wraps up in soft malaise,  
Stars ignite in the velvet night,  
Marking paths in the silver light.

Twirling leaves in a midnight dance,  
Nature sways in timeless trance,  
With every gust, a story grows,  
In the hush where the wild wind blows.


- Vivienne Scott
