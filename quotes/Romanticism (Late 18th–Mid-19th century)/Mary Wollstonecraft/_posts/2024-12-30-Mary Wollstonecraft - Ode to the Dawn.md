Rise, gentle dawn! The ancient trees  
Whispering peace, grant us this grace,
Their leafy crowns—the sun’s decree,
A sacred shelter, a hidden space.
Let nature be our tranquil home,
Where souls in stillness freely roam.

- Mary Wollstonecraft
