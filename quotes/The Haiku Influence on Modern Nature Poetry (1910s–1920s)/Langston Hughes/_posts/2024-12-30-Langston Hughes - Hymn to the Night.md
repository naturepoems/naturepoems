Hymn to the Night
Night, the veiled heavenly source,
Let's gather the songs of praise,
In the quiet of the silence,
Where nature softly plays.

The moonlight bathes the forest,
And wraps the earth in dreams,
Where the shadows dance around,
Until morning's light redeems.

- Langston Hughes
