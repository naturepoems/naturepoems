The wildflowers

The wildflowers pull,
hold, sway
in the breeze, singing
with colors ablaze.

Dancing in rhythm
to the pulse of the earth
as I tread lightly,

unraveling time,
watching the bright
moments make their escape
into the open sky.

- William Carlos Williams
