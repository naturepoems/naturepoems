In the quiet hour,
    clouds drift lazily,
      casting shadows on the hills,
while the landscape breathes
      a sigh of remembrance.
Every crag tells a story,
   of wind and rain,
        of time grasped in its hand,
helping us to trace
      the outlines of our hearts.


- William Stafford
