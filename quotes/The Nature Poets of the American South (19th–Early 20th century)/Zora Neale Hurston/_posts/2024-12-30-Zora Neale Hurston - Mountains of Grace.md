The mountains rise in regal grace,
Each silhouette a sacred space.
The echoes of their timeless hold,
Whisper stories yet untold.

Through valleys deep and ridges high,
Where eagles dance among the sky,
I find my joy in nature's might,
A lover of the day and night.
