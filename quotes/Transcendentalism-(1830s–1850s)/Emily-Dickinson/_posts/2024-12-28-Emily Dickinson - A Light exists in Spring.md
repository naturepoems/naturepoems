A Light exists in Spring  
Not present on the Year  
At any other period –  
When Mars is out of reach –  

And Speech itself, almost  
Like Joy,  
May be put on any name –  
Just We and It –  

All on the Poles  
Plus beams of Want –  
Or warmest heights  
Of even sound –  
—Emily Dickinson

- Emily Dickinson
