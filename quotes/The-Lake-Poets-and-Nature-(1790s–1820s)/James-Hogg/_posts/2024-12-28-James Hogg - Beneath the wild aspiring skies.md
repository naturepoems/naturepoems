Beneath the wild, aspiring skies  
My feet have wandered free,  
Where the lonesome mountain lies  
And the waves applaud the sea.  
The fast falling streams,  
In whispers soft and sweet,  
Tell tales of tender dreams  
In Nature's spirit fleet.

- James Hogg
