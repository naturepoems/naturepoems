Winter's End

The last snow melts,
revealing the earth again,
green blades of grass,
a promise of life returns.

Birdsong fills branches,
a revitalizing choir,
celebrating warmth, rest,
itself—a soft revival.

Feel the sun, soft kisses
on your back, embrace
the coming bloom.

Philip Whalen

- Philip Whalen
