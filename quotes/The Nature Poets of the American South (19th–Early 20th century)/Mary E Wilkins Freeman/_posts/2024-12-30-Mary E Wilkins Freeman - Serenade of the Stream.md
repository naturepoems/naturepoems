Where the whispering waters glide,
And the golden sunbeams play,
I wander with a tranquil pride,
In the soft, still break of day.

The flowers bloom in colors bright,
A tapestry so fair,
In nature’s lap, the heart takes flight,
In the fragrant, scented air.

- Mary E Wilkins Freeman
