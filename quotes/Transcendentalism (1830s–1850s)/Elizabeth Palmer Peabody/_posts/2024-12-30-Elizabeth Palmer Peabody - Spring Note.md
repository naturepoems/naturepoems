Spring Note

I hear the voice of spring;
It doth sing,
Through the air's clear wings,
Of the flowers that bring
In their bloom, all things;
The earth, a world in green,
Doth sheen;
Oh, the joy of the seen,
The pure delight!

- Elizabeth Palmer Peabody
