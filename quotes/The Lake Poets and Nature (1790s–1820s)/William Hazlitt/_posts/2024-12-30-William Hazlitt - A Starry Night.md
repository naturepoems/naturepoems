A starry night with balmy breaths,
  Breeding life in silent song,
  Thy thoughts to love and bequeath,
  Where every heart belongs.

With love and peace nature shall bless,
  The light that sets me free:
  In tears of joy and wild excess,
  I stand beneath the tree!

- William Hazlitt
