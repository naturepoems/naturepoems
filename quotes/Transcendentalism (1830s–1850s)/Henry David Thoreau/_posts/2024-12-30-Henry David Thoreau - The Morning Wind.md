The morning wind forever blows;
The streams of life are ever flowing;
The heart of man is never at rest;
The world is ever showing.

I never felt more alive,
Than when the sun kissed the trees,
And the flowers painted the fields,
Exploding in colors to please.


- Henry David Thoreau
