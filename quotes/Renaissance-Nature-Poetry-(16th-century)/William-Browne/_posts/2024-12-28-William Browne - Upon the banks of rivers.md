Upon the banks of rivers wide,
A tapestry of life unfolds;
Where every creature may abide,
And every tale of nature holds.

Through weeping willows, on they flow,
The currents strong, yet soft as air;
In every ripple, wisdom shows,
As nature dons her beauty rare.


- William Browne
