O Love, as long as grass doth grow,
As long as sun shall shine,
As long as moonlight shall bestow
Its glimmering light divine.

We walk the flowery meads of time,
And life is sweet and gay,
On golden paths where all shall rhyme,
And the winds shall softly play.

- Thomas Hardy
