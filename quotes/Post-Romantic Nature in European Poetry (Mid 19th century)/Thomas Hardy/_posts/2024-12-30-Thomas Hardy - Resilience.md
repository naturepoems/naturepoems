When clouds obscure the golden day,
And shadows whisper low,
The beauty still stands bright as rays,
Of memories we bestow.

So let the storms add depth to life,
And tempests stir the sea,
For out of pain and strife will rise,
The artistry of thee.

- Thomas Hardy
