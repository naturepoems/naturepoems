I love to see the sun go down,
And watch the shadows come;
I love to see the flowers grow,
And hear the birds a-hum.

I love to walk in woods so green,
And hear the leaves a-shiver;
I love to sit by winding streams,
And watch the ripples quiver.


- John Clare
