Summer
In the dawn of the summer,  
A blue sky and sweet sun,  
The air is fragrant with blossoms,  
And the day has swiftly begun.  
But in the core of the sun's might,  
In shadows of tall sunflower  
The bees come buzzing with delight  
To drink from out of each flower.

- Amy Lowell
