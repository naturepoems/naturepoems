A Blue Crab

      Little blue crab,

A noble example;

Twelve feet and two claws,

I assure swiftness.

It is a pinching straddler,

Over rock, hopping

Along the coral,

   It’s a stellar creature,

In the undulating waves.


- Marianne Moore
