The Moon is a white gossamer,  
A soundless, golden light,  
And far among the sleeping woods,  
A mystery, pure and bright.  

It flutters through the darkened trees,  
And weaves a distant song,  
And every beam is like a dream,  
And all the world moves on.  
—Emily Dickinson

- Emily Dickinson
