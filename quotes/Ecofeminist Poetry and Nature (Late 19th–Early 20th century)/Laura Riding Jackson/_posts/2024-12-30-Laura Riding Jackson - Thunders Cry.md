In every thunder’s distant growl,
The skies cry out in vibrant hue,
While raindrops paint upon the earth,
A melody of life anew.

Let the storms sweep across the land,
For every tear is but a birth,
And in the afterglow of light,
I find my place upon this earth.

- Laura Riding Jackson
