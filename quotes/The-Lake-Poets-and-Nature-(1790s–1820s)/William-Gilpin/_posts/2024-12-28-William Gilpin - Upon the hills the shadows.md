Upon the hills the shadows creep,
As dusk descends and swallows light;
And golden stars awake from sleep,
To pierce the velvet night.
The whisper of the crickets rise,
In serenades of love;
While moonbeams silver dreams of skies,
Float gently from above.

- William Gilpin
