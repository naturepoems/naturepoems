In the quiet woods I roam,
Beneath the trees so tall,
Nature sings a gentle tome,
In the night’s enchanting thrall.

The moonlight bathes the ground,
In silvered beams so bright,
In a world so profound,
I lose myself in night.

- Mary E Wilkins Freeman
