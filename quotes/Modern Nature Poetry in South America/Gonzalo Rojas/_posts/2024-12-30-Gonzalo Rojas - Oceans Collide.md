Oceans collide
against the shores of my fate.
Deep in the blue,
I feel their whispers,
waves drawn
by the moon’s caress.

Ah, sweet solitude,
under the stars,
again I hear
the calls of the night,
and the silence breathes,
like an ancient hush,
in the palm trees’ glow.

- Gonzalo Rojas
