In the stillness of the forest,
Trees stand tall, guardians wise,
Their roots deep in the earth,
Branches brushing the sky.

Shade and sun play,
A timeless dance of light,
Whispers of leaves,
Tell stories of the wind's sigh.

- Władysław Broniewski
