As the sun dips low,
a horizon painted bright,
colors cascade,
a slow dance of shadows and light.

In the midst of change,
a stillness remains,
a calm before the twilight,
the promise of night to come,
a continuation of the cycle,
a harmony in chaos.

- Alejandra Zambra
