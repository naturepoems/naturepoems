The fragrant blooms of summer’s kiss,
Awake the senses, fill the air,
As butterflies perform their dance,
In gardens rich with colors rare.

Each petal soft, each leaf aglow,
In sunlight’s warm and gold embrace,
Nature’s wonders, freely show,
A harmony, a sacred place.


- Rudolph Rudy H Dalrymple
