In the whisper of the pines,
Underneath the vast sky's blue,
Nature's song begins to twine,
Softly echoing, pure and true.

Fragrance of the earth, so sweet,
Gentle rivers dance and flow,
In every corner, life replete,
Such beauty in the sunlight's glow.


- Mirdza Ķeniņš
