To a Wren

Little Wren, that flits about,
    Twinkling with glee,
In the bushes round me here,
    Joyous and free,
What is love to you, dear heart?
    Tell me, if you can;
With your song that fills the morning
    While it flits and ran.


- Alfred Domett
