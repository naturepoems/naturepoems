Ode to the Mountain

Tall and proud, 
You pierce the clouds,  
Carved by time's slow hand,  
A monument to peace,  
In your shade we find repose.

The valleys sing beneath,  
Rivers trace your roots,  
In your stillness,  
We glimpse eternity,  
A meaning far beyond our reach.


- George Ripley
