A Farewell

The softening winds blow sweetly sighs,  
  A whisper through the foliage green;  
And echoes seem to rise and rise,  
  As lovers pause to glean—  
Yet, I would kiss the whispered wave,  
  That laps where yellows gleam.

- George Meredith
