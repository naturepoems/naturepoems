The sea calls out with mighty waves,
A rhythm, strong and bold,
Its azure heart, a treasure trove,
Of mysteries yet to unfold.

Upon the shore, the gulls take flight,
Their cries a song of freedom sung,
While distant horizons beckon forth,
A journey waiting to be begun.

- Margaret Atkinson
