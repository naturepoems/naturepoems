A man said to the sea,  
'What power do you have?'  
The sea replied,  
'You cannot fathom my depths;  
You cannot know my moods.  
All that is yours  
is never made mine.'  
The tides ebb and flow,  
Like the whispers of a lover,  
Like the unreturned calls  
of distant suns.

- Stephen Crane
