AWAKENING OF THE FIELD  
Awake, dear field,  
And take the sun’s hand,  
For a dance on this dawn,  
Raise the dew as tears of joy,  
In warm embrace.  

The colors call in waves  
Of green and gold,  
As silence is broken  
By the songs of life  
Flourishing in abundance.  


- Bessie Head
