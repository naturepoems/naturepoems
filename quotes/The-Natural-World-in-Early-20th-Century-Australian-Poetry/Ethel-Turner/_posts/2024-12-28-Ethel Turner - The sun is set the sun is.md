The sun is set, the sun is set;
To-night the dark is near,
A hush on every whispering bough,
A hush on every cheer.

And then the radiant moon will rise,
With pale and ghostly light;
And in the solemn silence sing,
Of earth's serene delight.

- Ethel Turner
