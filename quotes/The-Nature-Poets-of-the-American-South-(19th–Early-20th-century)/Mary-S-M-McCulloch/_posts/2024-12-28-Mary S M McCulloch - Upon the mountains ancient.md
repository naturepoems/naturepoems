Upon the mountain's ancient crest,
Where eagles dare to soar,
The winds of time, they never rest,
And echo evermore.

The valleys deep, where shadows play,
Hold secrets of the past,
In every stone and sunlit ray,
A memory, steadfast.


- Mary S M McCulloch
