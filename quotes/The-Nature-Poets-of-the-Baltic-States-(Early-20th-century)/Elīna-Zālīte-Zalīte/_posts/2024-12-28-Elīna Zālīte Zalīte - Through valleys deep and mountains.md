Through valleys deep and mountains wide,
A path of wonder flows,
Nature cradles dreams inside,
In every bloom that grows.

Here I wander, here I roam,
In nature's vast embrace,
With every step, I find my home,
In this sacred space.

- Elīna Zālīte Zalīte
