A silver moon upon the lake,
Reflects the stars in silent cheer,
The gentle breeze begins to wake,
Carrying dreams both far and near.

The nightingale sings tales of old,
In melodies that weave and wind,
Through forests deep and fields of gold,
The heart of nature entwined.


- Mirdza Ķeniņš
