Transient Dreams
Fog rolls like breath in the morning,
Kissing the world awake anew,
Each droplet a fleeting promise,
Of mysteries concealed from view.

Sunflowers turn with bated breath,
Following warmth from astral zenith,
Chasing phantoms, casting shadows,
In the dance of light’s sweet death.


- Dadas Rallying Poets
