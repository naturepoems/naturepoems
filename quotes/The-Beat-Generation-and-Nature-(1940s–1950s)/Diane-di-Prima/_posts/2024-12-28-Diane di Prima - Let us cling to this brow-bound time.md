Let us cling to this brow-bound time,  
a well of earth’s hushed breath;  
a book opening like the structure of canyons,  
a slung bow of shadow  
against the sharp sun’s mouth.  
Nature is alive  
far in the middle of beauty’s cores,  
in the way the blossoms chat
from their trees.

— Diane di Prima

- Diane di Prima
