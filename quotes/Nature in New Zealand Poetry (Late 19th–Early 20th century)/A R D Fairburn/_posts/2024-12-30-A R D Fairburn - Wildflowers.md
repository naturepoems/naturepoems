Wildflowers

In meadows where wildflowers grow,
A palette splashed by nature’s hand,
Each bloom a story, soft and slow,
An essence of the timeless land.

Their colors dance in golden light,
A gentle sway to breezes’ song,
With joyous spirits taking flight,
A celebration of where we belong.


- A R D Fairburn
