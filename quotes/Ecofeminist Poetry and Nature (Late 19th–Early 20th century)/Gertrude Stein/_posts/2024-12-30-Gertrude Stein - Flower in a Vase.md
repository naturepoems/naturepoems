A flower in a vase
Is a work of art,
You can look at it, you can love it,
You can feel it ante, you can feel it say.

To be an invitation to the passing
Of a wave, a tide, of eyes passing away,
Between the sun and the mud,
A flower in a vase is a place to stay.

- Gertrude Stein
