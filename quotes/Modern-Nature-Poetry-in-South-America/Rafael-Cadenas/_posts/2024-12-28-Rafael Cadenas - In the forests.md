In the forest’s heart,
 stillness sings,
 the pulse of life 
 beats beneath the bark,
 where every leaf 
 is a witness 
 to the stories 
 of the sun and rain,

and the earth, in its wisdom,
 cradles the chaos
of existence,
 weaving harmony 
 in the wild dance of nature.


- Rafael Cadenas
