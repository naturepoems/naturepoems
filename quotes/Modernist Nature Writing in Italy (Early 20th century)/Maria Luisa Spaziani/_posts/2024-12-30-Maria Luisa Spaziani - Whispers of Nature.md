Divine scent, a whisper rides along the air.
Grasses sway, in deep sunlit currents,
The light kisses my skin, warm and inviting.
Shadows dance in the grove, vibrant,
Each leaf tells a story, a secret, unknown,
The earth breathes beneath the sky, endlessly blue.
