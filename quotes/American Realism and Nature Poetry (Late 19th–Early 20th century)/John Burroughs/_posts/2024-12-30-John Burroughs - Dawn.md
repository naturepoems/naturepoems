Each day brings a dawning light, 
As the world awakens from the night;  
The sun’s warm kiss, the sky aglow,  
Awakens dreams and hopes to grow.  
In each sunrise, the promise stays,  
Of love and beauty in life’s arrays.

- John Burroughs
