In twilight hues, the day retreats,
The colors blend in soft caress,
As darkness falls, the night entreats,
To share its secrets, none can guess.

Faint stars peer through the tender veil,
While crickets weave their serenade,
In nature's arms, all worries pale,
As dreams awaken in the glade.


- Mirdza Ķeniņš
