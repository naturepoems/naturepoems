Underneath the silvered sky,  
  Where rivers curl and flow,  
  The breath of earth is heavy  
  With secrets it might know.

  The mountains wear their wildness,  
  The valleys quilted wide,  
  In every nook and cranny  
  A thousand dreams do bide.

- Marian McCulloch
