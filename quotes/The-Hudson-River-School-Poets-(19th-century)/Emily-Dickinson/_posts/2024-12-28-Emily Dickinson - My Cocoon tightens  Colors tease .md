My Cocoon tightens – Colors tease –  
I choose my own –  
The Sunshine must be given way  
To keep the comets known –  

The Moon still idle at my side,  
No Date in this vicinity –  
Through Shatter, handpick – helpless, clear –  
And empty Feet towards me –

- Emily Dickinson
