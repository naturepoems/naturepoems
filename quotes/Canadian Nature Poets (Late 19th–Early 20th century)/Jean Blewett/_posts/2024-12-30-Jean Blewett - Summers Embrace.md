With the blush of summer's glow,
The fields burst forth in color bright,
Where every bud begins to show,
Nature’s wonder in full sight.

In her arms, the world does twirl,
As laughter dances on the breeze,
Paths of gold where wildflowers swirl,
A joyful song that never flees.

- Jean Blewett
