A dusk of sleeping flowers lies
Beneath the drifting moon's pale light,
And silver stars in velvet skies
Shine softly with the coming night.

The shadows gather, sweet and deep,
While breezes hum a soft refrain;
Then let me close my eyes and sleep
Beneath the Silver Birch, my lane.

- Joyce Kilmer
