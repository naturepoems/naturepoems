My love is like the fragrant rose,
That grows beside the stream,
So pure and rare, my heart it knows,
That blossomed from a dream.

And every leaf that trembles light,
That dances by the shore,
Is like the thrill, the sweet delight,
Of love that lives no more.

- Lady Mary Wroth
