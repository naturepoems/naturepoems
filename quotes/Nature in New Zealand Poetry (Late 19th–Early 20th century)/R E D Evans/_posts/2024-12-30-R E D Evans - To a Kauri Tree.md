To a Kauri Tree
Awake, majestic Kauri!
In your fragrant crown,
Gaze upon the heavens,
Your limbs held high,
In ancient solemnity.
Guarding stories of the past,
Whispers in the leaves,
Beckoning the wanderer,
To find solace under your shade.

- R E D Evans
