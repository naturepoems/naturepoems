In Work
In work and toil,
with nature's breath,
we find the way,
a rhythm sung in
joy, in struggle, in sweat;
often forgive the time lost
between dusk and dawn.

Out there
the rivers run,
the earth breathes softly
as we walk,
furrows deep,
as wild creatures come.

— Gary Snyder
