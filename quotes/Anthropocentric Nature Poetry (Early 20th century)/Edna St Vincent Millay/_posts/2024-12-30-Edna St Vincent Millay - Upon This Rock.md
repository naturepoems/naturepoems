Upon this rock, that laves the shore,
I sat alone at dawn.
The world was waking; birds did soar,
And the breeze whispered on and on.
Oh, the joy in each moment,
In the stillness of the air!
For nature is love, is beauty, is hope,
And I breathe it in with care.

- Edna St Vincent Millay
