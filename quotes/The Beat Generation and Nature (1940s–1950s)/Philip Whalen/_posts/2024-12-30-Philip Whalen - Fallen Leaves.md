Fallen Leaves

Crisp and dry,
falling leaves
carry within them
stories of life.

Breath of autumn
whispers the end
of all that is green,
readying for silence.

Each crackle beneath
footsteps holds treasures,
remnants of summer's
emotional dance.

Philip Whalen
