O gentle is the morning air,
And deep the coolness of the wood.
When silence walks, in peace and bare,
I find my soul in solitude.

The river’s heart is wide and clear;
The foamy ripples softly glide.
Come walk with me, my love, come near,
As sunlight breaks upon the tide.


- Rupert Brooke
