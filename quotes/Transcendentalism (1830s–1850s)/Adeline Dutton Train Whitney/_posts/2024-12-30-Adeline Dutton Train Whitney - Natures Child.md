Nature's Child
With eyes so bright and spirit free,
A child of nature I would be;
To dance in fields where daisies grow,
And paint the skies with colors’ glow.
To wander far where wild things play,
And savor life in every way;
Oh, to be a child of thee,
Nature, in all thy majesty.

— Adeline Dutton Train Whitney
