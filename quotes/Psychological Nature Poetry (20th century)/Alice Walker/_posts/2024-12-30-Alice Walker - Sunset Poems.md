Sunsets are poems, 
scribbled on the skin of the sky,
almost too beautiful to hold.
They remind me that endings
can melt into a soft sigh,
a promise of dawn waiting,
nudging us toward tomorrow.

Look closely,
for beauty blooms
in the depths of goodbye.


- Alice Walker
