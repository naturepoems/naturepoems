The trees are in their autumn beauty,
The woodland paths are dry,
Under the evening beaming sun
The choristers melodize.

Beneath the pines youth’s laughter does not die,
From where the soil does sigh,
With scattered leaves that whisper by,
Freeing themselves as the sky.

— To Autumn

- John Keats
