The Grasshoppers

Some can eat the fat rose,
Some can chew a stem.
At the edge of the grass-and-leaf
Honey goes from flower,
From flower to flower,
All summer long,At night,
The sky is dark,
But in the morning,
The grasshoppers chirp
And the world is dense with small sounds.

- Carl Sandburg
