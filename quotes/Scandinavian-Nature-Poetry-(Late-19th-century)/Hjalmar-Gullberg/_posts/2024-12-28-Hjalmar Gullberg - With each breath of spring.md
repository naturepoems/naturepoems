With each breath of spring,  
New life starts to bloom,  
Birds sing their sweet songs,  
Banishing the gloom.  

The vibrant wild meadows,  
So full of delight,  
Awaken our spirits,  
In the soft morning light.

- Hjalmar Gullberg
