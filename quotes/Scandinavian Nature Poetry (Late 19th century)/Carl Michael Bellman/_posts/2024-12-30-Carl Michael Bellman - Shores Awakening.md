The morning mist upon the shore,
A silken veil of dreams,
In every wave, a gentle roar,
A dance of light it seems.

The ocean breathes, a timeless sound,
As seagulls cry above,
In nature's arms, my heart is found,
A world of endless love.


- Carl Michael Bellman
