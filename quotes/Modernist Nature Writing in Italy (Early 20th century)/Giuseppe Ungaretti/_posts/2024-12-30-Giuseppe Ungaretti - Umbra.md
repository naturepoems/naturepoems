Umbra

        Shade  
        of the leaves,  
        Stretched upon the earth;
        It cradles the day,  
        The serene song,
        The laughter of a child,
        The rise of a sundown,  
        Gloriously wrapping the dusk.
        
        
        


- Giuseppe Ungaretti
