Clouds and Stars

The clouds flit by,
Like thoughts in the night,
And the stars twinkle bright,
Through the dusky flight.

They light up the sky,
With their shimmering grace,
And the moon, a pale face,
Watches over the space.

- Elizabeth Palmer Peabody
