Near the brook, where daisies sway,
And the lilacs softly bloom,
Life unfolds in sweet array,
Banishing all sense of gloom.

Here, time urges not on speed,
But lingers, like the gentle stream,
Each moment's like an unplucked seed,
Ripening in the sun's warm gleam.
