A morning dew upon the grass,  
As daylight breaks anew,  
Sprinkles nature with a joy,  
In every crystal view.  

Each blade reflects the promise,  
Of a day that’s bright and fair,  
A moment’s grace in silence,  
In the soft and fragrant air.

- Selma Lagerlöf
