Beneath the canopy of stars,
Where night unfolds her velvet cloak,
The secrets of the earth are ours,
In silence, nature’s heart awoke.

With every sound, a heartbeat true,
The rustling leaves, the owls that call,
In this embrace, I find anew,
The pulse of life, the sacred thrall.


- Rudolph Rudy H Dalrymple
