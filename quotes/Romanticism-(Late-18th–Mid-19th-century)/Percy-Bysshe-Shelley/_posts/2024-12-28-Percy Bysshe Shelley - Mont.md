Mont Blanc
The everlasting snow
And the high crushing wind,
The silence of the mountains—
Where the eagles soar and bend.

Glistening rocks crumble,
Before the mighty hand,
That carved these higher volumes,
In a deep majestic land.






- Percy Bysshe Shelley
