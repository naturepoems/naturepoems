The dawn awakes with hues so bold,
In golden crowns of light,
A promise for the day to hold,
In nature’s pure delight.

As shadows fade, and colors bloom,
These moments, fleeting, dear,
In every breath, an endless room,
Where life becomes a sphere.

- EB Eleanor F Williams
