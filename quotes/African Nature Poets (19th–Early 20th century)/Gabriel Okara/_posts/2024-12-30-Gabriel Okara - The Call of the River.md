**The Call of the River**  
O river!  
You call my name  
With your murmur,  
You tug at my soul  
With your gentle pull.  
Your waters flow,  
As my heart beats with thee,  
Soft echoes of your laughter  
Blend with the winds of the trees.


- Gabriel Okara
