De profundis,
A vast blue sky above,
A whispering of leaves,
The rustle of the wind,
Nature sings its sorrow.

In the depths of the forest,
Where the sun barely passes,
Lies a truth, stark and cold,
Silence wrapped in shadows.

- Witold Gombrowicz
