Cinq, de la Saison
In the land of the lilies,
When the string breaks—
   You will lie against a floated arch,
And your heart will break,
   And you shall cast it off,
Let us lean and laugh...
   Body of conceiving,  
To take flight 
   At the making of the bouquet.

- Ezra Pound
