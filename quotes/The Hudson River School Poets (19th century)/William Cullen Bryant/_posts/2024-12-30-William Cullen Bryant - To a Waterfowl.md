The beauty of the summer day,   
The daisy in bloom,   
Where the soft purple glows,   
And the joy that soon dooms.   
In the reach of memory flames,   
Of the glory-bearers;  
Where birds are singing,   
And all as divine as the dreamer's.

- William Cullen Bryant
