Rain falls gently,
dancing on the leaves,
a soft song that
fills the air.

The earth breathes,
water beads on grass,
creating a mirror
to the sky above.

As droplets fall,
each becomes a world,
a universe reflecting,
as life grows
eagerly,
a little brighter now.

- Ted Berrigan
