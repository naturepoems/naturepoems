The clouds lie heavy,
     Dark and full,
Over the mountains,
        Heavy with thoughts,
         Spilling shadows,
           Vast and rolling.
With each sigh of wind
   Through branches,
They whisper
            To the earth,
          A strange desire.

- Sylvia Plath
