A crystalline snowflakes dance,
In the soft winter's light,
Blanketing the earth in peace,
Bringing silence to the night.

With each flake's gentle landing,
The world is hushed and still,
Nature wraps its arms around,
In an icy, tranquil thrill.

- Lillian B Miller
