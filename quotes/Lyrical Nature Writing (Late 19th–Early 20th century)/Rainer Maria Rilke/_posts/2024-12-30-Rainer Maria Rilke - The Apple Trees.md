The Apple Trees

We live in a world,
    crammed with that most ancient of trees;
    those apple trees that flower
    in spring, by the bitter frost.

    When I think of the flowers,
    fragrant in the sun,
    it speaks of a heart,
    and love, that never fades.

—Rainer Maria Rilke
