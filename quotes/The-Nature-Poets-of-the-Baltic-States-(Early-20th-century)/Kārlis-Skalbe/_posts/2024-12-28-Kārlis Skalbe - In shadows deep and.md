In shadows deep and wide,
The ancient trees abide,
Each whispering leaf recalls
The secrets of time's tide.

Amidst the tangled roots,
Where wildflowers bloom,
Life's chorus sings of spring,
In nature’s fragrant room.

- Kārlis Skalbe
