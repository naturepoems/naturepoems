Upon the shores where breezes kiss,
And waves articulate their song,
The silence births a gentle bliss,
In nature’s arms, we all belong.

With every tide that ebbs and flows,
A rhythm deep, a heartbeat true,
The ocean’s breath, a sacred prose,
Whispers secrets, old yet new.


- William H Prescott
