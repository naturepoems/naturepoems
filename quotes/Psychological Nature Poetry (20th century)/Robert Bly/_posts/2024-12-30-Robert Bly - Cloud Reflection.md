Cloud Reflection
The clouds mirrored on the lake,
a shifting world, a broken sky,
a place where I suspended my worries,
traveling through feathered dreams,
each ripple carrying stories
from far, far away,
light and shadow woven together,
a breath of eternity.

- Robert Bly
