I trace paths of 
a forgotten lake,  
my footsteps linger 
in the scent of mud and moss,  
a tender memory  
wrapped in the embrace of trees;  
I learn to breathe again 
as petals shatter 
in the warmth of dawn.

- Ana Cristina Cesar
