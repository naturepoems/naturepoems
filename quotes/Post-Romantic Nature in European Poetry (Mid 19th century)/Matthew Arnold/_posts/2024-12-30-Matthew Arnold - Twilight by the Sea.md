By the sea, the tender twilight
   Brings its murmurs of the past,
And the waves, in whispers flight,
   Glide in silence, gently cast.

O, the peace of this moment,
   In the heart of nature's shell;
As the winds breathe out her sonnet,
   In a quiet, mystic spell.

- Matthew Arnold
