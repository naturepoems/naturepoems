The ocean waves, a lullaby,  
Kissing the shore with a gentle sigh;  
In their rhythm, my heart takes flight,  
With the ebb and flow of day and night.  
Each grain of sand, a story kept,  
In nature’s book where my soul has slept.

- John Burroughs
