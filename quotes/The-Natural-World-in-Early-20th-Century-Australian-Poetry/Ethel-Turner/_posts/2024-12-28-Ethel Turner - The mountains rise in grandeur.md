The mountains rise in grandeur vast,
Their snowy caps a-glow,
As if the sun's warm hues are cast,
Upon the world below.

The valleys deep, the rivers wide,
In nature's splendor flow,
In every turn, from side to side,
The beauty of the show.

- Ethel Turner
