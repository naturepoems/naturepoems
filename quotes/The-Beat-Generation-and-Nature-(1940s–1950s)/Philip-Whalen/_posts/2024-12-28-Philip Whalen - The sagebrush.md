The sagebrush smell,
   soaked in sunlight,
   drifting with the wind
   like whispering tongues,
   swaying along the brink
   of dusk's fading light,
   I breathe it in, deeply,
   sense of belonging rolled
   through my skin, essential.
By and by a day passes.


- Philip Whalen
