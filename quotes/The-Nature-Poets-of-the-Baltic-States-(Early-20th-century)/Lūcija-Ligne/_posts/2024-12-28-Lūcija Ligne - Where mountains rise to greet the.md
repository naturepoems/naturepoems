Where mountains rise to greet the sky,
And valleys cradle rivers deep,
The beauty stirs a longing sigh,
For nature's heart, a treasure to keep.

With every breeze that brushes past,
And every wildflower that blooms,
The spirit of the earth holds fast,
In halls of green, amidst the glooms.


- Lūcija Ligne
