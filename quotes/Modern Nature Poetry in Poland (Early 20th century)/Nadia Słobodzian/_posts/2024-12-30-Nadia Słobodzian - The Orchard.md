The orchard bursts with ripeness,  
a bounty of nature's love,  
each fruit a promise fulfilled,  
a blessing in leaf and sun.  

Gather the harvest,  
taste the sweetness of the earth,  
as life circles back  
into the hands of time.

- Nadia Słobodzian
