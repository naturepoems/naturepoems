Upon the meadow's grassy lane,
Where wildflowers bloom in colors bright,
There blooms the joy, there fades the pain,
In the gentle touch of golden light.
The brooklet sings with a sparkling laugh,
As I sit upon the sunlit path.

- Edgar Lee Masters
