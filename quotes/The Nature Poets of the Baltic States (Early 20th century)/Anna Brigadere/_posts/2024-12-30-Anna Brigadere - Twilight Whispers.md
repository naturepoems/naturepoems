Imbued with the hush of twilight,
The forest whispers its lore:
Underneath the boughs so sprightly,
Every tree tells tales of yore.

Secrets of the roots undying,
In silence, saplings breathe and sway.
Nature's heart, in stillness lying,
Reflects the fading light of day.

- Anna Brigadere
