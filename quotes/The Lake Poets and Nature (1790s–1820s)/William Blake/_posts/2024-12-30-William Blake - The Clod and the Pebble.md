The Clod and the Pebble  
Loving to Love,  
And caution to take flight  
Left a clod of the valley,  
A pebble from the shore.  
  
Love has nothing but love  
Return, return to night!  
Love seeks its own delight  
Return, return, take flight.  
**William Blake**

- William Blake
