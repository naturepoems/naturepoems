To the River

Thou sacred stream, that rolls along,
With murmurs soft and sweet;  

In every bud, and every song,
I feel my heart's retreat.

Oh, let me rest upon thy side,
And in thy waters see  

The fleeting shades of time abide,
Reflecting joys in me.


- Philip Freneau
