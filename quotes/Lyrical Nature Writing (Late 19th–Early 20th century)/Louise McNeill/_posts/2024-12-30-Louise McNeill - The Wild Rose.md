The Wild Rose

Where the wild rose blows,
   In the light of the dawn,
   With the dew on her petals,
   I wander with song.

And I laugh with the leaf,
   And I dance with the breeze,
   As I gather my dreams
   From the roots of the trees.


- Louise McNeill
