The long slender cottonwoods  
That lifted on the Wind.  
In all the world — the gleam of Sun —  
Will tell their story still!  

For they are filled with Nature —  
And dance in joy today —  
Whose whispers linger — sweetly —  
And cast the Winter ... End!  


- Emily Dickinson
