As twilight drapes the land in gold,
And shadows start to weave,
The stories of the dusk unfold,
In every breath we breathe.

Nature's gentle hand creates,
A canvas rich and rare,
In every moment, love awaits,
In the twilight's tender care.

- Elīna Zālīte Zalīte
