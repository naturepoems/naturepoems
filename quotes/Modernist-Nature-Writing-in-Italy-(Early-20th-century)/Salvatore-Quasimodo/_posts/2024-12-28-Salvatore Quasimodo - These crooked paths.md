These crooked paths,  
Where the eternal sunshine hits,  
Seem to listen to the footfalls  
Of my heart, torn asunder.

The leaves converse with me;  
The rustle of hope  
That bears the heart's sigh  
Is a dirge that wanes to white mist.

- Salvatore Quasimodo
