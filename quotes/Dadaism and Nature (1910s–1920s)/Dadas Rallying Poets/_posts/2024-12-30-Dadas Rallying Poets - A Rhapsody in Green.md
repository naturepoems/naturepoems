A Rhapsody in Green
Over hills of emerald waves,
Where silence reigns in hues of grace,
The whispering grass kisses the breeze,
Galloping freely through the space.

Branches sway, a ballet grand,
Beneath the watchful gaze of skies,
Nature’s tapestry unfolds,
Each thread a moment passing by.


- Dadas Rallying Poets
