Wind and Sea

The wind is dancing on the sea,
A rippling music on the deep;
It sets the boats a-wondering,
And fills the sails with joy and leap.

The waves leap up to touch the sky,
Their brilliant tongues of white unfurl,
And I who gaze upon the scene,
Am lost within its endless swirl.


- A R D Fairburn
