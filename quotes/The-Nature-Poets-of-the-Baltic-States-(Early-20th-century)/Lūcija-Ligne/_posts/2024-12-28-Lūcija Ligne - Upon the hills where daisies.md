Upon the hills where daisies sway,
And sunbeams paint the morning bright,
The world awakens to the day,
In hues of gold, and pure delight.

The sky a canvas, vast and clear,
With clouds that drift like ships of old,
In every breath, the earth draws near,
A symphony of green and gold.


- Lūcija Ligne
