The ocean's roar, a mighty hymn,
Against the cliffs, the waves crash low,
In every splash, the rhythm's trim,
A testament to ebb and flow.

The tides embrace both loss and gain,
In salty breeze, we find our sail,
To ride the waves, through joy and pain,
In nature's heart, we shall prevail.


- Lūcija Ligne
