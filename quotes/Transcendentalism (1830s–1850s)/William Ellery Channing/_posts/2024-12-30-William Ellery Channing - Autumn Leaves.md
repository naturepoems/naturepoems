The autumn leaves, in fiery hues,
A tapestry of change,
In every gust, in every muse,
Life’s cycles rearrange.

Amidst the gold, I pause to see,
The beauty in the fade,
In nature’s art, I am set free,
As life’s sweet debt is paid.
