A Porch

From an unbuilt  
porch  

one could see  

the stars above  

and from each tiny  

piece of stone  
the view could rest  

across whatevers chief business  
is.  

Up in the night  
the real triangles  

hovered as one needs  
to breathe.

- William Carlos Williams
