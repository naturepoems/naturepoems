Upon the winding pathway,  
  Where wild ferns sweetly grow,  
  The forest breathes a secret,  
  That only few may know.

  The dappled light filters softly,  
  Through the arms of ancient trees,  
  Where whispers of the spirits  
  Sing softly on the breeze.

- Marian McCulloch
