My Poet's Heart
The river of this world
flares and rounds like the sun,
yet I have no other
place to go.
I plead and plead
with the trees, but still
my cheeks are against
this moss.
Let the rivers come
and lick your feet;
it is good to wander
with the leaves in your hands.



- Robert Bly
