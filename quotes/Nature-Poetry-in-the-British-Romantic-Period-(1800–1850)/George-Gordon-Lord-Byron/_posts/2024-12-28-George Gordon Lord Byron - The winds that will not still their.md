The winds that will not still their breath
Have swept my soul from shore;
Where flooded waters, in their death,
Still linger, as before.

But love for Nature's soul still shines
In every autumn hue,
And all her gardens wrap their vines,
As with a kindly dew.

- George Gordon Lord Byron
