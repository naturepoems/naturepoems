In the whisper of the wind,
Through the forests deep and wide,
Nature speaks in tones so kind,
To the river's flowing tide.

Each leaf tells a story new,
Each flower blooms in beauty rare,
The sun, the sky, a canvas blue,
Paints the earth with tender care.

- Anna Brigadere
