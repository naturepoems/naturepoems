IN THE HILLS

When we merry wanderers roam,
Joy is in the hills we climb,
Where the forest grants us home,
And the meadows sing in rhyme.

Let our laughter light the way,
As we dance through the woods so green;  
For in nature’s arms we shall stay,  
Forever bound, forever seen.  


- A E Housman
