Out from the blooming vale of dreams
  The nightingale does sigh,
  When wildest winds and deepest streams,
  Of lullabies gone by.

Each rosy dawn shall bring to me,
  Twinkling stars by day,
  Through every pause and memory,
  In nature’s bright array.

- William Hazlitt
