O birds that fly in free delight,
Over hills and valleys wide,
Your songs are music to the heart,
In you, the wild woods' pride.

With every flutter, every call,
You paint the sky with endless grace,
Your wings, the brush of nature's hand,
In eternal, soaring space.

- Margaret Walker
