Is love a flower that shall fully bloom,
From sweetest spring,
Or in the woodlands of my room,
Like a little creature sings?

A rose in whom to pray shall rest.
And who can understand or find,
A guide falling on simple zest,
Whose form and light are blind?

- Coventry Patmore
