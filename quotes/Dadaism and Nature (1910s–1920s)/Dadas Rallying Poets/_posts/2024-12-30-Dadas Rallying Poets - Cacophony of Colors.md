Cacophony of Colors
Nature bursts in loud rejoicing,
Colors clash, collide and meld,
In chaos blooms a sweet discord,
As petals cry, “Behold, we’ve held!”

Roses blush in wild abandon,
Daisies giggle, carefree and bright,
Violets whisper secrets softly,
In the riot of a springtime night.


- Dadas Rallying Poets
