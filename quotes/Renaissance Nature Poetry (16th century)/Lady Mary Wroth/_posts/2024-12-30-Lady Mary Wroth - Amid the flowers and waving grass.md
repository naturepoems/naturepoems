Amid the flowers and waving grass,
Where sunlight drips like dew,
I find the joys that moments pass,
In Nature's grand review.

The sky unfurls its azure blue,
The clouds drift soft and light,
In every bloom, my heart I woo,
To dance within the night.

- Lady Mary Wroth
