What is there in me that I should need
To take my own in hand,
To plant and reap, to sow the seed,
By my own clear command?

The earth was naked and bare,
More naked I sit at her side;
I weave my thoughts like a weaving air,
And my heart will not abide.


- Edna St Vincent Millay
