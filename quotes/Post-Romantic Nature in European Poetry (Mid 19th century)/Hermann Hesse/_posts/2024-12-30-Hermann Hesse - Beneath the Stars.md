Beneath the stars, the earth does breathe,
In soft and tender sighs,
A symphony of life awakens,
As day meets night, it dies.

But in this loss, a beauty blooms,
In darkness, light is spun,
For nature knows the secret song,
Of cycles never done.


- Hermann Hesse
