Sonnet LXIX
In the blue mornings
when the winds weigh nothing,
when all the light
appears in your face,
like morning silk,

I caress the world
with my liquid fingers
and I plunge them,
among the trembling leaves,
to awaken them to the afternoon.


- Pablo Neruda
