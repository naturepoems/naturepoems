Ode to the winter night,
The stars, a distant glow,
As snowflakes weave a quilt of white,
On earth below so slow.

In every icy breath,
The world sits hushed and bright,
Wrapped in a gauzy blanket,
Of the tranquil, tender night.


- Ragnar Knutson
