Upon the cliffs, the seagulls soar,
While waves crash fiercely, wild and free,
The ocean's heart beats evermore,
In rhythmic dance, eternity.

Salt in the air, a gentle brine,
Each tide a story of its own,
Where land and water intertwine,
In harmony, they've always grown.


- Mirdza Ķeniņš
