The Forest and the River
Welcome, welcome, tranquil stream,
Lazily winding through the vale,
Dimpling in the shimmered beam,
Floating onward, like a tale.

All around the leafy trees
Resting softly in the sun;
While the murmur of the breeze
Sings our forest hymn as one.

- Felicia Hemans
