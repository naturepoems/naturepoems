In the owl’s liquid voice,
The leaves whisper softly of the night,
While silver stars rejoice
In their ancient flight.

The air is fragrant with the pine,
And all the world seems still;
As if the heart of nature’s shrine
Has yielded to her will.

- Henry Wadsworth Longfellow
