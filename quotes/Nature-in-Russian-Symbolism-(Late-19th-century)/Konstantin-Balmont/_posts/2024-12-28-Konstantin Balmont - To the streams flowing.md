To the streams flowing,  
To the trembling flowers,  
I sing in silence.  
   With wonder after you,  
A ray dancing,  
A whirlwind of ecstasies,  
I run to the heart of nature,  
   Where your heart beats in the glow.


- Konstantin Balmont
