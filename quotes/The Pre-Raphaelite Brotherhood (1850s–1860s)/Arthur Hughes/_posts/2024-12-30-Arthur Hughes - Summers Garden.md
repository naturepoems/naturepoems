Amidst the blooms of summer's grace,
   The bees in joy do hum,
Their busy dance, a sweet embrace,
   In gardens rich and plumb.
  
Oh, let me lie in fragrant air,
   And feel the sun's warm hand,
For in these moments, free from care,
   I know I understand.


- Arthur Hughes
