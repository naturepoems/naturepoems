Still Here

I been scared and battered.
My hopes the wind
tossed and scattered.
Snowed and rained on,
I’m still here.

The race and fuel of all
Braced against me.
I been hurt, stomped and spilled.
Yet through the shadows,
I’m still here.

- Langston Hughes
