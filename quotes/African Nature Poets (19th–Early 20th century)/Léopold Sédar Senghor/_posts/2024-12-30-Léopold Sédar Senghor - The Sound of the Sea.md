The Sound of the Sea
The sea whispers secrets,
In a language known to few,
Crashing waves caress the shore,
With stories of distance, of azure.

Each wave a memory,
Each foam a dance,
In the salted air,
The world finds its rhythm, its chance.


- Léopold Sédar Senghor
