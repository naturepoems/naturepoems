Oceans rise and fall as breathing,
a rhythmic pulse beneath the sky,
where waves caress the shore
and secrets of the deep emerge.
Salt upon the air,
the call of seagulls in the distance,
encapsulates the world of wonder,
a moment captured in eternity.
