In the stillness of the night,
Beneath the glimmering stars,
The whispered tales of ancient woods,
And rivers soft as guitars.

Where the frangipani blooms,
And the golden ferns entwine,
Nature hums a lullaby,
In a world that's pure, divine.

- Robert Bob O Lockhart
