The Fall of the Leaf

The leaves drift slowly to the ground,
A multitude of gold and brown;

In autumn’s dance, they whirl around,
And settle softly, soft as down.

I ponder with each falling leaf,
Life’s transient beauty and its grief.


- Philip Freneau
