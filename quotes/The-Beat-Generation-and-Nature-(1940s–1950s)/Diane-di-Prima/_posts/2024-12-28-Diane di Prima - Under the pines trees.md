Under the pines, trees whisper
words of the ancients,  
yearning unto earth,  
calling body away from noise,  
toward the quiet lake at sundown,  
toward heat of glowing mirrors.  
The light turns to pages,
a great wisdom overflowing.  

— Diane di Prima
