A gentle rain falls, 
On the thirsty, dry ground, 
Each drop a soft whisper, 
A life-giving sound.

The world drinks the nectar, 
A blessing from above, 
In each tiny droplet, 
Resides nature’s love.

- Liselotte Aasmäe
