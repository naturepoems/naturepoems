The river curves  
   like a dancer's body,  
   smooth, fluid,  
   carving a path  
   through the land,  
   whispering secrets  
   to the banks,  
   flowing onwards  
   to the sea,  
   carrying with it  
   stories of the world.

- AR Ammons
