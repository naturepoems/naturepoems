The Flower

A simple flower grew in the field,
With petals spun from dew,
And each sunbeam kissing it rich,
Breathed wisdom, wise and true.

O sweet snap of color bright,
In the morning’s haloed gleam,
You stand amongst all this vast life,
And hold a delicate dream.


- Samuel Taylor Coleridge
