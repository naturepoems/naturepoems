Winter

Beneath the cloak of heavy snow,
The earth lies still and deep,
In the quiet of the cold below,
Where nature’s secrets sleep.

I wander through the frozen land,
With every breath a fleeting sigh;
In winter's grasp, I understand,
The beauty found in quiet goodbye.
