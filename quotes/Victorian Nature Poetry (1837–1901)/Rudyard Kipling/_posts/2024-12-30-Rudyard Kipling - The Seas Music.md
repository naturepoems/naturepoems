The azure seas that starkly rise,  
With waves of foam like tired sighs,  
Are the music of eternity,  
A tune to charm the skies.  
 
As gulls wheel and dance upon the blue,  
And brushes softly kissed in dew,  
The rhythm of the Earth beats strong,  
And sings a song so true.

- Rudyard Kipling
