Nightfall
As night descends,
A blanket of stars unfolds,
In the hush of the dark,
Nature breathes, gentle, bold.

The moon, a watchful eye,
Guides the wanderers’ way,
In the realm of shadows,
Life continues to sway.


- Léopold Sédar Senghor
