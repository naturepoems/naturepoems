A silver mist upon the pond,
Reflecting morning's light,
Where lilies bloom and waters bond,
In tranquil, pure delight.

The herons stand like sentinels,
In silence, they await,
Nature weaves her timeless spells,
With beauty, soft and great.


- Mary S M McCulloch
