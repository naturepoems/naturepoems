The city sleeps beneath stars,
As shadows of trees stretch,
Reaching towards the heavens,
In a silent prayer for night.

The moon, a silver watchman,
Rests upon the rooftops,
Gazing tenderly,
At the beauty below.


- Giorgio de Chirico
