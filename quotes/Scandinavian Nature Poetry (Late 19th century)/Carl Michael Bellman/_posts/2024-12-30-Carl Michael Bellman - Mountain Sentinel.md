A mountain peak, proud and tall,
Above the clouds it stands,
A sentinel that watches all,
The valleys and the sands.

The winds of change blow through the trees,
Whispers of ancient lore,
In every gust, the spirit frees,
To wander evermore.


- Carl Michael Bellman
