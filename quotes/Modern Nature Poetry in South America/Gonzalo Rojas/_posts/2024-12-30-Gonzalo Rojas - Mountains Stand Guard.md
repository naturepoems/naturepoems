The mountains stand guard,
a chorus of granite and sky,
watching over the secrets
that dwell in the valleys,
a testament of time,
a brushstroke of eternity.

And I, the seeker,
inhaled their majesty,
let my heart wander,
freed by their solitude.

- Gonzalo Rojas
