In twilight’s glow, the shadows play,
As colors start to fade,
The sun dips low, in its decay,
A quiet serenade.

The stars begin to twinkle bright,
As crickets sing their song,
In nature's heart, the deepest night,
Where dreams and peace belong.


- Carl Michael Bellman
