The river flows, as eternal
   As the love which we all seek,
And its depths, rich and infernal,
   Hold the wisdom of the meek.

O! the beauty in the currents,
   The dance between the trees,
In nature, life unrepentant,
   Whispers softly in the breeze.

- Matthew Arnold
