This is my heart,
Open like a flower,
Lacing the sky,
With silken petals.

Spread wide for the sun,
Each ray a gentle touch,
Inviting the bees,
To hum sweetly above.
— Sweet summer,
Bend low with your fondness,
Let love root deep,
In the rich, warm earth.

— Amy Lowell
