Crickets chant,
   in tangled
   dimness,
   oscillating,
   winged fog,
    breaching the boughs
   with insistence.
   Oh, above,
   the fate of
   nebulous breaths,
   as stars drip,
   poison in the amber evening.
   Bees wax into darkness.

- Elsa von Freytag-Loringhoven
