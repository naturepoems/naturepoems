The Garden

So many drops
of good chance lucidity 
that made an evening
of eating upon anchor stones
for all the forces
hushed.

There beauty mixed,
and splendid shadows, pulling
through only presence,
not undulate;  
there presences
divers and preen.


- Marianne Moore
