A solitary tree stands,
a sentinel of time,
eroded bark tells stories
of seasons endured,
of storms weathered and passed.

In its branches, life flourishes,
a sanctuary for the fragile,
woven amongst leaves,
a dance of survival,
a testament to nature's resilience.

- Alejandra Zambra
