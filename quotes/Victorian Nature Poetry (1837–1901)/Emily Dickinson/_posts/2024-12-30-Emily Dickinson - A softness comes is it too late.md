A softness comes! is it too late?  
For me to hold, partake?  
And that deep green — should swim around  
The garden of the Lake?  

A snow white cloud is wrapt around  
The Earth — a glowing Pirate!  
And in that Garden Blue — I came --  
Where now the Birds are righted!  


- Emily Dickinson
