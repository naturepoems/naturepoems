But see, the stars awaken,
   In velvet skies so bright,
And the night grows ever faken,
   In the glow with silver light.

For the dreamers in the garden,
   Make their wishes with a sigh,
While the leaves, like hearts, still harden,
   To the whispering winds that fly.

- Matthew Arnold
