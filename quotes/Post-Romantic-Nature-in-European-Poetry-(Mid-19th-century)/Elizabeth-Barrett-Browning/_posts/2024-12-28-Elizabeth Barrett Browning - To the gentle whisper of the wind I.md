To the gentle whisper of the wind, I yield, 
As it plays through the branches and sways; 
Every rustle a secret, every sigh, a hymn, 
Nature beckons with her tender ways.
Oh, let my spirit rise with the birds,
In this sanctuary of grace, freed from words.


- Elizabeth Barrett Browning
