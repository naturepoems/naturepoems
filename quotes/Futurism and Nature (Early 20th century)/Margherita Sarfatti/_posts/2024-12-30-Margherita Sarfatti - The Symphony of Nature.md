In the sky, the cloud
sprinting shadows,
a great feeling of movement.
The sun-washed earth,
sings with colors,
green, ochre, gold.
Space stretches, elastic,
a tension within me,
a harmony of forms,
like a bird caught in flight,
a bright, blinding light.
