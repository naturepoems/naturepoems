Quietude

In quietude, the world retreats,
The silence speaks, a sacred space,
Where nature’s heart and spirit meets,
And time collapses into grace.

Take a moment to simply be,
To feel the pulse, the earth's embrace,
In every breath, a symphony,
In stillness, life begins to trace.


- A R D Fairburn
