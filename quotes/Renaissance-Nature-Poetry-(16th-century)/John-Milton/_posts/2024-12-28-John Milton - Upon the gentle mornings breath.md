Upon the gentle morning’s breath, 
Where blossoms dance in light's caress,
The earth awakes, shedding its death,
Wrapped in the sunshine’s warm embrace.
Oh, Nature sings, to worship I’ll bend,
In every ray, a promise without end.

- John Milton
