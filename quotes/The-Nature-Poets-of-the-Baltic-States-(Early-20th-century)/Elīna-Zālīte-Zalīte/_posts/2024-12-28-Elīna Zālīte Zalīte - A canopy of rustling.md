A canopy of rustling leaves,
Invites the day to play,
In this haven where the heart believes,
The spirit finds its way.

A symphony of life unfolds,
In whispers soft and clear,
In nature's arms, my soul beholds,
The beauty of each year.

- Elīna Zālīte Zalīte
