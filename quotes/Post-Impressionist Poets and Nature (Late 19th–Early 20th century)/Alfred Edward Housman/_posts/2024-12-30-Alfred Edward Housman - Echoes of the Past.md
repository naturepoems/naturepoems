There’s an echo of the past,
In every leaf, each moment cast,
A history rich, amid the trees,
As whispers float upon the breeze.

For as the earth turns ever on,
And memories fade into the dawn,
Nature remains, steadfast and sure,
A witness to each heart’s allure.
