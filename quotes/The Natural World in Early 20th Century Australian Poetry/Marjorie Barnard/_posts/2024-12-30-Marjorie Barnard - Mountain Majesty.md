The mountain stands with majesty,
A sentinel of ancient stone,
With valleys deep, where rivers free,
Carve paths that nature calls their own.

Above, the eagle soars on high,
With strength imbued in every wing;
Beneath, the earth and sky reply,
In symphony, the wild birds sing.

- Marjorie Barnard
