As evening paints the sky,
With strokes of fiery hue,
The stars awaken softly,
Like dreams that drift anew.

The moon, a faithful guardian,
Watches o'er the land,
And whispers to the shadows,
With a gentle, guiding hand.

- Kārlis Skalbe
