Where the blue sky
meets the horizon,
a sweet release
of shadows dances,
playful spirits
that weave in and out
like wind through leaves.

Here in the quiet,
under wide branches,
repose flourishes,
like a heart
content and unfurling,
opening softly to the sun.

- Carl Sandburg
