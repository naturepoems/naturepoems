O love, O softness of the air!
   O beauty of the scene!
Where the sunbeams meet in the verdant lair,
   And naught remains unseen.

For nature, all-enfolding,
   Lays her blessings sweet and wide,
And the heart that dares beholding,
   Finds there its truest guide.

- Matthew Arnold
