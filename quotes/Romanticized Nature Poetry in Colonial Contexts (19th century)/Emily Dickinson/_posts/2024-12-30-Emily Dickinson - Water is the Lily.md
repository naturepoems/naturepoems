Water, is the Lily—  
A Water-lily only—!  
With a Thousand ways you know—  
To measure time with Heav’n!  

Oh, I catch a glance—  
Of your bright length—  
Resounding girths that bend!  

*Water, is the Lily*

- Emily Dickinson
