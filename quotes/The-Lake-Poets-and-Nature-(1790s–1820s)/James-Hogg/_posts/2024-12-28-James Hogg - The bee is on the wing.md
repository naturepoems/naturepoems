The bee is on the wing,  
Through the glade it flits,  
In the soft sunshine singing—  
And the wild thyme slips,  
By the nest of the robin  
On the boughs swayed gently  
Amid the verdant cobby  
Of the vine that grows so free.

- James Hogg
