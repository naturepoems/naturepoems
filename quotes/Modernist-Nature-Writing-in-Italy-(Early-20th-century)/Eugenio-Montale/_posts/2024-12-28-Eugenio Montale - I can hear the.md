I can hear the sea
unclenching its fingers
on the damp sand,
a cadence of light,
sea without advantages.

The tide rises and falls,
a lizard hiding from the sun.
The only sign of life :
a shuttered window
along this hollow turn of coast.

- Eugenio Montale
