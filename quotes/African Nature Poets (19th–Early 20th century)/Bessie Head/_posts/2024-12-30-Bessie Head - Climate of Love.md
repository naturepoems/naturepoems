CLIMATE OF LOVE  
The sun embraces the earth,  
In a symphony of light,  
While rain washes the spirit clean,  
Creating space anew  
For love to bloom.  

Each season tells a story,  
Of the joy and the pain,  
Yet in every change,  
Lies the magic—  
Of life’s endless dance.  


- Bessie Head
