Behold the waves with their silver crest,
In the lift of the gusty gale,
Each crest a soul in its wildest quest,
Each swirl a tale to unveil.

The ocean sings like a thousand larks,
Her voice a sweet harmonious tune;
Hearts whisper songs when the rain embarks,
As twilight brings forth the moon.

– George William Curtis
