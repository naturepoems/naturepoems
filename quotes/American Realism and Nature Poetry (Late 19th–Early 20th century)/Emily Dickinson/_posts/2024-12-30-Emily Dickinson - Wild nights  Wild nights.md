Wild nights – Wild nights!  
Were I with thee  
Wild nights should be  
Our luxury!  

Futile – the winds –  
To a Heart in port –  
Done with the Compass –  
Done with the Chart!  

Rowing in Eden –  
Ah, the sea!  
Might I but moor –  
Tonight in Thee!  


- Emily Dickinson
