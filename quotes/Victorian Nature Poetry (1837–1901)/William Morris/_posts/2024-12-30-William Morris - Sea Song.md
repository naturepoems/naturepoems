Come unto these yellow sands,
And then take hands:
Curtsied when you have and kiss’d,
The wild waves whist, 

And brought the world as ’t is —
What else? what’s done? You told me true.
Those made the winds to play;
Drifting here, not looking for rest.


- William Morris
