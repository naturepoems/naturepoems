The brook is my constant companion,
Whispering tales as it flows,
Through valleys and rocky terrain,
It gives voice to countless woes.

The mountains rise like giants,
Guardians of secrets held tight,
In their shadows, I find my solace,
As day turns softly to night.

- Walt Whitman
