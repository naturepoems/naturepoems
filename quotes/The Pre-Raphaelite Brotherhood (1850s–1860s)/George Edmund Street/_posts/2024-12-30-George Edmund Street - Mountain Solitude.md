Where the towering mountains touch the sky,
And the clouds drift slowly by,
Thou art presence among the hills,
In the babble of the rills.

O sweet nature, how thy charms entrust
The hearts of those that roam,
Within each glade, and among each gust,
To find a spirit's home.

- George Edmund Street
