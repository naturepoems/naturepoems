In the silence of the forest,
Where the tall pines whisper low,
I walk amidst the fallen leaves,
In a world where time moves slow.

Moss carpets the ancient stones,
Streams trickle with gentle grace,
A tapestry of nature's hands,
In this, my sacred place.

- Gerhard Gran
