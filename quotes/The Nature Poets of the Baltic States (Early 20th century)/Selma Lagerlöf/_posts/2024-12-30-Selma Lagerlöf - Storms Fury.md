When the storm clouds gather,  
And thunder rolls nearby,  
I find a strange enchantment,  
In nature’s wild outcry.  

For in the clash of lightning,  
And the rain that fiercely falls,  
Lives the raw, untamed spirit,  
That nature’s crisis calls.

- Selma Lagerlöf
