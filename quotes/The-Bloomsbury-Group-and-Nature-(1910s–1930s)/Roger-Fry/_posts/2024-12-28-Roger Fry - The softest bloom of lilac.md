The softest bloom of lilac lies
In twilight's gentle, hushed reprise,
A perfume borne on zephyr's wing,
From quiet earth where nightingales sing.

Each petal holds a world anew,
In colors kissed by morning dew,
And in the dappled shade we find,
The echo of the heart’s own mind.


- Roger Fry
