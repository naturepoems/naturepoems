Autumn's Embrace
Leaves of amber, gold, and red,
Fall as whispers on the ground,
A tapestry of words unsaid,
In autumn’s embrace, we are found.

Crisp air tingles on the skin,
With every breath, a story starts,
Nature’s symphony within,
The dance of life that warms our hearts.

- Aasta Holth
