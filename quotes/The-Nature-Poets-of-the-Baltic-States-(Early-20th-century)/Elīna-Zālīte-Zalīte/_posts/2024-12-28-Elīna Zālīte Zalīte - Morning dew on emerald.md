Morning dew on emerald blades,
Sparkling gems of the dawn,
Nature's quilt of silken shades,
Awakening the fawn.

Sunlight pours like honey,
A golden, warm embrace,
In this quiet symphony,
I find my sacred place.

- Elīna Zālīte Zalīte
