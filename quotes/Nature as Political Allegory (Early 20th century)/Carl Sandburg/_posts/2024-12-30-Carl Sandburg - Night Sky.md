In the night sky,
Like a scattering of stars,
Dreams drift off,
To the memory of the blue.

The moon alone
Walks, watches,
Silent in its authority,
While we below hope,
Reach for the gentle touch,
And pour out our dreams
Into the waiting arms
Of the cosmos.

- Carl Sandburg
