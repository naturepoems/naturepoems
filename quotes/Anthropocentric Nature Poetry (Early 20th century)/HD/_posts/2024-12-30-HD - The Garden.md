The Garden

Walled in by lilies,
secluded and shy;
blooming arms stretch
between our whispering hearts.

Take me where shadows
mingle with dew;  
the bending sun spills
over glades and water;  
I am a captive
to the soft echo,
of moments past,
where time slows
as earth and sky
become one.  

- H.D.
