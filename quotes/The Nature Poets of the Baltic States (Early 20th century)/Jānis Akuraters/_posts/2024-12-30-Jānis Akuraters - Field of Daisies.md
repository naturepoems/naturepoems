Kā field of daisies dances,
Unfolds under sky’s embrace,
The whisper of the grasses,
The pulse of nature’s grace.

Above, the lark is soaring,
In the azure dome so wide,
A song of life adorning,
With the sun as its proud guide.

- Jānis Akuraters
