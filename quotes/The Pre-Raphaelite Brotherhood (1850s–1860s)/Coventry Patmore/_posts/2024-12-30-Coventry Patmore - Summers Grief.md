The summer's azure skies,
Will nothing if I grieve,
But in its fleeting freedom flies
And waits where I will cleave.

Though hold me to the love-affairs,
I shall feel lost amidst the blue,
And in the shroud of scattered hairs,
My tears will cascade, anew.

- Coventry Patmore
