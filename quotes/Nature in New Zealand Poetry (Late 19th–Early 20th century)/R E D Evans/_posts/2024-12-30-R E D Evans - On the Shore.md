On the Shore
The sea whispers secrets,
As waves kiss the shore,
A timeless rhythm,
Of birth and decay,
Seafoam crowns the rocks,
A delicate lace,
Each ebb a farewell,
Each flow a welcome,
In this eternal embrace.

- R E D Evans
