Beneath the arching skies so blue,
Where wide horizons stretch,
I feel each moment, fresh and new,
In nature’s arms, I etch.

The clouds drift by, like thoughts that flee,
In tranquil, endless grace,
Among the leaves, I sense the glee,
Of freedom’s warm embrace.

- Mary E Wilkins Freeman
