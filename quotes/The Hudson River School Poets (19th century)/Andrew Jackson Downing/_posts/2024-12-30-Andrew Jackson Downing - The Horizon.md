The Horizon
Oh, the horizon stretches wide and far,
A canvas painted by nature's hand,
Where the earth meets the sky, a distant star,
Holding the dreams of a far-off land.
In the colors of twilight, beauty resides,
As the day yields softly, and night abides.

- Andrew Jackson Downing
