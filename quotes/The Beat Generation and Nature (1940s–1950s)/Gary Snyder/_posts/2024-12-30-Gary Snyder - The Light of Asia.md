The Light of Asia
The light of Asia,
a soft caress on rolling hills,
the pot of green waves
rolls endlessly on in silence.
Buds bloom in a dance,
as hoot owls mourn—as
breezes stir the willows,
a past unseen but present.

— Gary Snyder
