The ocean waves are calling,  
  With every crash and swell,  
  They weave a tale of olden,  
  A rhythm like a spell.

  The salt-scented breeze awakens,  
  The shore an enduring line,  
  A dance of tides and moonlight,  
  A world forever fine.

- Marian McCulloch
