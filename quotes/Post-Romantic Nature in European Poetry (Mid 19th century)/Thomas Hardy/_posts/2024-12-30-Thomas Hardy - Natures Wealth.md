Amidst the hills, the rivers run,
With melodies in flow;
Where nature weaves her verdant thread,
And all of life doth glow.

So let us rejoice in every breath,
Embrace the gift we share,
For in the realms of nature's wealth,
Our souls can find repair.

- Thomas Hardy
