The ocean roars with timeless might,
Waves crashing hard on rocky shore,
In salt and spray, a wild delight,
The winds of change forevermore.

Each tide a tale of far-off lands,
A journey's depth, beneath the foam,
Nature's rhythm guides my hands,
As tides return me to my home.

- Gerhard Gran
