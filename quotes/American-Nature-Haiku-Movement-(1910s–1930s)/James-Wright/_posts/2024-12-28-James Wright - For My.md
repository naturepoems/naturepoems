For My Daughter
I write in the dark,  
All the images of your youth  
Filling me. Your laughter  
Fills the apartment like sun,  
And I wonder how  
You can be so much like  
Each whisper of grass,  
Each sigh of the moon in a  
Silent room  
Where the only silence is love.
