The Emperor of Ice-Cream

Let be be finale of seem.
The only emperor is the emperor of ice-cream.

Let the lamp affix its beam.
The only emperor is the emperor of ice-cream.

Death is the mother of beauty; hence from beauty.
The only emperor is the emperor of ice-cream.

- Wallace Stevens
