This is our hour of morn,  
The fulgent sun ascends:  
Before us, a field of corn,  
In waves of gold, extends.  

With poppies tall and bright,  
They dance, and seem to move:  
A glorious vision of light,  
In which we dare to rove.

- John Everett Millais
