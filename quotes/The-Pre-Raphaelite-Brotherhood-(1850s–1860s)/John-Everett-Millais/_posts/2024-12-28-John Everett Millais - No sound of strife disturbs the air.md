No sound of strife disturbs the air,  
All nature rests in peace;  
In harmony beyond compare,  
All struggles find release.

The meadow’s breeze a gentle sigh,  
Brushes the tips of golden grain,  
As clouds drift softly, like a sigh,  
Embracing earth in calm refrain.

- John Everett Millais
