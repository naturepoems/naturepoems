In the fields of wild flowers,
  Where the brook will wander on,
  I will sit among the hours,
  And hear my distant song.

The lark will sing above me,
  As I dream my life anew,
  And the leaves will whisper softly.
  That the skies be ever blue.

- William Hazlitt
