Protection

In every rock’s embrace,  
   The seeds of tomorrow lie,  
   Waiting for the whispering rain  
   And the playful sun in the sky.

Trap not the winds of change,  
   Let them dance through loyal trees,  
   For nature's arms are wide,  
   Breathing life through every breeze.

- James Weldon Johnson
