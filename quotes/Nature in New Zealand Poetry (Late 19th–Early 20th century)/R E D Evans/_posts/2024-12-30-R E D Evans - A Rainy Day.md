A Rainy Day
Softly falls the rain,
Creating a symphony,
On leaves and earth,
Each drop a note,
In nature’s grand concerto,
Puddles reflect the gray sky,
While flowers dance,
In the gentle embrace,
Of the soft, nourishing tears.

- R E D Evans
