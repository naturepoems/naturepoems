The autumn leaves, like memories fall,
In hues of crimson bright,
A tapestry that nature calls,
To greet the coming night.

With every breeze, the stories tell
Of seasons gone with ease,
In nature's arms, I bid farewell,
To days like fragrant breeze.


- Carl Michael Bellman
