A swan glides upon the lake,
Its neck curved, a silent grace,
Fashioned from the threads of dreams,
That mingle with the rising mist.

Moments held in quiet awe,
Where beauty finds its echo,
In the ripples that embrace,
The secret depths of nature's song.
