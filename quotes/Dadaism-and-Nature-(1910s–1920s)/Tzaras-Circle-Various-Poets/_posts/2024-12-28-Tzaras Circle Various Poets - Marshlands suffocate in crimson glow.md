Marshlands suffocate in crimson glow,  
      a sorcery of twilight hues—
   the crows, ominous,  
    weave tales from dusk to dawn.
   Life’s canvas, painted with the brush of chaos,
   floods swirl, essence receding,
     soaked in phosphorescent dreams,
   drifting along forgotten streams.
         
         - Tzaras Circle, Various Poets
