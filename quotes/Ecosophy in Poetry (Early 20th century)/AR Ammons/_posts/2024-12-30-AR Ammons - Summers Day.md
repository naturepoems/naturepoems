Summer’s Day

This hot breath
of summer,
pressing into silence.
A soft whisper
slips through leaves,
howing how
the light winks
at a distant moon.
What does it mean,
the endless glow,
or the brief brush
of a breeze on skin?
Nature unfolds,
a tapestry woven
of delight.

- AR Ammons
