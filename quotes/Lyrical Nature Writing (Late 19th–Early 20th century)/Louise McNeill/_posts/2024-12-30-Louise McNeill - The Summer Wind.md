The Summer Wind

The summer wind whispers,
   Through the trees down the lane,
   With a song that is timeless,
   Like the love of the rain.

It carries the warm scents,
   Of the earth and the bloom,
   As I walk in the magic
   Of the garden's soft room.


- Louise McNeill
