The River
Oh, sweet river,
Your waters whisper legends,
Flowing through valleys,
Beneath the silent stars,

Your banks, a cradle for dreams,
Lingering echoes of laughter,
You carry the world,
In your gentle flow.


- Léopold Sédar Senghor
