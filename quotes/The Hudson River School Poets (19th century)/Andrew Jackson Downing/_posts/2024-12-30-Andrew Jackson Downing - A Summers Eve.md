A Summer's Eve
As the sun dips low, the horizon's glow,
Colors of crimson and gold unwind,
A summer's eve wraps the world in slow,
Her beauty leaving the heart intertwined.
The soft evening breeze kisses the land,
And whispers sweet secrets, only stars understand.

- Andrew Jackson Downing
