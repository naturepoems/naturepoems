The sunset paints the world in gold,
A canvas vast and bright,
Each hue a brush of destiny,
In the fading light.

As day surrenders to the night,
And stars begin to gleam,
We find ourselves in nature's grasp,
And live the sweetest dream.


- Hermann Hesse
