The wind carries secrets,
  rustling through leaves,
  a traveler of pasts,
  now making
  their way to tomorrow.

Beneath the vastness of the sky,
  a single flower blooms,
  a fragile ode 
to existence,
  a pulse,
  within the great heart of the world.


- Rafael Cadenas
