A spring a thousand ways to bloom,
A touch to every shivering tree,
A breath of life dispels the gloom,
With sun afire, and birds all free.

Take heart! The joys of spring will come,
And every tear is just a mist;
For the heart of nature beats like drum,
In the garden kissed by the bliss.

- A E Housman
