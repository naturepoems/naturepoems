THE BIRDS AND THE TREES  
The birds sing,  
Dancing from tree to tree.  
In their song,  
The world is born again,  
In the arms of a soft breeze.  

The trees sway,  
A slow rhythm,  
Cradling nests,  
While the sun smiles down  
And the earth breathes in rhythm.  


- Bessie Head
