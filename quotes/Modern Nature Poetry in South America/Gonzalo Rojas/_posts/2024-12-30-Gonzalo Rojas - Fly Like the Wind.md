Fly like the wind,
like twilight,
down the mountains,
from the peak to the river,
where the roots breathe,
all the stories of the earth,
once a myth, once a sound.

And from the tall grass,
the river runs,
at the sun’s embrace,
carrying the dreams
of tomorrow’s rain.

- Gonzalo Rojas
