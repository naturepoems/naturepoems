The willow weeps, the vine does twine,
As sunlight kisses the past,  
The earth with blessings doth entwine,  
A thousand voices, alas!
Through nature’s streams and soft sways,
Where wildflowers sing, and beauty lays.

- Mary Wollstonecraft
