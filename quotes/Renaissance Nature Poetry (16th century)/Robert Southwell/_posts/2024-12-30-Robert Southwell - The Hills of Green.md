Lo! The hills are clad in green,
Nature's tapestry is seen;
Rivers dance on silver wings,
Glistening pearls in sunlight sings.
The mountains rise, steadfast and true,
Against the blush of evening hue;
Oh, heart of Earth! My refuge be,
In every scene, I find the key.

- Robert Southwell
