The Hour of Grace
The sun is shining brightly,
The shadows softly play,
And all the blooms are dancing
In the bright, warm day.

Like children in the garden,
With laughter and with glee,
The flowers are rejoicing
In their perfect harmony.

- Dorothea Mackellar
