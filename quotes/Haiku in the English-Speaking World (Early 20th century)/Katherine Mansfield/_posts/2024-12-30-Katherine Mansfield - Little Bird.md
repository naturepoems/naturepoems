In the dark
A little bird
Sings on the bough.

I ask, ‘Where are you?’
‘Out in the fields, 'tis fine,
In the clover,”

‘Oh! and flowers they bloom? ’
‘And glimmer through the dew.’
—Running, flying, basking,
Winds whisper songs anew.  
—Katherine Mansfield
