There is a joy in the woods,
   A joy that the heart can restore;
There is a hope in the mountains,
   An echo unheard before.

The great world is full of shadows,
   Of murmurs and voices sweet,
But the trees have a deeper secret,
   And the wise heart is still at their feet.

- Matthew Arnold
