Upon the hills, the heather blooms,
A sea of purple, wild and free,
The air is rich with earth's perfume,
A song of life's simplicity.

Beneath the vast and watchful sky,
The larks ascend in joyful flight,
In this embrace of earth and sky,
I find my spirit's true delight.

- Gerhard Gran
