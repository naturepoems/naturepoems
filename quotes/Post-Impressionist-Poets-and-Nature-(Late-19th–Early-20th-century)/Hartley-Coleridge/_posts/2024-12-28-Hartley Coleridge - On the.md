On the Lake
The lake is calm as a crystal sea,
Reflecting the azure sky;
The willows weep with a melancholy,
As the soft breezes sigh.
O, who can resist such a tranquil scene,
As the world fades away from gaze;
In this paradise, serene and green,
Days pass with soothing grace.

- Hartley Coleridge
