Moonlit Night

The night stretches quietly,
the moon a lantern.
Trees reach,
their shadowed fingers
tracing lines in twilight.
Crickets play
as soft as sighs,
each note
punctuating the stillness.
This is beauty,
found in dark,
a pulse of serenity.

- AR Ammons
