In every grove where blossoms grow,
I wander hand in hand,
With every breeze that lifts the bow,
To find our hearts' command.

Oh Nature's love, it warms the air,
With every fragrant breath,
A bond more true than any prayer,
To conquer life and death.

- Lady Mary Wroth
