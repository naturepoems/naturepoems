At dusk, the fireflies ignite the night,  
Like wandering stars in a fleeting flight;  
They twinkle and whisper their secret charms,  
Inviting the soul with soft, tender arms.  
In their glow, I find the light  
Of dreams weaved gently in the night.

- John Burroughs
