Stay, you birds of summer!  
    The wind is soft and slow,
Carry from the hills  
    a hymn, a tune, a sound.
Let the echoes fill the valleys,  
    along the stream,
As we wake the hiding thoughts  
    of yesterday's dreams.

- Carl Sandburg
