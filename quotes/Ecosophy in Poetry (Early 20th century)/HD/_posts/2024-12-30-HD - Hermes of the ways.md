Hermes of the ways

   The rosegold
   in the chord
   draws back
   from the sky
   & on the leaves,
   heavy drops of rain; 

   in the stone
   a dark flat jewel
   drops down,
   into the green silence,
   while wise birds, scattered
   drop here & there,
   on the reeds.


- HD
