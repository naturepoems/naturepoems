Dejection: An Ode

Awake, my soul! For thou hast slept;
And all things round thee grieve!
Then eye the evening sun,
The ocean-spice of our soul!

Nor read thy life like a weary night,
That never changes its ways.
But let them ''come; let them arise!
Shine far, deep in the dark.
