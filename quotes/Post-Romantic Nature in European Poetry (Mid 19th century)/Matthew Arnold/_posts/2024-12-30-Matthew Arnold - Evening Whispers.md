In the stillness of the evening,
   When the sun sets in gold,
There lies a languid feeling,
   In the heart’s deep fold.

For the world is hushed in dreaming,
   And the stars begin to swell,
In the quiet, chance redeeming,
   In the nature’s mystic shell.

- Matthew Arnold
