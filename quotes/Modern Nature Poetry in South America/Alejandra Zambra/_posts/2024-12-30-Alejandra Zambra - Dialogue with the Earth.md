In the stillness,
even silence speaks,
a soft murmur of nature,
a heartbeat echoing along the paths.

Each step taken,
a dialogue with the earth,
a rhythmic connection,
knowing we are part of this,
a delicate, wild home.

- Alejandra Zambra
