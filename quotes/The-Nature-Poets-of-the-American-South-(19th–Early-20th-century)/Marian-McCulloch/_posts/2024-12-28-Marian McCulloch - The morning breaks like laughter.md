The morning breaks like laughter,  
  Across the fields so green,  
  With hues so bright and joyful,  
  It’s Nature’s canvas seen.

  The flowers bloom in splendor,  
  While birds begin to sing,  
  A symphony of color,  
  A chorus of the spring.

- Marian McCulloch
