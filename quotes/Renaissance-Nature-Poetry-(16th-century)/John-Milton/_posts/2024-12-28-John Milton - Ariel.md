Ariel, 
your gentle winds of morn, 
Rippling through the leaves, shatter the dawn.
Awake, fair Nature, awake! Fly up to the bright, 
With golden cheecks, the day's unfolding flight.
These waters serene, still glassing the sky,
Reflect the peace that walks where eagles fly.

- John Milton
