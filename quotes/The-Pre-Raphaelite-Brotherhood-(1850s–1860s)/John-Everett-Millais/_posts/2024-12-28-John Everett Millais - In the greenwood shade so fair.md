In the greenwood shade so fair,  
With the trellised vine around,  
We wander, and light, as air,  
The wild birds’ song resound.  

Here blooms the fragrant thyme,  
High banks of flowers we cherish,  
What rapturous bliss sublime,  
When all things seem to perish!

- John Everett Millais
