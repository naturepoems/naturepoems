Snowbound

  In the long, silvery twilight
  Of the winter day,
  Looking out on the snow clad fields,
  The world seems far away.

  The silent, white enchantment
  Wraps the earth in a pall,
  Where dreams are woven, slowly,
  In its hallowed thrall.


- John Greenleaf Whittier
