Storms rise over the tall cliffs, 
Riding the breath of waves;  
Where the foaming white, bright as a pearl, 
Crest and break upon the sand,    
And the air is enriched with exulted cries.  
Life's true nature, speaking from the wild.


- Dylan Thomas
