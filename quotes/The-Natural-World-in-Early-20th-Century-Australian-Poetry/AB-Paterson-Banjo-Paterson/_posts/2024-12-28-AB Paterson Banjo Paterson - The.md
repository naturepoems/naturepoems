The Bushfire
The sun is a red ball of fire,
And the wind has a cruel breath,
And the smoke from the burning forest,
Is a sign of a forest's death.

The trees are a crackling chorus,
And the leaves turn to ashes and dust,
And the embers dance in the starlight,
As they vanish to nothing but rust.


- AB Paterson Banjo Paterson
