Come hither, little children,  
To the river soft and blear;  
Where the shadows chase the sunlight,  
And the grasses gather near.  
 
Where the daisies lean and listen,  
In the wildness of their bloom;  
And the air is full of laughter,  
Filling all the woods with gloom.

- Rudyard Kipling
