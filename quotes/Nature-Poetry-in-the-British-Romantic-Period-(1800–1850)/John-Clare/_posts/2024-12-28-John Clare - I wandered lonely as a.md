I wandered lonely as a cloud,
Across the moonlit fields,
Where flowers wave and breezes loud,
Bring joy that nature yields.

With every step, the world unfolds,
A tapestry so rare,
In dappled light the heart beholds,
The beauty everywhere.


- John Clare
