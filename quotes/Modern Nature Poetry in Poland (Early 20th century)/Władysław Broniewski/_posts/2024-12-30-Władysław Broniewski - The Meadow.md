A meadow stretches wide,
Flowers bow to the soft breeze,
Nature’s colors blend,
Creating a masterpiece alive.

Busy bees hum softly,
Pollinating dreams with gold,
Life teems in every stalk,
A tapestry of being whole.

- Władysław Broniewski
