From bough to bough the soft winds sigh,
Telling tales of times long gone,
With secrets that the woods comply,
And dreams that linger on.

So let the world around us grow,
In quiet, timeless grace,
For in nature's arms we come to know,
Our rightful, cherished place.

- Thomas Hardy
