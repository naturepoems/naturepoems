The Wind’s Magic
Oh, the wind’s a magic breath,  
That brushes past the trees,  
With silken touches on my face,
It carries laughter through the leaves.

In every gust, a playful tease  
That sweeps my spirit high,  
I feel its love in whispered tunes  
As I let my cares goodbye.
