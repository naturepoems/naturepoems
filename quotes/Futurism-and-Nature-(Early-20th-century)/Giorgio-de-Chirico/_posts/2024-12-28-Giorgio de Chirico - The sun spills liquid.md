The sun spills liquid gold,
Upon the rippling stream,
Where reflections of nature,
Capture a moment eternal.

Birds trace arcs in the air,
Like brush strokes unending,
Painting the melody,
Of life in an embrace of light.


- Giorgio de Chirico
