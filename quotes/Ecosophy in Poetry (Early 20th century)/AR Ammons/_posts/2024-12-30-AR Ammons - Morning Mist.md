Morning Mist

A veil drapes
over fields,
a secret shared in whispers,
morning mist softly curling,
wrapped in silence.
The world awakens,
each moment stretches,
a lingering touch
between night and day.
Gentle breaths of dawn
bring new visions.

- AR Ammons
