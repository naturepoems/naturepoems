The stream of a thousand hills,
Clear, deep, and gently flowing;
Its banks adorned with willows,
Whence an unseen breeze is blowing.
Amid green fields and daisies,
Far on the valley's side,
The ever-charming aspect
Of Nature, deified.

- William Gilpin
