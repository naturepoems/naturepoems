In the vast expanse of night,
Beneath the gleam of stars,
Whispers of the wild speak light,
A chorus from afar.

Across the mountain's mighty height,
Where rivers carve their path,
Nature beckons with pure sight,
A treasure's aftermath.


- William H Prescott
