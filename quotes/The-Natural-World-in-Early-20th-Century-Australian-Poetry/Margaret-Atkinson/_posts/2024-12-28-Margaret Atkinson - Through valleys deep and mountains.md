Through valleys deep and mountains high,
The australian bush unfolds,
A tapestry of life and love,
In shades of green and gold.

Each call of bird, each rustle near,
A reminder of our place,
In this vast, unyielding landscape,
Where nature's heart we softly trace.

- Margaret Atkinson
