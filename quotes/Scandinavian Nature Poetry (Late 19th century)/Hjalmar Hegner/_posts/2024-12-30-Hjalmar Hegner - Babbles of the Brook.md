The gentle brook that babbles by,  
Its laughter mingles with the trees,  
A lullaby beneath the sky,  
That flows with every softened breeze.

In nature's realm, I find my peace,  
With every ripple, every sound,  
A moment's joy, a sweet release,  
In harmony with life around.

- Hjalmar Hegner
