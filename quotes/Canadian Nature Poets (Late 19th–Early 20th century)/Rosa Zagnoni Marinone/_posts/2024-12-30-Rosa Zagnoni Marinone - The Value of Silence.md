The Value of Silence

In the quiet woods, I tread,
Where the echoes softly flee;
There, beneath the mossy bed,
Lies a peace that’s pure and free.

For in every rustling leaf,
In the hush of twilight’s fall,
Nature whispers, free from grief,
A sacred truth to us all.

- Rosa Zagnoni Marinone
