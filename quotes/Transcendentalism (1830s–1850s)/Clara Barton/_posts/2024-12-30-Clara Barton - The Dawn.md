The Dawn

In the dawning light, it is bright,
Ere the day begins to sigh;
Nature, fresh in a pure delight,
Breathes beneath the azure sky.

The dew-drops gleam in the flowers,
And the birds sing sweet in the trees;
Time loses count and powers,
As day unfolds with gentle ease.

- Clara Barton
