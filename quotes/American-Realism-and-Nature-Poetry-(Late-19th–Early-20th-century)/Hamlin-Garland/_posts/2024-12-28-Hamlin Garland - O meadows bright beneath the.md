O meadows, bright beneath the air,
Where sunbeams dance and butterflies dart:
The wealth of green, the gift of the care,
In every bloom a tender start,
For in this beauty lies the heart,
Of nature's lore and gentle grace;
In quiet moments, calms depart,
With every step, we find our place.

- Hamlin Garland
