The twining trees, with leafy crowns,  
Are attired in green festoons;  
The sweet birds sing, their tuneful sounds  
Resound in playful afternoons.

Each flower a star, each blade a gem,  
In God's creation's lovely realm,  
I ponder silently within,  
Where nature's secrets overwhelm.

--- "In the Garden"

- Henry Vaughan
