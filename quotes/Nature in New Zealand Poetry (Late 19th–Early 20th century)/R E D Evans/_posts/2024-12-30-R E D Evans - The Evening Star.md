The Evening Star
Glimmering, the evening star,
A lantern in the dusky sky,
Echoing stories of the day,
Binding earth and sky in grace,
The world slows to listen,
To the twilight’s hymn,
As shadows stretch and fold,
And the heavens whisper peace.

- R E D Evans
