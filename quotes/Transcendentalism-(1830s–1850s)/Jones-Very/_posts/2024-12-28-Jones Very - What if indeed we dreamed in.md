What if, indeed, we dreamed in lines,
  That Love’s soft hand could blend
  With Nature’s hues, a sweet design
  And peace must be our end.
    
Then can I hope before I die,
  For such a peace as this
  To bloom upon my last goodbye,
  So sweet, it must be bliss.

- Jones Very
