In the Waiting Room

In Worcester, Massachusetts,
it is February, and
I am waiting to hear my name.
I read a few pages of a magazine
and wait, and then read
a few lines of the dictionary,
which makes me feel
like the whole world tells me,
“Do not let me sleep.”

- Elizabeth Bishop

- Elizabeth Bishop
