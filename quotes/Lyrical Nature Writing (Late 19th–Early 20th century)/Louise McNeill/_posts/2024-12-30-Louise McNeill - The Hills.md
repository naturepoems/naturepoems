The Hills

The hills are alive,
   With the scent of the thyme,
   And the songs of the crickets,
   Like notes in a rhyme.

They hold all the seasons,
   The sun and the rain,
   In the cradle of valleys,
   Where all things remain.


- Louise McNeill
