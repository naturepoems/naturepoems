The air is thick with winter
and the horizon fills with clouds.
A scream of pine trees
stiffs the scent of salt,
wrapping it in resin.

Mad winds rise,
and the earth is laden,
out of breath and sighing,
ready to explode into spring.

- Eugenio Montale
