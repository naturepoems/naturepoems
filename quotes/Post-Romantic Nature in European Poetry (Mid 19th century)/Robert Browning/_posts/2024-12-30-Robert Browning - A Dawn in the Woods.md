‘Tis a matter of delight!  
         To spy where birds in nests of a night,  
         Lay soft as moonlight on powdered oak,  
         Their smiles eternal, as sunlight broke,  
         And echo carried, betwixt the trees—  
         When fear the skeins of dusk release!

- Robert Browning
