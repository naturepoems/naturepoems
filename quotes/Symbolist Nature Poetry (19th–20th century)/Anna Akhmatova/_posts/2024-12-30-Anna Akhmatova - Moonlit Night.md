Moonlit Night

Underneath the silver moon,
The world transforms, soft and bright,
The night unfolds its velvet cloak,
Embracing all within its light.

The trees stand tall, a silhouette,
Against the sky, a canvas wide,
In this serenity, I find,
A harmony where dreams abide.


- Anna Akhmatova
