Autumn Leaves
The leaves, they flutter down,
Golden notes in a symphony,
Each one a whisper of change,
Dancing to the ground —
A tapestry of seasons,
Wraps the world in warmth,
As nature gently turns her page,
To a softer hue of rest.

- R E D Evans
