The trees have entered my hands,  
As the sap enters the trees;  
You may see my fingers  
Are branches of the yew,  
And my heart a trunk,  
And the sap is of the plum.  
Let us drink,  
For we are of the earth,  
The earth,  
And the trees.  
-- Amy Lowell

- Amy Lowell
