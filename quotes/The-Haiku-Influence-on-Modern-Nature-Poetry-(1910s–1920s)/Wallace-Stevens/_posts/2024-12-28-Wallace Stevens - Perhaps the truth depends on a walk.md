Perhaps the truth depends on a walk around the lake.
No! the world must be in our minds;  
In the center of our gaze,  
Those trees must wait in the steady dew  
Among their petals,  
Bending to the gentle curve  
Of sunlight, as if they hear  
The solemn innocence we create.

- Wallace Stevens
