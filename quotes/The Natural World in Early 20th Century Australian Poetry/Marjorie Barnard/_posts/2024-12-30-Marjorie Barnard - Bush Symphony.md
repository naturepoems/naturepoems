Amidst the gum trees tall and grand,
The bushlight dances, wild and free,
Where nature weaves with tender hand,
A tapestry of life to see.

The kookaburra's laughter rings,
Melodic in the summer air,
While blossoms bloom and nature sings,
A harmony both pure and rare.

- Marjorie Barnard
