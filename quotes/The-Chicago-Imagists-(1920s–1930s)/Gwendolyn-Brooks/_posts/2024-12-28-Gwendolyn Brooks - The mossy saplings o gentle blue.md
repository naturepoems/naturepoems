The mossy saplings o’ gentle blue,  
A candle’s dancing flames, 
And, with no tears, 
They rise above the briny pools,  
The infinite, rising stars.  
They fade, fade, fade,  
Where the poets came from in a world of light.

- Gwendolyn Brooks
