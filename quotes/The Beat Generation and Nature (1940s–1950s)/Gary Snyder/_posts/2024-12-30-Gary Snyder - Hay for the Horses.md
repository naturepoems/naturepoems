Hay for the Horses
Hay for the horses,
the barn door swings,
quiet, the sun folds
the clouds away;
the day stretches,
to fill the valley,
down in the fields,
where life stirs beneath the grass,
awakening:
old tired roots
clasp the earth and dream.

— Gary Snyder
