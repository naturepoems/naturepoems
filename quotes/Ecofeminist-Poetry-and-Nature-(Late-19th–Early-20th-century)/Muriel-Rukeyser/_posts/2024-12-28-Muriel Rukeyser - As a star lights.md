As a star lights   
a new galaxy,   
that fading echo,   
and I am in it -  
a tree bending under   
the weight of its own blooms.

Time passes —  
time thickens like honey,  
like the sweetness of spring   
when the flowers stand still   
in silence, only   
the wind knows.

- Muriel Rukeyser
