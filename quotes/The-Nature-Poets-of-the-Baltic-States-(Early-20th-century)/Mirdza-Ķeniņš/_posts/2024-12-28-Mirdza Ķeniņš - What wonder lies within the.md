What wonder lies within the dawn,
When morning breaks in hues of gold,
The sleepy world, from night withdrawn,
Awakens tales waiting to be told.

Birds burst forth in joyful flight,
As blossoms stretch their petals wide,
In every corner, pure delight,
Where harmony and peace abide.


- Mirdza Ķeniņš
