Easter Wings
Lord, who createdst man in wealth and state,
Thou didst withhold thy leave to go astray;  
And still thou shew'st thy power in hearts that pray.
The heavens, earth, wide as the waves' own jate
Thou mad'st alone light, darkness took a weight.


- George Herbert
