A brook sings sweetly as it flows,
Over pebbles, smooth and round,
Where the humble wildflower grows,
In the cradle of earth's sound.

I pause to listen, hearts align,
In the hush, where dreams take wing,
Every ripple bears a sign,
That whispers of the joy of spring.

- Jean Blewett
