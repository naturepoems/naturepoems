The earth is full of thy creatures;
Though the soul keeps its stillness,
And the air is full of the break of day,
Our sounds are hushed, but our eyes are light,
In the morning, The world was made anew,
At the frame of the dawn, we may not view.

- Elizabeth Barrett Browning
