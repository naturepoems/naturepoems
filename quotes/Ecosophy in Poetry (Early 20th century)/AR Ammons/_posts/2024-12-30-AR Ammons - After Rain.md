After Rain

The earth breathes,
a pause; then,
a freshened spirit,
a washed charm,
pure scent
rises from the ground,
mingling with clouds,
turning the day
a shade brighter, deeper.
A dance of droplets
disappears,
and the green
sings again.

- AR Ammons
