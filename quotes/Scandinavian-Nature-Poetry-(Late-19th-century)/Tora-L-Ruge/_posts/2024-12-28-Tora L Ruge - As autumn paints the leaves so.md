As autumn paints the leaves so bright,
In hues of amber, gold, and light,
The chill of air, a crisp embrace,
Nature dons her finest lace.

Underneath the oaks, with roots so deep,
The world around begins to sleep,
Yet beauty lingers in the fade,
A fleeting dance that love conveyed.


- Tora L Ruge
