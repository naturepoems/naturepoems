Nature’s Chorus
Hear the chorus of the leaves,
A symphony of rustling sound,
Echoes dance as daylight weaves,
In nature's heart, we are unbound.

Each creature sings a part in tune,
Together in this grand design,
Underneath the watchful moon,
Life’s melodies forever shine.

- Aasta Holth
