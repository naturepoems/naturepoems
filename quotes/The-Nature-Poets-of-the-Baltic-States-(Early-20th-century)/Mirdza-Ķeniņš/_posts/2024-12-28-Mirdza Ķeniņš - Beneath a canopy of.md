Beneath a canopy of leaves,
The forest breathes, alive and whole,
In every rustle, the heart believes,
Nature's rhythm, a sacred roll.

Mossy stones and whispering streams,
Echoes of ancient paths they share,
In sunlight's glow, we find our dreams,
As earth and sky dissolve in air.


- Mirdza Ķeniņš
