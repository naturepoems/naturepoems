The Mountain

Lofty peaks that kiss the sky,
Stand as guardians of the land;
Where eagles soar and spirits fly,
Nature’s throne on which I stand.

The air is pure, the view divine,
In silent majesty, they rise;
With every step, my heart aligns,
In harmony with the skies.

- Clara Barton
