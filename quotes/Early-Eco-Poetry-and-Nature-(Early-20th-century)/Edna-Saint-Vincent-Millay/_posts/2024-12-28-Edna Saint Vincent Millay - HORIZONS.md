HORIZONS

Into the fog goes the sun,
The ocean rolls out to sea.
What hunger I felt for the light,
The salt air tugs at me. 
I sing out loud to attract it,
The twinkling stars that I have tracked,
The vast unknown stretches onward,
But this flame I cannot crack. 


- Edna Saint Vincent Millay
