To the Moon

O thou silver deity of the night!
Whose throne is in the clouds, and whose soft light
Is shed on the wide world, and on the sigh
Of the sad lover, and the wandering eye.
O come, thou Moon, with thy pale beams, and throw
Thy silvery light upon the sylvan flow.

— Charlotte Smith

- Charlotte Smith
