The First Snow
A soft hush descends,
As snow blankets the earth,
Transforming it, anew —
Each flake a delicate lace,
Crowning the branches,
In a frosty embrace,
A silence profound,
Where dreams drift in white,
And the world waits in wonder.

- R E D Evans
