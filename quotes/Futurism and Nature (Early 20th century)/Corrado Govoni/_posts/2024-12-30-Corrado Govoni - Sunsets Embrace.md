Sunset spills its colors
on the horizon's edge,
a fiery embrace,
the day falls gently
into the arms of night.
Stars blink awake,
like shy lovers,
glistening softly
down on the world,
while the cool breeze
gathers memories,
lulling them
to sleep in shadows.

- Corrado Govoni
