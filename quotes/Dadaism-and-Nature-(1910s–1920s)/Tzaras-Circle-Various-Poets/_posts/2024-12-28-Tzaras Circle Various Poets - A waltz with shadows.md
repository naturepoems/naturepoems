A waltz with shadows,  
    in the heart of night,  
         every star a witness,
    to the silence  
         of gravel paths.
   The forest bears witness  
       to an unseen dance,
   blossoms flicker as soft sighs,
        in harmony with the moon’s  
   celestial scrutiny.
          
          - Tzaras Circle, Various Poets
