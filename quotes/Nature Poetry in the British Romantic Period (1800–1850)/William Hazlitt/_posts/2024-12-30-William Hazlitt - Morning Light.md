In the morning when the light,
Skyward leaps with liquid art,
And the shadows fade from sight,
Nature fills my yearning heart.

Through the blooming flower its race,
Behold the beauty of the day,
In Nature’s generous embrace,
Long, like a child, I wish to stay.


- William Hazlitt
