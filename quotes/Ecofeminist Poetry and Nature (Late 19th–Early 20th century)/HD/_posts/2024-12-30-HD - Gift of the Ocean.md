This is the gift of the ocean:  
The soft crash of waves,  
The sea foam  
Kissing my feet,  
And the smell of salt in the air—  
Here,  
With the gulls that swirl above,  
My heart is heavy yet light,  
With fear yet with joy,  
As I watch the sun sink down  
Into the deep.

- HD
