At Dawn
With the dawn’s first light,
The waters stir,
Ripples dance like laughter,
Through silvered mists —
A symphony of awakening,
Where night kisses day.
Nature’s breath, so gentle,
Awakens the wren,
Each note a soft caress
Against the stillness of time.

- R E D Evans
