With every leaf that falls to ground,
A story shared, a journey told,
Of seasons passed, of time unbound,
Of nature’s heart in hues of gold.

In autumn’s grasp, the world does change,
Yet beauty lingers in decay;
For even in the wild and strange,
Life cycles on, come what may.

- Marjorie Barnard
