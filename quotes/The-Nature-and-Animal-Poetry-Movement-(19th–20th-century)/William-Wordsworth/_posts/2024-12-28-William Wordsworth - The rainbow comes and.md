The rainbow comes and goes,
And lovely is the rose;
The moon doth with delight
Look round her when the night
Is bare, and when she glows
In heaven's delicate night;  
Then will I sing, to thine own praise!
To you, O fragrant dew,
I’ll lift this grateful gaze.

- William Wordsworth
