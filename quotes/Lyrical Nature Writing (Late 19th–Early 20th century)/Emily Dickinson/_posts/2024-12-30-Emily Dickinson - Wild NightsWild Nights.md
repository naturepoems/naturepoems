Wild Nights—Wild Nights!  
Were I with Thee  
Wild Nights should be  
Our luxury!  

Futile—the winds—  
To a Heart in port—  
Done with the Compass—  
Done with the Chart!  

Rowing in Eden—  
Ah, the sea!  
Might I but moor  
Tonight in Thee!  


- Emily Dickinson
