Morning breaks with whispers low,  
Dew-kissed grass felt beneath,  
A symphony of life aglow,  
Nature’s joy, a kindred wreath.

Each petal stretched, each leaf relates,  
In silent stories and woven dreams,  
The sun’s ascent, a dance it creates,  
In nature’s arms, time gleams.


- Yves Tanguy
