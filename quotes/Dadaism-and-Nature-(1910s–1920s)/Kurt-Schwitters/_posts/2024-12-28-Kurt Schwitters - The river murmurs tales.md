The river murmurs tales  
of stones and journeys,  
its surface glistening,  
a shimmering silk,  
reflection of the universe,  
metaphors of time  
floating on its currents,  
while willows weep silently  
by the banks,  
hearts dipped in water.

- Kurt Schwitters
