The hills are cloaked in emerald hues,  
Where quiet rivers wind,  
And in the hush of morning mist,  
A solace here we find.  

Each breath we take, infused with life,  
The earth a sacred place,  
Where nature's heart beats steady, strong,  
In delicate embrace.

- L W Lydia H W Milligan
