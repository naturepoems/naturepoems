The caterpillar sheds its skin,
And the ground is velvet green,

O flowers! I see you bud and bloop,
While the larks with their long notes are breeding,
And pray in the fields with me,
When the resplendent beam of summer light,
Will dazzle the eyes of your Queen.

- William Wordsworth
