The mountains tower, bold and grand,
Their edges carved by nature’s hand.
With every peak, the sun will rise,
A golden crown ‘neath endless skies.

In quietude, the eagles soar,
Over valleys rich in lore;
The breath of earth, the touch of rain,
Brings life anew, time and again.

- Elin Wägner
