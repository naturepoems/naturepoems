Petals unfurl,
A canvas painted in bloom,
Spring bursts forth in joy,
Life awakens from winter's hold.

Fragrance fills the air,
A symphony of colors,
Nature's choir sings,
Of rebirth, of love, of light.

- Władysław Broniewski
