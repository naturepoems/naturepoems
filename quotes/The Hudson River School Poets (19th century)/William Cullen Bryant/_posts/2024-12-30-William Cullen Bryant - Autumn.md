The yellow sun is beaming,   
On a long and slope,   
On the darken'd hills of autumn,   
Where the trees the leaves have lost.  
Gathering clouds o'er all the sky,   
With their shadows haunts the lee,   
While the winds, wide-wandering,   
Sweep the woodland, ruffle sea.

- William Cullen Bryant
