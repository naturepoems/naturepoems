The bonfire crackles and sighs,
The moon spills silver singeing the night, 
The leaves sing soft laments in the wind.
This is the time when autumn dies, 
And like the stars, fires flicker bright. 
In gathering silence, crowns of rust
Amidst the murmuring hush of dust.

- Louise Bogan
