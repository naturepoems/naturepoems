In the heart of the forest,  
a stillness, like a prayer,  
where shadows weave stories,  
carved in the ancient bark.  

Light spills through branches,  
painting patterns on the ground,  
as nature listens,  
a keeper of time,  
a guardian of moments.

- Nadia Słobodzian
