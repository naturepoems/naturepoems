The Octopus

     With imperial grace,

Like an emperor,

   Robe lined in a velvet console,

Capturing the look of
   Fluidity in silence,

Of absorbed surroundings,

   The folding upon itself,

   The polypus in its pacing,

Trapped in a whirlpool of water.


- Marianne Moore
