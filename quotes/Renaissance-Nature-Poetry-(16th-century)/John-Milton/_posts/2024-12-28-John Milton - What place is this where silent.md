What place is this, where silent darkness moves,
And whispers soft act upon the leaves?
Where weary clouds lie low, and space appoves
Form tranquil realms, held by Nature's eaves? 
Under the tender watch of the silent trees
This scene is nature's grace - a peace that frees.

- John Milton
