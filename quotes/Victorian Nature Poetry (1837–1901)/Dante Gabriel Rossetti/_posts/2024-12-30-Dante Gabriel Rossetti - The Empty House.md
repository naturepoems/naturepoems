Within the empty house of dreams, 
Where glad strife can have no place; 
I see a wood upon the streams, 
As the trees be wide in their embrace.

Oh, may I linger long, I pray, 
With nature round me twined, 
Where blooms no empty woe or fray, 
But peace upon the mind.

- Dante Gabriel Rossetti
