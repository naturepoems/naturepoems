In quiet woods the deer do play,
And wander on the mossy ground;
Their beauty in the soft decay,
Of autumn’s glow around.
With every rustle, leaves adore,
The tranquil, breathing air;
While Nature’s peace forevermore,
Restores our weary care.

- William Gilpin
