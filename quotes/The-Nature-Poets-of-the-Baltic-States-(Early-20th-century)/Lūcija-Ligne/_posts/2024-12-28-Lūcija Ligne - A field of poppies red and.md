A field of poppies, red and bold,
Waves gently in the summer’s glow,
Each petal, a piece of sunlit gold,
In the wind’s embrace, they sway and flow.

The whispers of the earth resound,
In every bloom, a heartbeat found,
In nature's canvas, life is drawn,
A fleeting moment, dusk till dawn.


- Lūcija Ligne
