Amidst the pines, where shadows dwell,
A forest deep and wide,
The scent of earth, a rustic spell,
In harmony, abide.

The song of thrush, a gentle lark,
In nature's bounds we find,
Within the wild, ignites a spark,
Of peace and love entwined.


- Mary S M McCulloch
