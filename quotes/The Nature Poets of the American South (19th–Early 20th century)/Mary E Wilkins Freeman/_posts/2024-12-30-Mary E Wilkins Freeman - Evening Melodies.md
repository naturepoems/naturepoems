The rustling leaves, the breezes light,
Call back the past so dear,
The gentle stars shining bright,
In my heart, forever near.

The melody of evening surrounds,
With whispers soft and low,
Each note of beauty astounds,
In the twilight's gentle glow.

- Mary E Wilkins Freeman
