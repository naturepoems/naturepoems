Oh, the joy of the storm!
    When lightning glints
    across the dark canvas of sky,
    the air crackles—
    each drop of rain is a bracing drink,
    invigorating the earth’s ardor!
    Let us dance in this wild tempest.

- Filippo Tommaso Marinetti
