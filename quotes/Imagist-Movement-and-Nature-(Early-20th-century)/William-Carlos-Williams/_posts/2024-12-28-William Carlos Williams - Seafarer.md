Seafarer
A whole white
body of the sea,

as the morning
slowly cools,

under water,
white expanse
blurs the fragile waves,
with blue of gold.

Beneath this is why
I look beyond the blend 
into blue, where much white,
does nothing at all,
beyond shadows.


- William Carlos Williams
