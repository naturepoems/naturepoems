Birds

Thou lovely bird,  
In mid plumage bedight,  
My thoughts in song!  
Thy aerial flight,  
In distant woods,  
Flashes upon the bright.  

I could rest thy song,  
And yet the sweetest of refrain,  
Let it caress the day.  
Rest thee, and I shall sing.  
- Ralph Waldo Emerson

- Ralph Waldo Emerson
