And when I found you in the grove,
I praised the gods for such a fair,
With every flower that blooms, I prove
That Nature's beauty holds you there.

The sun that warms your gentle brow,
The trees that bow with graceful sway,
All whisper sweet, and somehow
Make every moment seem to stay.

- George Gordon Lord Byron
