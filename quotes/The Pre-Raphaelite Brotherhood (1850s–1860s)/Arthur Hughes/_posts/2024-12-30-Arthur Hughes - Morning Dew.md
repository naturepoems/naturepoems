Upon the hill there lies the dew,
   Where daisies softly grow,
Their petals kissed in morning blue,
   To frame the light aglow.
  
What dreams these flowers boldly weave,
   A tapestry divine,
For one who dares the heart to leave,
   And seek the verdant line.


- Arthur Hughes
