The grass is like a green sea,
a fairy sea,
wavy and shining,
a cradle of perfume
with little specks of gold;  

And I lie down on it,
my heart full of deep silence,
to rest for a little while
and let the earth hold me
in its embrace.


- Carl Sandburg
