Rushing waves crash, 
The ocean sings its mighty song,
Salted air, so fresh,
Reminds of a dream long forgotten.

Driftwood whispers tales,
Carried on the tides of time,
Each grain of sand holds,
A memory of the deep.

- Władysław Broniewski
