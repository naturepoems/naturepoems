Autumn Leaves

The leaves that in the forest fall
And lie about the trees,
Are all so lovely, rich, and bright,
They whisper in the breeze.

These gems of nature's beauty,
So tender and so rare,
Shall yield me sweetest solace,
For every quiet prayer.

- John G Whittier
