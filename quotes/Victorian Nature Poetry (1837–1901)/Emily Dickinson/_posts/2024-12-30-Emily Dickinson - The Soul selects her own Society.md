The Soul selects her own Society —  
Then — shuts the Door —  
To her divine majority —  
On one — and one — alone —  

Yet I see her everywhere;  
In trees, and leaves, and stones —  
The Sons of Earth, are close —  
But none — is close to Bone.  


- Emily Dickinson
