In the quiet of the woods,
Where the air hangs thick with dreams,
I hear the call of distant thunder,
And the rustle of leaves in streams.

Each moment, an echo of life,
Each sound, a note in the symphony,
Nature sings of ancient stories,
In every heartbeat, a memory.
