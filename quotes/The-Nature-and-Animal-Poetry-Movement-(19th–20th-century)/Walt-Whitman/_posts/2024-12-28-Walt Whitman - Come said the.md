Come, said the muse,
Let us take a journey farther,
To the fields where wildflowers bloom,
And suffer not the weight of burdens.

Breathe in the earth, the rain,
The soft touch of the sun on skin,
For nature speaks in murmurs,
In the heart of the wild, we begin.

- Walt Whitman
