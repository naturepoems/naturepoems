Amidst the shadows
of aged oaks,
where the sun’s rays play,
I find the echoes of life
and the sighs of the past.

Each leaf falling softly
is a story unspoken,
a testament to seasons,
nurtured by the earth’s tears.

- Vincenzo Cardarelli
