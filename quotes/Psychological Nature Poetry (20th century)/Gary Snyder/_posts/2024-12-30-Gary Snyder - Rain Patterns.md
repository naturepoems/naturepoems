Rain is falling
in delicate patterns,
a soft song on the roofs,
   and down the streets,
  puddles forming,
as colors misalign and swirl.
Heartbeats in the dark
  resonate with the rhythm,
of water, of life, flowing.
                  Listen.

- Gary Snyder
