The mighty mountains stand so tall,  
Guardians of the skies,  
Whispering tales of ages past,  
In their sturdy, ancient sighs.  

Their peaks touch heaven’s splendor,  
Where eagles dare to soar,  
Each rocky crag a testament  
To the earth's majestic lore.

- Selma Lagerlöf
