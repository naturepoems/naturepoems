April Rain Song

Let the rain kiss you.
Let the rain beat upon your head
With silver liquid drops.
Let the rain sing you
A lullaby.

The rain will wash your heart
And you will be born again.

Let the rain sing you
A lullaby.
Let the rain kiss you.

- Langston Hughes
