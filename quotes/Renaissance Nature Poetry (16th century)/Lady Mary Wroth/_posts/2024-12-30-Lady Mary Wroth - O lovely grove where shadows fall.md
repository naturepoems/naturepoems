O lovely grove, where shadows fall,
In stillness I embrace,
Each leaf that dances to sweet call,
Each flower's soft, warm face.

The sunbeams tease beneath the trees,
The whispering winds caress,
In Nature's deep, warm melodies,
I find my heart's true rest.

- Lady Mary Wroth
