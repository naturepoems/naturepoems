Spring Song
The world is a glad song,
With the brook’s merry laughter
And the robin’s cheer
As he flutters near
The tender blooms of dawn.

Each petal that petal unfolds,
Each ray from the sun,
Speaks with the joy of  
The joy of being young!
Rest, yet be still!
