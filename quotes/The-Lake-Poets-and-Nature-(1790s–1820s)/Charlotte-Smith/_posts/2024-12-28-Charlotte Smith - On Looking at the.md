On Looking at the Sea

Oh sea! thy deep, blue bosom wide;
Thou swellest rudely with the changing tide;
Yet havest peace within the storm’s uproar,
And whisper'st softly, as thou touch'st the shore.

Thy waves caress the sands of time,
Eternal rhythms, nature’s rhyme.

— Charlotte Smith

- Charlotte Smith
