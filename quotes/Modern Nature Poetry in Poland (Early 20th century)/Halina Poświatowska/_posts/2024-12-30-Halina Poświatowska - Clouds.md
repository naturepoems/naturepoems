Clouds above like whispers of dreams,
   float slowly through a cerulean sky.
Each one carries a tale of shadows,
   stories of rain that danced in the woods.
The earth waits, thirsting for their return,
   for the kisses of water, the stepping stones.

- Halina Poświatowska
