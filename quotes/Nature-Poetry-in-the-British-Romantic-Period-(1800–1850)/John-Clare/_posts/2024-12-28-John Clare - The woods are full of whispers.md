The woods are full of whispers sweet,
Of love and joy and peace,
Where nature's song is pure and neat,
And all the sorrows cease.

In every bud and every bloom,
Reflects a golden ray,
In nature's arms, there's no more gloom,
Just beauty day by day.


- John Clare
