Upon this green and pleasant spot,
Where rising sun brings day,
The flowers bloom, the dews are hot,
And nature's in her play.

In every breeze the fragrance flows,
The brook sings out the tune,
And in these woods, the beauty grows,
Beneath the silver moon.


- John Clare
