Dancing breeze,
Softly rustling the leaves,
Melodies of nature’s song,
Whispering to each other.

In the glowing sun,
Under a sky of azure,
The body of the earth,
Lies wrapped in lush greenery,
Pulsing to the heartbeat of spring.

- Władysław Broniewski
