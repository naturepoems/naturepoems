The light, with its coming, bursts the dark,
The day awakens dawn soft, breaking,
Glancing the dew on the grass, in the dark,
Nature sings awoken, all well,                     
And life goes on through fields of morning,  
Rested under the sure, still, warming sun.

- Dylan Thomas
