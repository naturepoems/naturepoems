O to be alone with the wild,
The woods that seem to whisper,
The streams that flow with life,
In every leaf, a tale to tell.

The ocean calls with crashing waves,
And in silence, I find my strength,
The beauty of the earth surrounds,
In its embrace, I lose myself.

- Walt Whitman
