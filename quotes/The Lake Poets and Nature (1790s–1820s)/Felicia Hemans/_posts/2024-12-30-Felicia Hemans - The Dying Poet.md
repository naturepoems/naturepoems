The Dying Poet
Lo! in this tranquil hour,  
Before the final call,  
I feel a sacred power,  
In Nature's mighty thrall.

Each star that sparkles right  
Amid the velvet night,  
Will bear my hopes away,  
To realms of gold and day.

- Felicia Hemans
