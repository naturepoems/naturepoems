Fruits of labor blossom,
ripe dreams laced in color,
this life's bouquet,
wild roses and petals unbound,
from earth to echo,
from echo to skin,
all our struggles,
echoing back,
a conversation of beings,
cradled in nature's hands.

- Vicente Aleixandre
