Stanza

As I reflect, in this deep solitude,
Where trees and rivers hold my thoughts subdued;
Beneath the shelter of each leafy bough,
I muse on nature, and I ponder how.

The sounds of life, and love’s sweet melodies,
Are echoed softly in the whispering trees.

— Charlotte Smith

- Charlotte Smith
