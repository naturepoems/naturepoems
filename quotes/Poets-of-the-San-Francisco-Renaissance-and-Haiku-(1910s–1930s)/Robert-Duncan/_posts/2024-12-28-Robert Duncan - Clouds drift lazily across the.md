Clouds drift lazily across the horizon,
Painting the vast canvas of blue with shadows,
And as daylight wanes to twilight hues,
The symphony of crickets fills the evening air.
Each note a reminder of the cycle of life,
In this quiet serenade of dusk and dawn.

- Robert Duncan
