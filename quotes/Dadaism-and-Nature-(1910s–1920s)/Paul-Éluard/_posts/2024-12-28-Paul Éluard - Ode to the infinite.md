Ode to the infinite garden,
Where creation bursts forth unhindered,
We, the wild sculptors of our fate,
Digging, laughing, planting dreams,
Embracing transience,
A cascade of colors
Whispering through time,
Nature's love reflected in our hearts.

- Paul Éluard
