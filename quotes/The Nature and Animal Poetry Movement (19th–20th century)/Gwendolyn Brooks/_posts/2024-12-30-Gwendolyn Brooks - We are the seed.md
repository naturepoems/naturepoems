We are the seed

We are the seed of a new tomorrow,
Quietly bursting forth,
Glistening with morning’s dew,
Kissed by the warmth of the sun.

We flourish as we flow,
A forest in hearts touched,
Where the golden fleece binds us,
A wholeness pure, dispersed.


- Gwendolyn Brooks
