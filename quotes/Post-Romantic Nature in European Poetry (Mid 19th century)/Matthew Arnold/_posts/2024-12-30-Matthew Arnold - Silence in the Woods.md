In silent woods where shadows creep,
   And the stars begin to show,
There is comfort in the deep,
   Where the quiet waters flow.

For the night, with all its dreaming,
   Wraps the earth in her embrace,
And the heart, in stillness gleaming,
   Finds its quiet resting place.

- Matthew Arnold
