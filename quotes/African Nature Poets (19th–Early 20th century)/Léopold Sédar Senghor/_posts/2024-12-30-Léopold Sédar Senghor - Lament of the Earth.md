Lament of the Earth
Earth, my mother,
Your valleys echo silent cries,
In the plight of your children,
Your beauty slowly dies.

In your arms, we gather,
Your gifts, we must protect,
For in preserving you,
Our world finds its respect.


- Léopold Sédar Senghor
