The Skylark

O Skylark, thou dost soar and sing,  
  From meadows where the sun is bright,  
Our poems dance on quickly’d wing  
  And turn to winter’s joy of night.  
Hark to thy call, with life so true,  
  In every height that meets the blue.

- George Meredith
