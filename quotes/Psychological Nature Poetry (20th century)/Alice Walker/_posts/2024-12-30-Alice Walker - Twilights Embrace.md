Each evening
brings a painting to life,
where shadows deepen and colors sing.
The gentle touch of twilight
melts into night,
as stars blink awake,
having wandered through gasps of silence.

To see,
to feel,
this world breathing,
reminds us
of all we hold dear.


- Alice Walker
