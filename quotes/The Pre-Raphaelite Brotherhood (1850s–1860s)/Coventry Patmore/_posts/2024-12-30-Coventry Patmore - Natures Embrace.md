With ferns and grasses meant to speak—
Of soft meandering paths,
And every bloom so regal, meek,
In friendship sweet as baths.

With twisted roots upon each wall,
Shall guide our hearts with grace,
No longer blind shall have their fall,
In light’s inspired embrace.

- Coventry Patmore
