In wildflower fields, the colors blend,  
A tapestry of grace,  
The buzzing bees and laughter's sound,  
Fill the gentle space.  

Each petal kissed by sunshine’s glow,  
A promise of what’s new,  
Nature’s love in vibrant bloom,  
An ode to life’s debut.

- L W Lydia H W Milligan
