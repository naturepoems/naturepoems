Clouds at Play
Above us sailing, clouds at play,
Drifting softly, day by day,
Their forms are castles, ships of flight,
An ever-changing, wondrous sight.
In azure skies, they dance and twirl,
A tapestry that nature unfurl;
In their shadows, the world beneath,
Finds calm and peace—a sweet belief.

— Adeline Dutton Train Whitney

- Adeline Dutton Train Whitney
