The Locust Tree

I have heard

locust trees

sway / 
impressed

as the sun

playing in the

shadows of

the leaves

against the air.

Invisible, I follow

like a river flow.

O may I love you

always or come

down from the sky

between your branches?

- William Carlos Williams
