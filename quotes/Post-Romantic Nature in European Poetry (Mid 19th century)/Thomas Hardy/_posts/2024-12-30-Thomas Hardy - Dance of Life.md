Upon the breezes carrying ease,
The whispers drift and sway,
Where laughter bubbles, like honey bees,
In fields of wild decay.

The dance of life, so wild and free,
In nature's clasp, so warm,
Brings forth the joy that we may see,
As hearts are safe from harm.

- Thomas Hardy
