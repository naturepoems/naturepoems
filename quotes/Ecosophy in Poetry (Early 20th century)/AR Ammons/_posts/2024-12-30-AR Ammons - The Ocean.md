The Ocean

Waves crash,
a heartbeat so steady;
each curl an eternal story,
whispers of salt and foam.
A vast stretch
of blue dream,
imprints of footprints
tell of sojourns.
Wind and water,
a dance of elements,
a dialogue whole in the world.

- AR Ammons
