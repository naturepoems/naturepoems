The starry night sky holds secrets untold, 
With diamonds scattered across the deep blue,
A cosmic canvas, vast and bold,
In its silence, I ponder what is true.
The moon, a guardian, watches above, 
Bringing dreams softly to those in love.


- Elizabeth Barrett Browning
