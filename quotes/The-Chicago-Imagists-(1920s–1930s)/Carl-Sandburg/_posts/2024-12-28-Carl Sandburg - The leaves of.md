The leaves of autumn,
they rustle and shout!
Come play with me, they say,
under the harvest moon.
Find the soft beds of gold,
where the shadows dance,
and the light climbs high.

Let us breathe the night air,
and sing our voices,
for we are the spirits
in the heart of the world!

- Carl Sandburg
