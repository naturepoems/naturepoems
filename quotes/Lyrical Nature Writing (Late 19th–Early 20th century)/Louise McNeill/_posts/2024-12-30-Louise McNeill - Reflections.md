Reflections

In the stillness of water,
   Where the sky finds its place,
   I glimpse at the heavens,
   In the calm velvet space.

With the ripples they quiver,
   And the colors unfold,
   Nature tells all her secrets,
   In the stories of old.


- Louise McNeill
