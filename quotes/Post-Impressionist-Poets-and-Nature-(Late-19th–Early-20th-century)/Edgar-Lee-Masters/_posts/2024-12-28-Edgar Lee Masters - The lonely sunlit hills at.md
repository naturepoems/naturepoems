The lonely, sunlit hills at noon,
Where daisies nod in silver light,
Whisper to me in silent tune,
Of worlds beyond my earthly sight.
Shadows dance on the cooling brook,
As nature's pages softly look,
To reveal the mystery within,
A sacred place where dreams begin.

- Edgar Lee Masters
