An Afternoon in the Garden

Colors burst forth,
a riot of blooms,
each petal a laugh—
red, blue, and gold
fill the spaces
of this quiet garden.

Time stretches,
a moment as vibrant
as the honeybees,
floating from flower
to flower,
a blissful memory.

Philip Whalen
