There is a great noise
of the world,
if you stop to listen.
Therefore no noise
in your house.

Your own walls
are mossy,
your own doors
are good,
your own roof
is beautiful,

but if you will listen
there is a great noise,
a noise like silk
which drives you mad.

- William Carlos Williams
