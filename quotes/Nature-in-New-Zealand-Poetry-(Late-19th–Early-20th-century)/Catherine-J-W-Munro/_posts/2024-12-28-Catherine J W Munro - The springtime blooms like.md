The springtime blooms like laughter,
A riot of bright hues,
Each petal a sweet memory,
Of hope and morning dew.

Nature's quilt in patches,
Of green and gold and blue,
Each thread a gentle whisper,
In the winds that softly blew.

- Catherine J W Munro
