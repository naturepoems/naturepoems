Upon the hills where wildflowers roam,
A tapestry of colors grown.
In every petal, whispers sown,
Of nature's gift, we are not alone.

The dance of butterflies through air,
A fleeting joy beyond compare.
In every moment, kind and rare,
Nature's touch, a gentle care.

- Sigrid Unset
