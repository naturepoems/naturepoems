To A Nightingale

O Nightingale! thy thrilling song inspires
In me a love that evermore requires;
Thou tell’st of joy, and often thrills the night,
That dost the heart with tenderness unite.

Thy music brings the forests into bloom,
And draws the joy from every silent tomb.

— Charlotte Smith

- Charlotte Smith
