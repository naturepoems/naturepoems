In a green nook, and a woodland dell,
Where the shadows of the trees do dwell,
The winds of evening, soft and still,
Bring the whispering leaves, the murmurs chill,
And the rippling brook, that sings a lay,
A melody sweet of the close of day.

— Hartley Coleridge
