A poem is a living light,
A flower blooming in the air,
Each word a petal, soft and bright,
Bringing the joy of gentle care,
An echo of the numberless selves,
The tall trees reaching to the stars,
Awake to all that nature houses,
In laughter, love, the wound and scars.

- Dylan Thomas
