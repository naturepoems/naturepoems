The clouds, like dreams, drift slowly by,  
Casting shadows ‘neath the vast, blue sky;  
They change in form, coalesce and play,  
A fleeting canvas of transient day.  
With each gentle breeze, they wander free,  
Art of the heavens, wild and carefree.

- John Burroughs
