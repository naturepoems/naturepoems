Amidst the flowers on the hill,  
Where the wild bees hum their song,  
I wander with a heart so still,  
Content in nature's throng.

The fragrant air, a gift divine,  
Draws forth all sorrow's sting;  
In harmony, our hearts entwine,  
With every passing spring.

- John Everett Millais
