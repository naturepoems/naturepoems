Eternal Return

In a forest deep,
where shadows play,
and the air is filled
with the fragrance of pine,
I wander,
seeking solace in the quiet.

The trees bow low,
as if in prayer,
reminding me of the cycles,
the return of seasons,
the whisper of roots,
in the eternal dance.


- Czesław Miłosz
