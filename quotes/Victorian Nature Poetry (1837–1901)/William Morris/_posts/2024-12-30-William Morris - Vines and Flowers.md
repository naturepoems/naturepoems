The walls are green with growing vines,
The blooms like stars at night,
In fragrant bowls, the moon will climb,
And dance upon the light.

For every flower that finds a root
In soil both warm and true
Will view the verdant in pursuit,
While dreams take wing in dew.

- William Morris
