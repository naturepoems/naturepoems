In gardens lush where laughter dwells,
Nature’s hymn softly swells;
Each petal whispers love’s design,
Drawing life from the sacred vine.
Underneath the starlit glow,
Life’s gentle rhythm ebbs and flow.
With every dawn a promise sown,
In nature's heart, we are not alone.
