In the autumn’s golden splendor,
The leaves do softly fall,
A crunch beneath my wandering feet,
A crisp, enchanting call.

The harvest moon shines gently,
On fields of amber grain,
In nature’s slow surrender,
Summer’s warmth, now quaint and plain.


- Ragnar Knutson
