I in myself have longed for thee—
I, born of fragility,
The tears of this old world have passed,
A moment in their lonesome glance in me.

Upon the river's bottom, bare,
The lost will wander, to despair.
And alas, on this cool earth bring,
Those mountains far behind me sing.

- Coventry Patmore
