Oh, the wild and woolly world,  
Is filled with leaf and hue,  
When the world drops its woolly thread,  
And stitches skies anew.  
 
The brooks rejoice in laughter,  
The winds weave through the trees;  
And nature sings her merry tune,  
In harmony with ease.

- Rudyard Kipling
