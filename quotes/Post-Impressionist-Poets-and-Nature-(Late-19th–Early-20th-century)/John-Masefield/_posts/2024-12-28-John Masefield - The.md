The Quest

The woods are silent today and sorrowful,
The shadows curl in ancient tree roots deep,
And from the wild, the whispers weave a pull,
As the leaves breathe, dreamingly while they sleep.
In the heart of all nature's beauty before,
There lie the tunes of eternity's lore.
