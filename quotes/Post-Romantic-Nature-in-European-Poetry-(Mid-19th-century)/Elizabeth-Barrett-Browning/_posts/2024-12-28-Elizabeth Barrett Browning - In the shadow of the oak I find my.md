In the shadow of the oak, I find my solace, 
Where the shadows play upon the ground,
Each leaf a story, each branch a whisper, 
Of the memories that in nature surround.
Here, time stands still, and I am at peace, 
Cradled in the arms of the great trees,


- Elizabeth Barrett Browning
