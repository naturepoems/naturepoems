The Garden  
In the expanse of the garden,  
   blooms of silent story,  
every petal a heartbeat,  
   and honeyed whispers weave  
through thickets,  
   where the sun breaks through  
a canopy of green,  
to weave a golden thread  
   in the tapestry of love.

- Louise Bogan
