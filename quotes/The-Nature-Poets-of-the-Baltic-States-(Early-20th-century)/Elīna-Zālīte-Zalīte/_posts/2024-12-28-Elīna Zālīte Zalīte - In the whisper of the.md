In the whisper of the trees,
I hear ancient tales unfold,
Leaves dance like secrets,
Stories and truths untold.

Each breeze a gentle touch,
Each rustle a lover's sigh,
Nature speaks in whispers,
As time drifts slowly by.

- Elīna Zālīte Zalīte
