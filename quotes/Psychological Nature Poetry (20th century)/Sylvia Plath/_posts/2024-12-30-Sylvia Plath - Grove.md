In the quiet grove,
         Where the brambles grow,
I lean against a tree,
               Old and wise,
Rooted deep,
And solemnly silent.
        A refuge,  
        As dreams take flight,
In the petals of the flowers,
Where colors blend,
    And life ignites.

- Sylvia Plath
