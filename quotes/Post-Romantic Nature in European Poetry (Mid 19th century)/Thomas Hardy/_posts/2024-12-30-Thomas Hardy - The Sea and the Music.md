The sun upon the seashore
Brightens the sea and sand,
And the air, the wind, the rain,
And the shining golden land.

The wild waves roll and tumble,
And thunders the storm's advance,
With the peach and the willow, love,
The court of the sea to dance.

- Thomas Hardy
