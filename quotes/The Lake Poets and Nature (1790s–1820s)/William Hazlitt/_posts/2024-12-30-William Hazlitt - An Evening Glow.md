An evening glow or dazzling day,
  O let me wander free!
  The shadows dance and dimly play,
  The light invites to me.

The flowers weave a fairy song,
  And beckon to my feet,
  To hide me where the cool winds long
  With fragrant breath repeat.

- William Hazlitt
