At night the forest holds its breath,
And silence pleads for stillness deep;
Like shades that linger just past death,
Their secrets the shadows keep.

The stars can only dream of light,
As they descend on the open fells,
To watch the pure and virginal night,
Weaving song with the fountain wells.

– George William Curtis
