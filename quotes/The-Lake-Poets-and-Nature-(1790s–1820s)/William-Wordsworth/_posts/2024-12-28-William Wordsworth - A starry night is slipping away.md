A starry night is slipping away;  
   Yet not a breath;  
   The dew still drops  
   From the gathered flowers  
   And the butterflies are sleeping.  
   A breeze so gentle and sweet,  
   Is the moon whispering  
   In the night’s arms?  
   Night is a starry way.

- William Wordsworth
