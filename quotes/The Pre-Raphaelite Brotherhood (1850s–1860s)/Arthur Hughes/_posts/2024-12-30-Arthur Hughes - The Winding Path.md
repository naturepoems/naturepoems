What joy to walk the winding path,
   Where flowers bloom and sway,
And sense the peaceful aftermath,
   Of night becoming day.
  
With every step, the earth reclaims,
   The heart, a lightened breath,
In gentle sighs, each secret names,
   The dance of life and death.


- Arthur Hughes
