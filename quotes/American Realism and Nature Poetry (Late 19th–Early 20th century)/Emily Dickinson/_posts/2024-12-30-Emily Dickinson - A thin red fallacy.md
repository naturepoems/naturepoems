A thin red fallacy  
Of Light upon the Sea  
Only to Know that you are there,  
And know that you are simple as  
The Will of the Sea –  

A Death quite other than –  
A Path of Ways –  
The veil of the sea –  
And Wind upon your should  
Just a shadow of the Moon –  


- Emily Dickinson
