In the summer's wild embrace,
Life blossoms forth in color's sway;
Each petal writes a trace,
Of passing breezes on their way.

With heavenly hues that brightly sing,
The rhythm of the earth awake.
A dance of joy, where night will bring
The dreams that only stars can make.


- Rupert Brooke
