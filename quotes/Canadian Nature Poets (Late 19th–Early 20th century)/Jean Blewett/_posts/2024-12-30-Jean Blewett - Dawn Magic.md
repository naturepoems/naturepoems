There is a magic in the dawn,
The silver mist, the soft grey haze,
As life stirs brightly, to be drawn
Into the warmth of sun's embrace.

Every creature wakes anew,
From creature small to hawk on high,
Nature's wonder, ever true,
Beneath the endless, open sky.

- Jean Blewett
