We are all dew, we are all drops in the mind of an eternal butterfly.
In the flitting of her wings, we are as tears,
A thousand tears—a million.
We are a mountain a leaf a drop of rain.
We exist—only in the mind of the Eternal.
And the Eternal is a dreamer, lost in a beautiful woman.

- Mina Loy
