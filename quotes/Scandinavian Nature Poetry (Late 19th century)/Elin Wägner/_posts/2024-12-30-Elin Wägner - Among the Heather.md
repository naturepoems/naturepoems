Among the heather, wild and bold,
Stories of the land unfold.
Each flower, a tiny flame,
In nature’s dance, no two the same.

The moors extend, a sea of green,
Where peace is found, serene, unseen;
Every step upon this earth,
Reminds us of our humble worth.

- Elin Wägner
