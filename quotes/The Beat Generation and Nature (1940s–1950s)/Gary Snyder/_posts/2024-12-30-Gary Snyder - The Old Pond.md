The Old Pond
In the deep old pond,
a frog leaps suddenly
into silence,
splashing waves back to nothing.

The world shrinks down;
the echo pulls away,
and the stillness deepens,
a breath further from joy,
a moment held by light
and darkness fully.

— Gary Snyder
