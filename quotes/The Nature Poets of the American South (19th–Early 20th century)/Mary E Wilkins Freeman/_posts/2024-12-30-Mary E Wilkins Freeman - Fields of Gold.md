Amid the fields where time stands still,
A golden grain does sway,
And in the heart, a simple thrill,
As nature holds her sway.

The chirp of crickets, sweet and clear,
The rustle of the corn,
In each soft sigh, we feel her near,
In the light of every morn.

- Mary E Wilkins Freeman
