Among the ferns and ancient trees,
A world both wild and free,
Nature's pulse, a steady beat,
In harmony, we roam with glee.

The sun dips low in the golden sky,
As the day yields to the night,
In this tranquil, woven tapestry,
The beauty of the earth ignites.

- Margaret Atkinson
