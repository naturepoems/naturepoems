Underneath the silver moon,
The meadow glows with dreams,
Nighttime’s gentle lullaby,
In whispered silver streams.

Stars peek through the curtains,
Of the darkened velvet skies,
Nature wraps around my heart,
As quiet beauty lies.

- Elīna Zālīte Zalīte
