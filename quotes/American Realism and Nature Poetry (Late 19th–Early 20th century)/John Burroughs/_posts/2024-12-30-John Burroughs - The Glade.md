In the quiet glade where shadows play,  
And sunlight dances on leaves in sway,  
I find a world, so serene, so bright,  
A sanctuary in the soft twilight.  
Here in nature, my spirit feels free,  
A bond with the earth, my heart's decree.

- John Burroughs
