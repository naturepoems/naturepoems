In twilight's gentle fold,
Where golden hues softly blend,
A tapestry of stories told,
As day concedes, night shall tend.

Silhouettes of mountains rise,
Guardians of secrets, timeless, true,
The stars emerge in velvet skies,
In whispers of a world anew.

- Richard Wagner
