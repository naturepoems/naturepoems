Joy

I have as much as I can take
Of clouds and burdensome grains,

Too low to touch.
Growing out along the sidewalks
Saturated in tomorrow’s haze;

Let me dance in the rain,
Laugh joy at the world,
And scatter dreams like petals
That whisper soft—a little bird.


- Gwendolyn Brooks
