Amidst the fields of golden grain,
The gentle breeze whispers my name,
With each soft rustle, the earth awakes,
In harmony, the spirit breaks.

The dance of colors, greens and golds,
A canvas bright, a story told,
In every stalk, a life embraced,
In nature's arms, my fears erased.


- Feliksas Vaitkus
