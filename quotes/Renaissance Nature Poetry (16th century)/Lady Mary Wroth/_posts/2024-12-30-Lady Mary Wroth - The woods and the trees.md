The woods and the trees they whisper low,
And turn the air to gold,
But thy fair face, thou art the rose,
That ever shall be told.

When Spring adorns the hills anew,
And all the flowers appear,
To kiss the ground, and dance the dew,
Thy voice then I will hear.

- Lady Mary Wroth
