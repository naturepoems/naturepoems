Mulch

Under stumps
and fallen leaves,
your body mixes
with earth, and the scent
of mulch
clings like a memory.

These nights are
full of stillness,
the wisdom of trees
in starlight.

Where are you now, my love?
Will you return among
fern and past the pond
where the dragonfly waits?

Philip Whalen

- Philip Whalen
