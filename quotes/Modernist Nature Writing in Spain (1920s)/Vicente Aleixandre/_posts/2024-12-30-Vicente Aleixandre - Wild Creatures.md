I plant a kiss 
upon wild creatures,
a soft lilac breath,
oun the grass,
upon the wind,
a continuous vibration,
a gentle illusion of life,
everywhere in spring,
intense, unfurling.
Wherever I wander,
your delicate facets linger around me; we are the world.

- Vicente Aleixandre
