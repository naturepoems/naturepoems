The River
The river winds down gently through the trees,
Kissing the shores with its liquid breath,
In the shimmering light, the world seems at ease,
Lullabied by the water's caress of death.
Spin an amber thread; make a song of the flow,
Whisper the tales that only it knows.

- Amy Lowell
