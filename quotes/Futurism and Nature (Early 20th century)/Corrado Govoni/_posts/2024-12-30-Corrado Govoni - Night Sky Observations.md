Observe the night sky,
a canvas of dreams;
a tapestry of stars,
each a distant fire,
a beacon of hope,
each one tells a story
of journeys unknown.
Beneath this vastness,
i find my purpose,
carried along
by the whispers of stardust,
a part of the universe.
