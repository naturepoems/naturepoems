In gardens where the wild things grow,
Where nature makes her peace,
I wander lost in thoughts untold,
And find my spirit’s ease.

Each flower and weed, a friend to me,
In chaos, beauty sings,
Through nature’s arms, I feel the pulse,
Of life and all its wings.


- Hermann Hesse
