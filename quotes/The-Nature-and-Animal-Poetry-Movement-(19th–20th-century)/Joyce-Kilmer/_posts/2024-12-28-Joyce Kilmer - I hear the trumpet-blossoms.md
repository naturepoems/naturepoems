I hear the trumpet-blossoms blow
A nectar of sweet July.
Then down the pathway, free and slow,
A lark shall soar and cry.

While nature sings a perfect song,
The bubbling brooks will run,
And through the night till day is long,
Each night shall meet the sun.

- Joyce Kilmer
