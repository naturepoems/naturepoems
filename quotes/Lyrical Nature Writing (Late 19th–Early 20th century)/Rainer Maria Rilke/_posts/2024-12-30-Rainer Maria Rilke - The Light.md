The Light

The light is waking now,
    and I dream of pure shades
    flowering across the branches,
    touching the laughter of the leaves.

    It speaks to my heart,
    as only soft notes do,
    when the sky is a sea,
    and the world lies open before me.

—Rainer Maria Rilke
