O sleeping flowers, awake, awake!
The earth and sky are fair,
And robed in dew of summer break,
Thy petals, shining there.

And briar and thistle, golden pale,
From purple blooming ground,
Now waken dreams that shall prevail
In melodies profound.

- William Morris
