The Landscape
Painted in hues,
Of earth and sky,
A canvas crafted slowly,
By the artist’s hand —
Mountains loom in grandeur,
Rivers carve their path,
Every stroke a memory,
Of time-flowing grace,
In the heart of nature’s gallery.

- R E D Evans
