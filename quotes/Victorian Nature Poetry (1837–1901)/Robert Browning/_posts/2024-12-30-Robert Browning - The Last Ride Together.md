The Last Ride Together  
...  
For the love and the bond  
Of the earth, of the sky:  
Yes, let us depart now;  
Find rings planted high,  
A bloom to a child  
That, sweet scented, alights  
In the softest of moods  
As night comes alight!

- Robert Browning
