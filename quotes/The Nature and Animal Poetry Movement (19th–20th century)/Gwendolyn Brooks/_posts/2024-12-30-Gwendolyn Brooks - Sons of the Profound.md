Sons of the Profound

The sons of men,
To dirt and death,
To warming wind,
Clothed in random things,
Exude a comfort life—
But this profusion
Of cherubims and stains?
It can’t dictate
What’s true in Nature’s name.


- Gwendolyn Brooks
