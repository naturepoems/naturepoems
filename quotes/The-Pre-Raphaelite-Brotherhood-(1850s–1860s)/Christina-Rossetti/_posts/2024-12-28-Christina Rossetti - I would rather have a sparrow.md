I would rather have a sparrow  
Than a goldfinch or a quail;  
A common little sparrow  
That sings a natural tale.  
She trifles with the blossoms  
That cover up the green,  
And loves to dwell at ease  
Where the sunbirds fly unseen.

- Christina Rossetti
