Evenings in the Garden
As daylight fades,
The garden breathes in twilight,
Petals blush in gentleness,
And shadows stretch long,
The fragrance of night blooms,
Wraps the world in embrace,
Homage to the serene hour,
Where peace finds a home.

- R E D Evans
