Hymn to Proserpine  
O Proserpine,  
  Queen of the flowers!  
O Proserpine,  
  Crowned with the hours!  
In the fields of nature,  
  The world is fair;  
But a wilder beauty  
  Waits for thee there.  
 
Author: Algernon Charles Swinburne  
Title: Hymn to Proserpine.

- Algernon Charles Swinburne
