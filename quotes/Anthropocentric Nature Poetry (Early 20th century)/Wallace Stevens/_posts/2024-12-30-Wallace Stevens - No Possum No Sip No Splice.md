No Possum, No Sip, No Splice

No possum, no sip, no splice,
The moon is in the sea,
The sea without any water,
The sea without anything at all.

Let me follow the moon,
And the bluish light of her shadow.
And let me find it in the weight
Of the last summer.

--- Wallace Stevens

- Wallace Stevens
