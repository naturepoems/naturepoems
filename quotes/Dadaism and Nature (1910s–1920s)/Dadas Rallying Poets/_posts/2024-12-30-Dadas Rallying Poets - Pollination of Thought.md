Pollination of Thought
Bees and blossoms share secrets,
Gossamer wings trace the air,
Petal whispers exchange their stories,
In the hush of a garden fair.

In every color lies a melody,
In every scent, a vision bright,
Nature sings of joy and sorrow,
In the sun’s golden light embrace.


- Dadas Rallying Poets
