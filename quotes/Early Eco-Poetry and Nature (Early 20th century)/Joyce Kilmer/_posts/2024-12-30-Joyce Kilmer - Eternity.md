Eternity   
O trees of life with limbs stretched high,  
I look upon your majesty;  
And in your leafy canopy,  
I find my peace; I lift my cry.  
You whisper dreams through chilly skies,  
Your gentle song, a lullaby.

- Joyce Kilmer
