When the sun is set, and evening creeps,
With her veil of peace, and softening rays,
In nature's lap, my spirit sleeps,
Embraced by gentleness, it sways,
Among the whispering, rustling leaves,
As twilight threads through golden haze.

- George Gordon Lord Byron
