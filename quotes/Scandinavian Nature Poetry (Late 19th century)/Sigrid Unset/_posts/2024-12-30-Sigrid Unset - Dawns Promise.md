The dawn arises, soft and sweet,
Casts its glow where darkness meets.
In nature's promise, life repeats,
With every heartbeat, love's heartbeat.

The flowers stretch toward the sun,
A silent prayer, their race begun.
In nature's hands, we're all as one,
Eternal dance, forever run.

- Sigrid Unset
