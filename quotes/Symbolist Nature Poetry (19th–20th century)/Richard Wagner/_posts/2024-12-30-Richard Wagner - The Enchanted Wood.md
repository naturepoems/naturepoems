A forest deep, a sanctuary,
With ancient trees that touch the skies,
Where nature hums a melody,
And every leaf a secret spies.

The rustle whispers of stories past,
In the dance of light and shade,
Each moment here forever cast,
In the fading twilight, gently laid.

- Richard Wagner
