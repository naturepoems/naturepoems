Snow
   by Louis MacNeice

The snow is falling
And the sky is thin,  
The trees are bare  
And cold is the wind.  
It is the loneliness of winter,  
An approach to the heart of things,  
When all that remains
Is the silence and the sound
Of the snow falling on the ground.
