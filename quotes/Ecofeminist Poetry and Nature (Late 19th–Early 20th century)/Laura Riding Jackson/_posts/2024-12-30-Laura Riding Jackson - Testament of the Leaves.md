From the heights of whispering pine,
I cast my voice with the lark,
To touch the crimson of dawn,
As golden rays pierce the dark.

Every leaf a testament,
A story from the trees,
Their wisdom spreads like wildfires,
In the gentle summer breeze.

- Laura Riding Jackson
