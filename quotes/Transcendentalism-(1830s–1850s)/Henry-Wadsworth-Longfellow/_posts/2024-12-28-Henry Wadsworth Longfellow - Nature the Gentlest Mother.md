Nature, the Gentlest Mother,  
Impatient of no child,  
The feeblest of her creatures  
Does not believe she is wild.  
  
And with the sun and with the sky,  
Her love inspires life and light,  
Holding hands with every flower,  
It gifts the earth with soft delight.

- Henry Wadsworth Longfellow
