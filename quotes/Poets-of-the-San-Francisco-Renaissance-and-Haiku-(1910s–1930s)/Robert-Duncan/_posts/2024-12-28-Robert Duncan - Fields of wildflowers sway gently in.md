Fields of wildflowers sway gently in the breeze,
Each bloom a celebration of life's vitality,
Vivid splashes of color against the open sky,
Nature's palette, vibrant and alive,
Reminding us of the fleeting beauty of existence,
In the fleeting dance of petals in warmth.

- Robert Duncan
