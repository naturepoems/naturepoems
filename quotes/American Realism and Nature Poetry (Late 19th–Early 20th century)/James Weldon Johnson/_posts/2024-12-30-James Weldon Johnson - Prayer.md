Prayer

As I kneel by the river,  
   The water flows and sings,  
   I offer my heart in prayer  
   To the spirits of all living things.

You who weave through realms unseen,  
   Help me touch the essence of time,  
   Let me breathe in the wild,  
   And lose from my soul every crime.

---

- James Weldon Johnson
