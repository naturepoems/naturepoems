Among the towering giants, the redwoods stand,
Ancient witnesses to the passage of years,
Their roots wrap around stories buried deep,
In the sunlight filtered through emerald crowns.
Silence echoes amidst the whispering leaves,
As the forest breathes in harmony.

- Robert Duncan
