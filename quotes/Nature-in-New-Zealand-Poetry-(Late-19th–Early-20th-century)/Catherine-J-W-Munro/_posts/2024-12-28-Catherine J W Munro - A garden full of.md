A garden full of wonders,
Where every bloom tells time,
In the dance of bees and blossoms,
Nature’s perfect rhyme.

With every leaf that shivers,
And every blossom’s sigh,
A story circles ’round us,
Beneath the endless sky.

- Catherine J W Munro
