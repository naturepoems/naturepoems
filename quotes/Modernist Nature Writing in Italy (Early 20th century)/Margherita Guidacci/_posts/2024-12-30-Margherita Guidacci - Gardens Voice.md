In the sanctuary of a garden,
i find the quiet speak,
each blossom a voice, a song,
a memory etched in petals.

Even the rocks hold secrets,
smoothened by the passage of time,
every crevice a reminder
of what time will remember,
unfolding stories in sunshades.


- Margherita Guidacci
