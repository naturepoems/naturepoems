In the night, the forest breathes,  
a symphony of clicking insects,  
winds play among the leaves;  
underneath, creatures stir  
or drowse in the stillness—  
this realm alive with whispers,  
gathering the moonlight’s glow,  
a secret life seen only by stars.


- Aldo Palazzeschi
