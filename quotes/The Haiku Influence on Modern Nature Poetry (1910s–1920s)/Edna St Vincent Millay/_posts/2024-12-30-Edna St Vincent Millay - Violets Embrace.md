In dell where the wild violets bloom,
I find a solitude that seems to grow,
Each blossom an echo of nature’s sweet tune,
A symphony whispered between ebb and flow.
I breathe in the silence, let worries take flight,
For in this embrace, I am free from sorrow’s woe.
