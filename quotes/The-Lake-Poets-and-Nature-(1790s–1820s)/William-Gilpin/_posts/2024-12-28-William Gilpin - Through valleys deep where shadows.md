Through valleys deep where shadows glide,
And whispering winds come sighing,
In Nature’s arms, the worlds abide,
Beneath her boughs, all lying.
The petals fall, the seasons wane,
And yet the heart still loves,
The silent sky, the gentle rain,
These Nature’s hand, still proves.

- William Gilpin
