Among clouds woven like dreams,
The sky breathes in the fragrance of rain,
While rivers run wild, breaking
The shackles of their banks,
Singing their stories to the rocks,
And the earth embraces the chaos,
Loving the scraps of life,
In a rapture of blooms and thorns.
