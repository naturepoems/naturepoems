Oh, the glistening dew at dawn,
A treasure borne on leaf,
That greets the day with gentle yawn,
And bids the night to brief.

With every droplet, nature's tear,
A sparkle pure and bright,
It mirrors dreams that draw me near,
To the calm of morning light.

- William Ellery Channing
