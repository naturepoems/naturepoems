O for a draught of vintage!  
That hath been cooled a long,  
In a sandy deep, or cherry bough  
That is not borne away by wrong.  
O for a summer's night,  
Under the broad, starry sky,  
To lie and listen to the wind  
As it whispers softly by.

- James Hogg
