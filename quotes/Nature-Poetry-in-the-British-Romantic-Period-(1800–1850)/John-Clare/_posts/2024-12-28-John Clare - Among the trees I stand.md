Among the trees, I stand enchanted,
With whispers soft and clear,
The world around me is supplanted,
By nature's voice I hear.

From morning's light to evening shade,
Each moment is profound,
In harmony, the heart is made,
In this sweet, sacred ground.


- John Clare
