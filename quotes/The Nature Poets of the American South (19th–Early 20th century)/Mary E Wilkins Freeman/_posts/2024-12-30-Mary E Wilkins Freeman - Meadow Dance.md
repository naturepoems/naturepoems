In meadows wide where daisies bloom,
With colors bold and bright,
I find a peace that breaks the gloom,
In the soft morning light.

With every breeze, a thought takes flight,
And dances through the air,
In nature’s heart, I feel the right,
Of love beyond compare.

- Mary E Wilkins Freeman
