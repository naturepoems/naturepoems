The Wattle
I see it, wattle-flower,
Its golden blooms
In the soft Australian sun,
Framing the hills,
Smiling in the breeze,
A bright serenity,
Sweet scent that hovers,
A promise of warmth,
In the heart of the bush.

- R E D Evans
