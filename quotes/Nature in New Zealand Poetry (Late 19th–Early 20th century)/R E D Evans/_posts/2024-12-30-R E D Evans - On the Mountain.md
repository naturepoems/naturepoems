On the Mountain
Behold the mountain range,
Their peaks kissed by clouds,
In a majestic stillness,
Fingers of mist clasping stone,
A fortress of ancient whispers,
Time stands still,
As valleys cradle the silence,
And rivers carve their paths,
Through forests lush and green.

- R E D Evans
