As dawn awakens, fires the sky,
The colors blend, a sweet goodbye.
The day unfolds her arms so wide,
And bids the night a soft good-bye.

Come feel the warmth upon your skin,
In every moment, life begins;
The sun will rise, the shadows fall,
In nature's cycle, love is all.

- Elin Wägner
