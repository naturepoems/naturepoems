Wildflowers dance
   in the breeze,
   golden hues ablaze
   against the blue,
   they lift their faces,
   expectant and free,
   not waiting for a word
   but for the eye
   to recognize.
   I wonder, how does sunshine
   open such colors?


- Philip Whalen
