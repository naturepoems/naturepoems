In the hush of evening twilight,  
  When shadows softly creep,  
  The earth exudes a silence,  
  And nature falls to sleep.

  A sigh upon the river,  
  A star begins to gleam,  
  In the peaceful lap of dusk,  
  We float upon a dream.

- Marian McCulloch
