The garden breathes with colors bright,
A symphony of pure delight,
Where roses blush and daisies play,
In harmony, throughout the day.

The bee’s soft hum, the butterfly’s call,
In every petal, joy speaks all,
And in this grove, this sacred space,
The spirit finds its resting place.


- Roger Fry
