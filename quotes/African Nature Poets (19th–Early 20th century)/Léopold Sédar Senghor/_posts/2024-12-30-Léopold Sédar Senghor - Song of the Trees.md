Song of the Trees
Trees of Africa,
Your roots stretch deep,
Yours is the ancient wisdom,
In your shade, life blossoms,

With the whisper of leaves,
With the language of beasts,
Your trunks rising like giants,
Guardians of the sacred truth.


- Léopold Sédar Senghor
