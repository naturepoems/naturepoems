In winter’s grasp, the world turns white,  
A sparkling blanket enchants the night;  
The branches wear a coat so fine,  
In silence profound, the earth does shine.  
Each snowflake whispers a soft refrain,  
A promise of spring where life will reign.

- John Burroughs
