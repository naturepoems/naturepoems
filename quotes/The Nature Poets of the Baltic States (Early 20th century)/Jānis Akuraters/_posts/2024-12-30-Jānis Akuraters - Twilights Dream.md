In dusk, where shadows linger,
And fireflies softly gleam,
The heart is drawn to nature,
In twilight’s gentle dream.

A moment suspended,
In the hush of evening’s grace,
Filling our spirits’ longing,
In this sacred space.

- Jānis Akuraters
