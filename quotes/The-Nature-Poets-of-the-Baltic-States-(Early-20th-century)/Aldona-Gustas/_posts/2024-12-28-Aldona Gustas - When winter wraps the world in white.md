When winter wraps the world in white,  
stillness reigns, serene and bright.  
The trees stand stoic, adorned in frost,  
a fleeting beauty, never lost.  
In silence, nature finds its grace,  
a moment’s peace in time’s embrace.

- Aldona Gustas
