A Simple Idea

A woman

is most beautiful

when she is

small,

like a flower.

The only way

to think of her

is this way:

light drifting

over the land

like a breeze.

A motion of the

sun / this all

of anything.

It will die later,

but now grows

and is beautiful.

- William Carlos Williams
