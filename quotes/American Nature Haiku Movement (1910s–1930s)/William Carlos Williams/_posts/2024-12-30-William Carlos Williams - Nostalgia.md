Nostalgia
A flake
touched down
on the beautiful
carmine blossoms
of the cherry tree.
I remember
you there

as a child,
time out of mind,
breathing in the light,
your delicate lips
close-mouthed
silhouetted against
flaring petals.

- William Carlos Williams
