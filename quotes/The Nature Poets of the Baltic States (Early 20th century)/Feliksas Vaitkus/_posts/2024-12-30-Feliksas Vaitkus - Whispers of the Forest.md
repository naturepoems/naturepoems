In the forest deep, where shadows play,
A whispering breeze leads the way,
Among the trees, where silence thrums,
The heart of nature softly hums.

With mossy carpets underfoot,
And scattered leaves in wild pursuit,
The sunbeam dances, golden bright,
A beautiful tapestry of light.


- Feliksas Vaitkus
