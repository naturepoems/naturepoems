Ah, what a life of peace and rest, 
In the garden where the flowers bloom bright, 
Each petal a story, each stem a song, 
In this sanctuary of the heart.
Let me linger among the daffodils, 
And breathe in the fragrance of springtime's kiss.


- Elizabeth Barrett Browning
