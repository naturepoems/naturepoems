The Wind's Embrace
As I walked, the wind curled around me,
a gentle breath, a soft caress.
Dancing along the fields,
it sang a lullaby that warmed my skin,
reminding me of the power
nestled deep in nature's embrace,
a rapture of life unseen.

- Robert Bly
