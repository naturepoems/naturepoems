The mountain looms with majesty,  
Its peaks kissed by the sun,  
A sentinel of ages past,  
Where rivers’ roars outrun.  

In valleys deep, where echoes sing,  
Of ancient stories told,  
The earth, in stillness, holds its breath,  
As time begins to unfold.

- L W Lydia H W Milligan
