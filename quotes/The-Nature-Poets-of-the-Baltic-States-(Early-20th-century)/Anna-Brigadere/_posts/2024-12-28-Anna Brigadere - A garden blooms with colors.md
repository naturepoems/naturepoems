A garden blooms with colors bright,
In every petal, pure delight,
The honeybee, with gentle song,
Spreads life where nature can belong.

The fragrance of the earth awakes,
A symphony for all that aches,
With every breath, a joyful plea,
To cherish life and love the free.

- Anna Brigadere
