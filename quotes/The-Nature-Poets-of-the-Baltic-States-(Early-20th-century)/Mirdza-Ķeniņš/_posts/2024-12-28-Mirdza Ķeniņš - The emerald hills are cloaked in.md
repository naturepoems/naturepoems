The emerald hills are cloaked in mist,
Veil of silence draping time,
Among the shadows, memories twist,
Of nature's wonders, pure and sublime.

Crisp air filled with distant calls,
Of fox and deer in playful quest,
A canvas vast, where beauty sprawls,
In quiet moments, we are blessed.


- Mirdza Ķeniņš
