Beneath the Willow

Beneath the old willow,
   Where the shadows fall deep,
   I find my sweet refuge,
   In the silence of sleep.

With the breeze as my blanket,
   And the earth as my bed,
   I dream of the moments
   That tomorrow will shed.


- Louise McNeill
