Mountain Echo
by Robert Lowell

The mountains rise,  
a fortress of silence,  
as echoes haunt their heights,  
a guardian for the skies.

Their snow-capped crowns  
glistening in the sun’s gaze,  
a legacy of ages  
that speaks through the rocks,  
a hymn of resilience.

- Robert Lowell
