The Edge

The edge is clear. 
You must step off. The leaves
are the promise that greets you,
the wind plays in the thrush’
song. You will go on. 
No other dream
offers what is there. 
To tread on space, 
a dance of yours, moving in tree limb
and bark touch. The edges bend.


- Robert Creeley
