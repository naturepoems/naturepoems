Amidst the woods, a tranquil hush,
Where sunlight dances on the leaves,
The world outside fades to a blush,
In here, the spirit weaves and cleaves.

The calls of birds, a sweet refrain,
The rustle of the underbrush,
In nature’s hand, we seek no gain,
But solace found in every hush.

- John Rea
