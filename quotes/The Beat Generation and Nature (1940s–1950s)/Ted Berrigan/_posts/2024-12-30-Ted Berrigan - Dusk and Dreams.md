The sky turns violet
at dusk,
a painter washing
canvas with hues
lost in twilight.

As stars emerge,
they twinkle—tiny
glimmers of hope
in a world that rests,
giving pause
to the day’s
faithful end.

And in these moments,
we breathe deep,
a reflection of
a universe flowing,
where dreams can
grow.

- Ted Berrigan
