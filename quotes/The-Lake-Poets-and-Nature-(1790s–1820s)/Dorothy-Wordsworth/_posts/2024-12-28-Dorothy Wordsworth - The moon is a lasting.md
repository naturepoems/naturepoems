The moon is a lasting friend.
Though she may seem to be uncovered,
Her light bathes the world in silver tones,
And her beauty's hidden, sometimes discovered:
She makes the night serene and quiet,
While the stars whisper secrets, cast in a silver riot.

- Dorothy Wordsworth
