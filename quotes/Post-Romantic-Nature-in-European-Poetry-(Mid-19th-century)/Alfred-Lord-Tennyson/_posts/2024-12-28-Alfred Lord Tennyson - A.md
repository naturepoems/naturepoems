A Farewell

To the great tide of human life,
As waves roll forth and fade,
I bid adieu and joyfully,
The paths the heavens laid.

Nature's majesty and grace,
In every height and hue,
May always bless terrestrial face,
As Nature meets the new.

- Alfred Lord Tennyson
