The autumn leaves, crisp and bright,
A chorus of rustling sounds,
As they carpet the forest floor,
With hues that nature's brush surrounds.

A chill creeps through the evening air,
Yet the heart feels ever warm,
In every change, a beauty found,
A cycle, gentle, pure, and calm.

- Margaret Atkinson
