The air is filled with the light  
Of the spring and summer  
And the quiet of the thickets should not  
Refrain from secrecy,  
For it has been given a voice  
In the stream that stirs the leaves,  
Where all is radiant  
In the heart of the green, eternally free.

- Wallace Stevens
