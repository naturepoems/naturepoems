The autumn leaves, a fiery glow,
A whisper of the days gone by,
They twirl and dance with winds that blow,
A fleeting moment as they fly.

In every change, a lesson learned,
In every season, life portrayed,
Nature teaches, ever turned,
Through its cycles, we are swayed.

- Hermann Hesse
