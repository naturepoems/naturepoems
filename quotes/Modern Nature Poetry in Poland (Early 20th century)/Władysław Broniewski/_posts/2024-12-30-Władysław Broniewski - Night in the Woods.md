Under the canopy of stars,
Night wraps its velvet cloak,
The silence of the woods,
Cradles the dreams of the unseen.

Glow of fireflies,
Dancing like tiny lanterns,
Lighting the darkened way,
In the embrace of the night.

- Władysław Broniewski
