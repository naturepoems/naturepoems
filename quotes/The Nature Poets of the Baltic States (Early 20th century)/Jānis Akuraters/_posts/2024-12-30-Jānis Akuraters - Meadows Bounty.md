Through meadows filled with clover,
And skies so vast and clear,
The world is full of wonder,
In every sight and sphere.

With every bud that blossoms,
And every brook that flows,
Nature’s wealth surrounds us,
In beauty, love, and prose.

- Jānis Akuraters
