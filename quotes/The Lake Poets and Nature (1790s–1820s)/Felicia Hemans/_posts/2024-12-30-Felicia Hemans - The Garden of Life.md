The Garden of Life
In the garden's peaceful shade,  
Where fragrance lingers light,  
The flowers gaze at Heaven's face,  
In hues of pure delight.

There, whispering winds will play,  
And gather thoughts of grace,  
Sweetly nurturing the seeds,  
In this dear, sacred place.

- Felicia Hemans
