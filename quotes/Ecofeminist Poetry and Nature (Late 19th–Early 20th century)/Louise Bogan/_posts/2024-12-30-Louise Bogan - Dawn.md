Dawn  
Awake, the world in soft hues,  
   where the light brings forth  
a new scent of blossom and dew,  
   I stand in this dawn's embrace,  
with echoes still lingering  
   from the dark night's embrace,  
glancing at flowers,  
   at leaves forging their grace.

- Louise Bogan
