The Wild Rose   
A rose is not a rose   
Until it is a wild,   
Scented, rosy flower,   
Beneath the evening's sigh.   
But only when the sun does rise,   
And sparkles on the dew,   
Does nature's handiwork arouse,   
A sweet bouquet anew.

- Joyce Kilmer
