The blooming vale, a painter's brush,
With every petal, stories bloom,
In colors vibrant, hearts in hush,
Resounding life amid the gloom.

From valleys deep to mountain high,
Each blossom whispers ancient lore,
In nature’s arms, we learn to fly,
To cherish what we can’t ignore.


- Mirdza Ķeniņš
