Oh daybreak bright with light anew,
Awake the world with morning dew!
Each ray that pierces through the leaves,
A promise that the heart believes.

The chorus of the early birds,
In melodies beyond our words,
Reminds us of the wonder sown,
In every seed of love we’ve grown.


- Roger Fry
