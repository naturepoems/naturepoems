Rain Song

Softly it falls,
The rain, the dear rain,
Dancing on leaves,
It eases my pain;

With the drops on my cheek,
I feel the earth sigh,
And the flowers all smile,
As the clouds float by.

- Elizabeth Palmer Peabody
