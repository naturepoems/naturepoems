Awake to April’s gentle breeze,
As petals unfurl from their sleep,
To dance with laughter in the sun,
In the light where secrets keep.

Each blossom, a whisper of grace,
Entwined with threads of earthy loam,
Reminds us all of cycles true,
That nature calls us back to home.

- Laura Riding Jackson
