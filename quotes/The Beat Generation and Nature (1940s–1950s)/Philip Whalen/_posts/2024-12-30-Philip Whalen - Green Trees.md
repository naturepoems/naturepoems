Green Trees

In the cathedral
of green trees,
ancient whispers sound,
a secret language
of wind and sap.

What do they know
of time and space?
Standing still,
they watch the world,
its brief spell of existence.

Come, walk quietly
among them,Listening—
a gift of knowing.

Philip Whalen

- Philip Whalen
