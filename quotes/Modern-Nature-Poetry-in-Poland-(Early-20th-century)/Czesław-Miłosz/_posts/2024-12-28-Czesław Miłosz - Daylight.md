Daylight

Light breaks,
scattering gold across the fields,
where the wildflowers sway,
a soft orchestra of colors.

The air dances,
gentle and warm,
carrying whispers of life,
the breath of earth beneath my feet,
reminding me of promises,
woven in a tapestry of green.


- Czesław Miłosz
