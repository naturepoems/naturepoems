The Distant Hills
Upon the horizon,
The hills rise and fall,
Soft curves kissed by distance,
Where dreams live and wander —
A journey of the soul,
Into the depths of wonder,
Each rise a heartbeat,
Each valley a sigh.

- R E D Evans
