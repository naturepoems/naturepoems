Beneath the great blue dome of sky,
Where peace and beauty reign,
I sit beside the brooklet's flow,
And hear its sweet refrain.

The rustling leaves above me dance,
As if to greet the day,
With every glance, a fleeting chance,
To see their life at play.

- Ethel Turner
