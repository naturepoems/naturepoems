The sea is a wild chariot,  
With waves that roar and crash;  
It takes the ships upon its back,  
And rides with furious flash.  
 
Its songs are soft and tender,  
Yet fierce as life can be;  
So hold your sails and steady your hearts,  
For the sea is wild and free!

- Rudyard Kipling
