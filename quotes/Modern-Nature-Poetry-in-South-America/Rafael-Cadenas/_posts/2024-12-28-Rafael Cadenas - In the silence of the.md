In the silence of the mountains,
 I find the voice of the river,
the stones articulate 
a language of their own,

whispering stories 
of storms passed and future rains.
Nature folds itself 
in a relentless embrace,

gifting time, 
gifting breath, 
gifting life.


- Rafael Cadenas
