To a Poor Old Woman

munching a plum  

the old woman  

takes off her  

green stalk  

and opens  

toface the  

return of the skin  

to its purpose  

or the yield  

as it was withheld,  

like a sun  

heating sleep  

in green matter.

- William Carlos Williams
