The trees that are in the garden,  
In all their beauty,  
Their firm unfurling leaves,  
And the crystalline waters that flow,  
Their embrace my solitude,  
For beauty is furtive and common in this land,  
For beauty thrives in the breath of trees.

- Walt Whitman
