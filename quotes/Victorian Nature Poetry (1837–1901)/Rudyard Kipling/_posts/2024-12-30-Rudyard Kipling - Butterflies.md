The butterflies are like the dreams,  
That dance in sunlit skies,  
They sweep upon the dewy grass,  
And whisper soft goodbyes.  
 
They hold the secrets of the day,  
In colors bright and bold;  
A flicker here, a flutter there,  
And stories to be told.

- Rudyard Kipling
