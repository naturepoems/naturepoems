Come, let us hasten to the glade;
Beneath the oak’s broad leafy shade,
Where all the feathered tribes do sing,
And Nature’s hand doth summer bring;
No other charms do I desire,
Than flowers, birds, and fields afire.

- Sir Philip Sidney
