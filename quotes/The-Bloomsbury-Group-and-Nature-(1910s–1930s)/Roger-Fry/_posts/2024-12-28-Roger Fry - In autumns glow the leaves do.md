In autumn’s glow, the leaves do dance,
With russet hues that seem to prance,
And every gust, a fleeting kiss,
A moment held in nature’s bliss.

The harvest moon, a golden eye,
Illuminates the purple sky,
And in the air, a hint of change,
As life itself begins to range.


- Roger Fry
