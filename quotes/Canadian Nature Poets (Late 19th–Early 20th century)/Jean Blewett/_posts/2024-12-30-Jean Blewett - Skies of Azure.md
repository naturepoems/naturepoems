Skies of azure cradle me,
As I wander where daisies bloom,
Every petal, a memory,
In nature’s never-ending room.

With each step, and every sigh,
I converse with the wind and tree,
In harmony, as time slips by,
Lost in nature's melody.

- Jean Blewett
