Beyond the hills, the blue fades,  
Underneath, a cool grass shade,  
A fleeting day shall softly sigh—  
As twilight stretches among the leaves,  
And the stars light up the sky;  
An echo of space takes form—  
With nature’s voice, I am reborn.

- HD
