When the morning breaks, ah! softly,
   Upon the stillness of the earth,
A quiet hope wakes, unfaltering,
   In the heart of every birth.

The flowers awaken with laughter,
   The dew on the petals stays,
And around in the woods, that after,
   Echoes life with a thousand ways.

- Matthew Arnold
