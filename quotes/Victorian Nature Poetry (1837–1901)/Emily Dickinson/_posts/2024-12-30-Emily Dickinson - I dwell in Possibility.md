I dwell in Possibility —  
A fairer House than Prose —  
More numerous of Windows —  
Superior — for Doors —  

Of Chambers as the Cedars —  
Impregnable of Eye —  
And there, for Echoes, Hearers —  
And Memories — to fly —

- Emily Dickinson
