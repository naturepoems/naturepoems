The mountains rise like sentinels,
Guardians of the sky,
Shrouded in mist and mystery,
Bidding the clouds goodbye.

Deep in the heart of valleys,
Where the shadows softly creep,
Lies the echo of the earth,
In a secret place, we keep.

- Robert Bob O Lockhart
