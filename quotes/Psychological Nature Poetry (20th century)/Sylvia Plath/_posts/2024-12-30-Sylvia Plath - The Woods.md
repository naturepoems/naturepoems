I walk the woods,
            Among the tall pines,
Their needles whisper secrets,
               To the ground.
Each step,
           Underfoot, 
The crunch of bark,
Feeling the pulse
  Of the earth 
               Beneath,
A nature's song,
        Low, powerful.

- Sylvia Plath
