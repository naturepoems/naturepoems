The river flows, flows, flows
into the sea,
with dragging fogs,
carry me.
  

The air I breathe
gathers between the reeds,
my thoughts are leaves
carried by the breeze.
  

Moonlight spills,
like a waterfall,
into my soul;
I feel it all.

- Jack Kerouac
