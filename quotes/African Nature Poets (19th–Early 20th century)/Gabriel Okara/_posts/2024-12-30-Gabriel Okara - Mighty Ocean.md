**Mighty Ocean**  
O vast ocean,  
You rage with might,  
Crashing against the shores,  
Tell me your stories  
And the depths you hold,  
Of journeys untold,  
In your embrace I feel  
The pulse of time unfold.


- Gabriel Okara
