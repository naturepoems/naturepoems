Autumn Leaves

The leaves in their glory,
   Like flames in the air,
   They dance to the ground,
   In a wild autumn fair.

With a rustle and whisper,
   They tell of the skies,
   Of the soft fading sunlight
   Where the old raven flies.


- Louise McNeill
