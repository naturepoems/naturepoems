Each day the sun renews itself,
With morning glow and evening shade,
Awake to gift of life and health,
While nature’s wisdom will not fade.

So linger not in rows of stone,
But wander paths forged by the muse,
Where trees and waters gently moan,
And every moment is a muse.

- Henry David Thoreau
