The scent of pine upon the breeze,
In forests thick and high,
A melody that puts at ease,
A heart beneath the sky.

Each rustling branch and chirping sound,
A hymn of nature’s grace,
In every step, I’m glory-bound,
To nature’s warm embrace.

- Mary E Wilkins Freeman
