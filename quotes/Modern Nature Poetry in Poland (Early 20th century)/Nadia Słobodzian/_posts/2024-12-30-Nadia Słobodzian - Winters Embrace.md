A snowfall blankets the earth,  
a hush enveloping the world,  
every sound muffled  
in winter’s delicate embrace.  

Footprints whisper stories,  
of wandering souls,  
and the earth dreams,  
wrapped in its quilt of white.

- Nadia Słobodzian
