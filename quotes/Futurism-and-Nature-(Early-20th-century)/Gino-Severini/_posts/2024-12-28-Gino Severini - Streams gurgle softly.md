Streams gurgle softly, 
inivisible fingers of water— 
average stones smooth with time, 
a paradox— 
with each flow, a history of becoming.

With every ripple, 
the world redefines itself, 
a cycle unbroken, 
a narrative spun on nature’s loom— 
a tapestry of existence.

- Gino Severini
