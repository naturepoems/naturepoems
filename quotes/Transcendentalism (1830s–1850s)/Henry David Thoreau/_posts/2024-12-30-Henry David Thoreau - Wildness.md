In wildness is the preservation of the world.
So seek solitude, dear friend,
Among the trees and streams,
For there, our souls shall mend.

The river’s song calls softly,
A tranquil melody of peace,
Where heartache loses presence,
And the soul can find release.

- Henry David Thoreau
