In spring's embrace, the wildflowers rise,
A tapestry of hue,
They dance beneath the bending skies,
In colors bright, anew.

The gentle breeze, a tender kiss,
Caresses every petal,
In nature's garden, there is bliss,
Where hearts and spirits settle.


- Mary S M McCulloch
