The trees are our brothers;  
 the rivers our sisters.  
 We belong to the land  
 that birthed our souls;  
 we breathe together.  
 The heart of the earth  
 beats in our chests,  
 the spirit of the sky  
 whispers in our dreams.

- DH Lawrence
