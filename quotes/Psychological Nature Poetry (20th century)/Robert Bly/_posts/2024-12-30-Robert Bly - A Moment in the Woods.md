A Moment in the Woods
In the warming afternoon, I pause,
a light piercing through the dense canopy.
Leaves below my feet are but a soft surrender,
a moment pressed
between the pulse of birds
and the rustle of unseen life,
where silence frames the beauty of these woods.

- Robert Bly
