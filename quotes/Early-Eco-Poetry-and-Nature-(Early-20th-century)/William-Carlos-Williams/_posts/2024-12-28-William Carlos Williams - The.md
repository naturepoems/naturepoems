The Dance

We are like

the trees, moved

y the wind

taking us here

and there

and putting us

small, with roots

wound in soil,

or we rise

up and away,

to the light.

Like shadows

that drift

over grasses,

we slip and slide

in this dance

with nature.


- William Carlos Williams
