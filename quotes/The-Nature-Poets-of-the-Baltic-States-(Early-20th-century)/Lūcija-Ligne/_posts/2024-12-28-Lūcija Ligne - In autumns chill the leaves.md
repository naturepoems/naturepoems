In autumn's chill, the leaves descend,
A tapestry of red and gold,
Each falling whisper, a sweet blend,
Of nature’s stories, softly told.

The earth prepares, a quiet rest,
As winter’s breath draws near and near,
In every change, we seek the best,
Embracing cycles, year by year.


- Lūcija Ligne
