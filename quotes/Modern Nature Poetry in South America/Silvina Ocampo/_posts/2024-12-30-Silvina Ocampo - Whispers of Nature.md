Whispers of Nature

In the heart of dreams
where nature weaves
a tapestry,
I can hear whispers,
a heartbeat of the earth.
The wind carries secrets
wrapped in leaves,
a soft sigh beneath
when shadows dance
among the willows,
quivering in sweetness,
a lull upon my chest.

Silvina Ocampo

- Silvina Ocampo
