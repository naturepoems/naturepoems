The twilight sky in hues of fire,
A canvas painted bold and bright,
As day descends, a soft choir,
Sings the praises of coming night.

Stars emerge, in silence gleam,
In the vastness, lost in dream,
Nature's art, a sacred rite,
Guiding hearts through the tranquil night.

- Duncan Grant
