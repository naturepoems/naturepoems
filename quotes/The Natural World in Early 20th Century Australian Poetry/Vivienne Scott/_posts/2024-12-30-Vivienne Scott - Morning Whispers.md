In the stillness of the morning light,  
When dew-kissed grass is fresh and bright,  
The trees, adorned in emerald green,  
Whisper secrets, softly seen.

Birdsong dances through the air,  
Nature's chorus, rich and rare,  
Each note a brush, painting the scene,  
Of the tranquil, wild, serene.


- Vivienne Scott
