The Garden

Within the garden’s sacred ground,
    Where silence like a shroud
Enfolds the blooms of every sound,
    I stand alone and proud.

The petals hold a whispered charm,
    Beneath the willow’s sway;
And nature’s breath is sweet and warm,
    As light begins to play.

- John Shaw Neilson
