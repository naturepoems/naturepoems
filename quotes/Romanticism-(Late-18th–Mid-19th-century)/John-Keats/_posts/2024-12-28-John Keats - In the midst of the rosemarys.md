In the midst of the rosemary's green,
A gentler scent fills the air,
When nightingales make melodies,
And bring forth joys we cannot bear.

Nature paints with colors rich,
And carves out hidden glades,
Where echoes of sweet laughter rise,
So we might pause in serenades.

   — "The Rosemary" (Excerpt)
