Underneath the stars,
a tapestry of night unfolds,
revealing worlds unseen.

The moon reflects
on a quiet lake,
a mirror of tranquility
where dreams gently drift.

Each star a heartbeat,
a flicker of memory,
a reminder of the vast,
endless beauty that surrounds us.

- Alejandra Zambra
