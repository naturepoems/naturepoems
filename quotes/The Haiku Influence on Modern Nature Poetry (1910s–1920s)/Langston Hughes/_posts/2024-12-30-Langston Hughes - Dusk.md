Dusk
The sun's last light is fading,
As the day drifts away,
The twilight embraces softly,
With the calm of the day.

In the gentle touch of dusk,
  Where the shadows softly creep,
Nature breathes a quiet sigh,
In the quiet, come to sleep.

- Langston Hughes
