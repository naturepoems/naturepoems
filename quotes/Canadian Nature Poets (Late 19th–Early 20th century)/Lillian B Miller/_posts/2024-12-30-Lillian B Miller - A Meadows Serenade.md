Sun-kissed petals fall like rain,
Dancing on the breeze,
While golden sunlight bathes the lane
And stirs the honeybees.

The meadow sings a song so sweet,
With flowers in a row,
Nature's beauty at my feet,
In the gentle glow.

- Lillian B Miller
