Open thy gates, O Wind,
The white-capped waves shall go,
Dance from the hills, and silently
Climb every crest I know,
In the darkness of the sky,
Take me, o Wind! Take me.
To where the clouds gather great and free.

- Elizabeth Barrett Browning
