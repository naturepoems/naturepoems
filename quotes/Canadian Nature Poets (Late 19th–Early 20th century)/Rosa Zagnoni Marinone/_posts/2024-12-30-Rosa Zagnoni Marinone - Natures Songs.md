Nature's Songs

Let me hear the songs of nature,
The echoes of the trees,
The murmur of the gentle brook,
The whispers of the breeze.

For in that melody I find
A solace for my soul,
A harmony that binds my heart
With every drop and roll.

- Rosa Zagnoni Marinone
