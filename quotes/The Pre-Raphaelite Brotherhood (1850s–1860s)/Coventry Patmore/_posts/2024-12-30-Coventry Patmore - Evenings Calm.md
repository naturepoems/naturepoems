In the hush of evening's light,
The dew drops on the grass,
With humble love that dims the sight,
Such footprints let me pass.

In happiness serene we twined,
No storm within the boughs,
Flitting like a singed aside,
I feel the murmur’s sows.

- Coventry Patmore
