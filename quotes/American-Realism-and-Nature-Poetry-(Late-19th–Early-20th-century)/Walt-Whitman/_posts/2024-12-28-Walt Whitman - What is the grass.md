What is the grass?  
  I ask of myself,  
  And the winds that rustle through leaves.
I see them sway and dance,  
  Touching nature's very core,  
Their whispers, soft as secrets shared,  
  Poignant and pure, like a lover’s sweet lore.  


- Walt Whitman
