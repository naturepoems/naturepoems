The Bush

I love the deep and endless night,
    The stillness of the trees;
Where leaves are whispering with delight,
    And under starry keys.

A lonely scrub away from town,
    The ancient ancestors call,
While softly all the shadows drown
    In whispers over all.

- John Shaw Neilson
