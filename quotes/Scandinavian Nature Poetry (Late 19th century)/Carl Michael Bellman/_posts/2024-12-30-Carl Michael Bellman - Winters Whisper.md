O winter's chill, your breath so clear,
A whisper on the breeze,
The world transformed, the skies austere,
In every branch and freeze.

Yet in your heart, a beauty sleeps,
In every flake that falls,
A secret held, as nature keeps,
Her wonder through it all.


- Carl Michael Bellman
