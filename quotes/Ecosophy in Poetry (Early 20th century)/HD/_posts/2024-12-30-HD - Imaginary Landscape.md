Imaginary Landscape

   I follow the still shore,
   with a black front,
   shaken with little breaks,
   angry winds, the clusters  
   brush past me.

   I summon  
   the rain,  
   shake out the seaweed  
   draped & tangled in silence,  
   but there, I make a path.


- HD
