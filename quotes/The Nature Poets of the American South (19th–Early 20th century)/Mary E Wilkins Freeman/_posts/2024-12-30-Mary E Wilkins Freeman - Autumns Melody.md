The autumn leaves in colors bright,
Adorn the winding lane,
As day gives way to coming night,
In nature’s sweet refrain.

With every step, the crunching sound,
A symphony in cheer,
The beauty of this world I've found,
Whispers of the dear.

- Mary E Wilkins Freeman
