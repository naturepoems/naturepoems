A Forest Walk

Stretched beneath this verdant canopy,
Where trees like ancient pillars rise and sway;
I wander solitary, lost in thought,
And find within the silence what I sought.

Each leaf that quivers in the soothing breeze,
Reminds me of the beauty nature frees.

— Charlotte Smith
