The Fire

O wild flaming fire,
    gift of quiet, perfumed softness,
    make this heart be kind –
    if only the last sighs from these
    flourishing hands could spark.

    Be like the forest,
    matchlessly high,
    embroidering dreams
    on the quivering light.

—Rainer Maria Rilke
