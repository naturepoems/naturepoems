To the Moon

O lighted orb, thou dost suffice  
  To carry dreams to every heart;  
In the stillness of thy outspread voice,  
  Thy beauty serves life’s art.  
Thou'rt like the heart that beats in tune,  
  A reflective world below the moon.

- George Meredith
