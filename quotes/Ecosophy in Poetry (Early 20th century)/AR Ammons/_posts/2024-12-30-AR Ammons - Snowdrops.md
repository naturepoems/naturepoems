Snowdrops

Bright in winter,
snowdrops pierce
through layers
of bleakness
and slack soil.
They shake off
the chill,
burgeoning
with life.
The world stands still,
and yet,
of hope,
a reminder
a living surge,
fragile yet fierce.

- AR Ammons
