Upon the hill where violets grow,  
I roam in thoughtless joy,  
The sun-kissed petals all aglow,  
Nature's simple ploy.  
In solitude's embrace,  
The breezes breathe anew,  
Offering a soft, sweet space,  
Where wildflowers once and true.

- James Hogg
