Wild Nights – Wild Nights!  
Were I with thee  
Wild Nights should be  
Our luxury!  

Futile - the Winds -  
To a Heart in port -  
Done with the Compass -  
Done with the Chart!  

Rowing in Eden -  
Ah - the Sea!  
Might I but moor -  
Tonight - in thee!

- Emily Dickinson
