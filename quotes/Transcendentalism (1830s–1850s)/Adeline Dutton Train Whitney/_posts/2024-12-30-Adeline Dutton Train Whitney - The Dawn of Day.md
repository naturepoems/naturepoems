The Dawn of Day
The golden sun arises,
Painting all the waking skies;
Kissing each drooping flower,
Waking life to every hour.
The quiet brook twinkles bright,
As she flows in morning light,
Whispering secrets low and tender,
Of the day that’s soft and slender.

— Adeline Dutton Train Whitney
