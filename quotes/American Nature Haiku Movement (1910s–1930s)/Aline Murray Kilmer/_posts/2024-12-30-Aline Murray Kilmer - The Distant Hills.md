The Distant Hills
The hills are calling, calling so near,  
Wrapped in mist like a dream,  
They whisper softly on the wind,  
A song that echoes stream.  

With every rise, every fall,  
Nature sings to the sky,  
That we may chase our wanderlust  
And hear the solitude sigh.
