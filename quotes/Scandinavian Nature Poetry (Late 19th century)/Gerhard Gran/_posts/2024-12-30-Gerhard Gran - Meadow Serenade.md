Upon the meadows, wildflowers sway,
With colors bright, a painter's feast,
Bees hum softly at their play,
In this haven, nature's beast.

The sun dips low, the shadows creep,
As night falls gently from above,
In every heartbeat, memories seep,
Of nature's grace, of endless love.

- Gerhard Gran
