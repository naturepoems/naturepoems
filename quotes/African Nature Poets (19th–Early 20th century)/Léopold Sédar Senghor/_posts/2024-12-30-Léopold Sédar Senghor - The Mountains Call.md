The Mountain’s Call
Majestic mountain,
You rise against the sky,
A sentinel of time,
With your silent watchful eye.

In your shadows, stories dwell,
Whispered by ancient winds,
A calling echoing,
To the hearts of the brave.


- Léopold Sédar Senghor
