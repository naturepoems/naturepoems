Deep in the forest's leafy arms,
Where sunlight drips like honey dew,
The wildflowers in joyous bloom,
Speak the language known to few.

Petals bright, like splashes of paint,
On the canvas of the green,
They whisper secrets of the earth,
In the beauty that can be seen.

- Margaret Walker
