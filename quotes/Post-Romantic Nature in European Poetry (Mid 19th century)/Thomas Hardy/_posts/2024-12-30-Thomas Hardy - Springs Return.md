The branches twitch with life anew,
As soft rains cleanse the earth;
In fragrant soil, the blossoms grew,
To greet the spring's rebirth.

And as the days grow long and bright,
The sun's warm glow doth chase,
All shadows that have hid from sight,
To fill this tranquil space.

- Thomas Hardy
