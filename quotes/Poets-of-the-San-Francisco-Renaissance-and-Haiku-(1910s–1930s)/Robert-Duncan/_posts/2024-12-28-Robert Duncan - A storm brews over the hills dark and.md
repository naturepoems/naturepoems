A storm brews over the hills, dark and furious,
Lightning scrawls its brilliant stories across the sky,
Thunder rumbles like a heartbeat in the chaos,
The trees bend but do not break,
Emerging, they stand tall in the aftermath,
A testament to endurance in nature's wrath.

- Robert Duncan
