A lark’s sweet song does softly rise,
As violet blooms unfurl,
In meadows, where the clear blue skies
Embrace the earth's fair pearl.

The gentle touch of morning’s breath,
An echo of delight,
In nature’s arms, there’s naught but death,
For all is pure and bright.

- EB Eleanor F Williams
