Amidst the thicket's tangled weave,
I search for solace in the thorns,
For beauty often hides away,
In places where the heart is worn.

Let the mists envelop me,
As I wander through nature's maze,
For solace lies beyond the pain,
In the simplicity of her gaze.

- Laura Riding Jackson
