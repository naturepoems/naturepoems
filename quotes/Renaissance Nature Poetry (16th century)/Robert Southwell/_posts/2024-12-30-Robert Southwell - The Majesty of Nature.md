In valleys low and mountains high,
Where echoes of eternal sigh,
The majesty of life unfolds,
As courage from the unknown molds.
Within the realm of vibrant earth,
Each creature dances for rebirth;
O wondrous grace! Your bounteous feast,
In every leaf, in every beast.

- Robert Southwell
