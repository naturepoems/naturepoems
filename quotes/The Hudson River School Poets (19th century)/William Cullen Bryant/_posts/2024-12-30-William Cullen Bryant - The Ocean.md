Ye mournful echoes;  
That wander the hill and woods,  
What sadness lingers,  
In the sigh of the gentle moans?  
As soon shall the time be,  
When music shall fade and die,  
When the swans shall wail,  
And the peacocks fly.

- William Cullen Bryant
