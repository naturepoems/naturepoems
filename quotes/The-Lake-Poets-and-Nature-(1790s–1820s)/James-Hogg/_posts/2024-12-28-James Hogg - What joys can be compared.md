What joys can be compared,  
To the soft sweet breath of spring,  
When Nature's gentle hand  
Turns every leaf to sing?  
The whispers of the trees,  
The dance of flowers gay,  
Are signs of blissful heart  
In the fairest roundelay.

- James Hogg
