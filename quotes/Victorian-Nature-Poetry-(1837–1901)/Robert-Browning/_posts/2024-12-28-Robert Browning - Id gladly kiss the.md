I’d gladly kiss the trees,
Nor heed the dirt on my knees,
If only it brought me near,
To the love that shone so clear.
The sun, the stars, the moon,
All of nature’s sweet perfume,
Embrace me as I tread,
Where the path is richly spread.

- Robert Browning
