I’ve read your book of love, 
I see your pages, the stream of night; 
The falling stars, 
The murmur of leaves. 

Oh, love is a thing of beauty, 
A joy forever—
A fluttering song 
In the hearts of all.

So swap me an hour.
Or let me swim
In your rivers of life.
