A Wish

Would I were with thee, my native home,
Where no dark clouds or storms of sorrow come;
Where fragrance floats upon the gentle breeze,
And all the songs of birds create soft ease.

The very winds that linger round the hour
Should bear the balm of nature's fragrant flower.

— Charlotte Smith

- Charlotte Smith
