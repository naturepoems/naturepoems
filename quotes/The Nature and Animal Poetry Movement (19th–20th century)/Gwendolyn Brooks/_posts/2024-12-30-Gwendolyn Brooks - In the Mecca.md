In the Mecca

The congested breath
Of soot hangs hot, today.
My house is a slow sway.
Gather, friends,
And feel the inner hold,
Plant fruits upon the influence
Of present walls,
Boasting wisdom in-between
The green we loathe or clear.


- Gwendolyn Brooks
