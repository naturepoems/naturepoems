The Woodman's Daughter

In woods entwined where echoes play,  
  My love, my love can only share  
The quiet times, the gentle sway  
  Of leaves that linger in the air.  
And the heart that grounds her heavy stay,  
  In peace assimilates her care.

- George Meredith
