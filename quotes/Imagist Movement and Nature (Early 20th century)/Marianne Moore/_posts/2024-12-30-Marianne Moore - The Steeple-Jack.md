The Steeple-Jack

Hardened edges, navigating, gliding,
In the full experience, the active storm
Goes over thick thorns and flint.

Each turn tells part of its story,
Each turn asks of remnants circled.

Yet only the heart 
    of a leaf
    counts away freely.
