With heavy heart I trod the vale,
  Where shadows dimly tread,
  Yet leaves would shine to lift the sail,
  To carry me ahead.

From dusk to dawn, the starry sight,
  Shall bid me climb again;
  O nature, bend thy will with light,
  Let me ne’er breathe in vain.

- William Hazlitt
