Swallow, swallow!  
Fly from the cloud!  
Your horse is waiting!  
And I, amazed, begin to wait  
For the laughter that shone bright,  
Through the worthy ardour of days!  
In the radiant spaces and longing,  
   I heard that wondrous breeze.
