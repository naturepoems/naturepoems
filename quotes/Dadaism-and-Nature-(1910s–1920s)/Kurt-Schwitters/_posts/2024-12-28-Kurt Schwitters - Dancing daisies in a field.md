Dancing daisies in a field  
where silence hangs,  
softly shaken by the breeze,  
a riot of colors splashes  
the eye with abandon—  
dandelion, buttercup,  
each a little sun,  
each a little joke made  
in the laughter of nature.

- Kurt Schwitters
