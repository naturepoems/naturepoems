The brook runs clear and merry,  
Through valleys green and wide,  
It sings a song of joy,  
On a sweet and pleasant ride.  

With pebbles bright and dancing,  
It twinkles in the sun,  
A ribbon of pure laughter,  
Until its journey’s done.
