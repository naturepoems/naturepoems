O, the joy of the woods and the wild!  
The sky, the air, the streams, the trees,  
With their waving branches and their maiden look,  
So fair, so bright, so full of ease!  
O, the beauty of the earth in spring!  
How glorious when all green things sing!

- Samuel Taylor Coleridge
