A winter's hush, the world at rest,
Beneath a quilt of snow,
The silent trees, they wear their best,
In white, the earth aglow.

The frosty breath of morning light,
A crystal veil does lay,
In every corner, pure and bright,
A fleeting, sweet display.


- Mary S M McCulloch
