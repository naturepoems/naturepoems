In autumn's glow, the leaves take flight,
A tapestry of gold,
As whispers of the coming night,
Bring tales of time foretold.

The harvest moon hangs low and bright,
In tranquil, mystic air,
And nature's breath, a sweet invite,
Calls us to wander there.

- Ethel Turner
