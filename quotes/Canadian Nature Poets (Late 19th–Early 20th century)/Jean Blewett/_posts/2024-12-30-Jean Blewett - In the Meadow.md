In the meadow, green and bright,
Where the daisies dance in light,
Butterflies on wings of gold,
Fleeting joys that never grow old.

Cattle browse by the silent stream,
Contented in the sun's warm beam;
And the lark, with jubilant song,
Cheers the earth where we belong.

- Jean Blewett
