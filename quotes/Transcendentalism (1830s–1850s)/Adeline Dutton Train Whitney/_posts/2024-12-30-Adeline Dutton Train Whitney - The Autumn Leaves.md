The Autumn Leaves
The autumn leaves, like memories,
In colors bold, they drift and tease;
A tapestry of gold and red,
Like whispers of the year long fled.
They crunch beneath our eager feet,
A sound of time, a soft retreat;
And as they fall, they gently share,
The fleeting moments of autumn air.

— Adeline Dutton Train Whitney
