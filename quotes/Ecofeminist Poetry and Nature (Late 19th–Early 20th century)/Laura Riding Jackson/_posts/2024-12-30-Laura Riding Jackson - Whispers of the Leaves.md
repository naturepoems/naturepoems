All the rays
I have not caught
Rejoice that I have caught them,
With a shared cacophony,
Of vibrance and wild abandon.
And should I falter in the face
Of winter's cruel embrace,
Let the leaves whisper back to me,
That every loss,
Is simply a new flight to find.

- Laura Riding Jackson
