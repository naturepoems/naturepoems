After the Storm

When the clouds fly free,  
And the twisted trees  
Stand taller than before,  
The dark rocks will glow.

Green moss will lurch  
Under the feet of the stars  
And I shall wander alone  
Where the gentle rains  
Have washed all memory away.

- Amy Lowell
