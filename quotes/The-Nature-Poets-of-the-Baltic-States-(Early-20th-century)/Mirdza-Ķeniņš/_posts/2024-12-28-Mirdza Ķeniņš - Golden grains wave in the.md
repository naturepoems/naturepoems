Golden grains wave in the field,
Rhythmic with a summer's breath,
A bounteous harvest, nature's yield,
Life spinning forth through cycle's death.

With every seed, a story sown,
In earth's embrace, we find our place,
Among the flora - joy has grown,
In nature's dance, we move with grace.


- Mirdza Ķeniņš
