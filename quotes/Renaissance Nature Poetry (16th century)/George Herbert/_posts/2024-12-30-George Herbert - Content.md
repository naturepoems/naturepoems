Content

The joy of songs and a new endeavor  
As rising water wells upon the earth,  
Grips every spirit to its tender rib,  
And grants me my unlimited worth;  
Of love, of clever smiles, foal their grace  
Whose voices give and take from heaven and earth.



- George Herbert
