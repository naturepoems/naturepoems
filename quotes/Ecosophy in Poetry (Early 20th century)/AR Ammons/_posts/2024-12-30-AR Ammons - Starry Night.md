Starry Night

In the stillness,
many eyes see,
stars blaze,
a flickering legacy.
The vastness uplifts,
a gathering of light,
xylophones playing
ancient truths,
celestial narratives. 
This sky,
a blanket woven
of dreams and echoes.

- AR Ammons
