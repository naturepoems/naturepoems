Spring
This is when the wild beginnings
of life hold hands with the blooming,
a season where everything interrupts,
the earth drinking up the gentle rain,
as I wander barefoot through the ticking grass,
each step a prayer, each moment
an invitation, alive with the pulse of spring.

- Mary Oliver
