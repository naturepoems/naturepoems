In the forest, I wander,
Where the trees stand tall and grand,
Their whispering leaves a melody,
Nature's touch, a gentle hand.

The sunlight dances on the ground,
Through branches it descends,
A tapestry of shadows,
Where the world around me bends.

- Hermann Hesse
