Summer Song

When dusk spills its paint,
And crickets hum a soft tune,
Nature wraps its stillness,
In summer’s gentle bloom.

Flowers sigh in languid sleep,
While wild things come alive,
Delighting in the twilight’s spell,
Where pulsing shadows thrive.


- Gwendolyn Brooks
