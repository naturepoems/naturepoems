Song of the River

Water flows,
a gentle hymn
from high mountains.

Each bend tells a story,
in whispers or roars,
yesterday’s dreams
on a journey to the sea.

Do you hear it?
The river doesn’t hurry,
just carries its songs,
mingling with your heart,
your breath echoing.

Philip Whalen

- Philip Whalen
