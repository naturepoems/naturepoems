The Moon

   The moon
   is a still observer—
   wise in her pearl-gray
   and splotchy silver,
   the pale milk petals
   at her foot,
   the dim weeds drawn down,
   flank out to the dark side—
   a breathing absence is thus,  
   the echo of hands stroking leaf.

- HD Hilda Doolittle
