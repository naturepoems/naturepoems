The mountains stand like sentinels,
Guardians of the time,
In their shadows we find solace,
In their heights, we find the sublime.

With each peak draped in glory,
A testament remains,
To nature's timeless beauty,
That ever waxes and wanes.

- Kārlis Skalbe
