Here at the fountain's quiet nook,
Where we might hear the lilting brook,
We would entwine our love below,
And showers down of lilies flow.

A moment lost, yet never gone,
Will bond with us; in nature's dawn,
The joys of happy rising spring,
On myriad wings our hopes would sing.

- Robert Herrick
