Spring Awakening

When the crocus first shimmers,
   And the snowdrops come through,
   I dance in the meadow,
   In the soft morning dew.

With a heart full of joy,
   And the sun in my hair,
   I greet the new season,
   With love everywhere.


- Louise McNeill
