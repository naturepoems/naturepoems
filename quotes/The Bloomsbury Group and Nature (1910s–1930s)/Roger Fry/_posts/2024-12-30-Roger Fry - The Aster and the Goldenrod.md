The aster and the goldenrod
 Are waving for my eye;
 The broad, green meadows of the hill
 Are spread beneath the sky.

 And here the daisies frolic round
 Beneath the sunlit breeze,
 And in the river's peaceful sound
 I find a sweet reprise.


- Roger Fry
