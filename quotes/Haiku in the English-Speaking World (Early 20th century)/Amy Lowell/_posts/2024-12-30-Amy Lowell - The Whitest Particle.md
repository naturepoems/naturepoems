The Whitest Particle

The whitest particle of a flower  
Became a drop of dew.  
A touch of sunlight passed over  
For a moment, and then burst,
A flash of light and gone,  
Like a soft wind vanished  
Into urgent petals  
Of transformation.

- Amy Lowell
