In the green light
Under a thick sun,
Everlasting hills, 
Waters touch and sing
the thorns of a rose.

A whisper, a tide,
The earth replies content,
This sphere of murmurs, 
The laughter of branches
Beneath the springtime sky.

- Ezra Pound
