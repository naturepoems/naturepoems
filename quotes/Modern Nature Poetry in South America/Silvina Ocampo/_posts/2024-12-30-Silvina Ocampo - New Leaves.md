New Leaves

In the forgotten houses
of the old estate,
new leaves rise,
tender and bright,
an unearthly green
depending on the light.
Life collects warmth
as shadows fade away,
gentle and timid.
A single bloom,
delicate as glass;
it is but a pearl,
a grain of sand
a soft touch of dark sound.

Silvina Ocampo

- Silvina Ocampo
