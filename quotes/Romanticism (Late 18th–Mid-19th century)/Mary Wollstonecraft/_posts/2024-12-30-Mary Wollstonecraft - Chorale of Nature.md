I love to hear the neighboring choir
Sing to the fires, the silent stream,
The wild winds twinkle with mirthful fire,—  
Oh let my heart be lost in dreams!
For nature is a voice unheard,
Through my silence softly murmured.

- Mary Wollstonecraft
