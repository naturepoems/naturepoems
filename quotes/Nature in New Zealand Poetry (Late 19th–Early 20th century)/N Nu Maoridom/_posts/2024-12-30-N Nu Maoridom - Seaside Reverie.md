Seaside Reverie

Upon the shore where waves caress,
In salt-kissed air, I find my rest,
The ocean's breath, a lullaby,
In endless blue, the spirits fly.

With every tide, a tale retold,
Of moonlit nights and hearts, bold,
The seabirds call, the horizon sings,
While nature wraps me in her wings.

- N Nu Maoridom
