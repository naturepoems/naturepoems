The Great Figure

Among the rain

and lights

I saw the figure 5

in gold

on a red

firetruck

moving              

tense

unheeded

to gong clang

of the hoarse

wheeled

bell

my head

and held

forever

in that

golden

moment.

- William Carlos Williams
