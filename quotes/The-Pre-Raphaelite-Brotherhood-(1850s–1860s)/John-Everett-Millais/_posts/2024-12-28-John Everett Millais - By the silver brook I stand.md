By the silver brook I stand,  
Watching ripples dance and gleam,  
Here in peace, this tranquil land,  
Whispers softly, like a dream.

Nature speaks in silent ways,  
With every rustle and each wave;  
A symphony of sunlit rays,  
In the heart of the greenwood cave.

- John Everett Millais
