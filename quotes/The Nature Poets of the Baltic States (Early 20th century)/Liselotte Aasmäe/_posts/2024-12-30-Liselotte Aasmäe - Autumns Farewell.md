As autumn leaves flutter, 
In a crisp, gentle breeze, 
Nature dons her colors, 
With a grace that appease.

Gold, crimson, and amber, 
All dance in the air, 
A farewell to summer, 
With beauty so rare.

- Liselotte Aasmäe
