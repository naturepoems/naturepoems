Sunset Serenade

As the sun bows down and fades,
A tapestry of colors blend,
Gold and crimson dreams cascade,
A moment where day meets end.

The horizon whispers softly,
A lullaby to the stars,
In the arms of the twilight,
Nature heals all our scars.

- Ossie Mthembu
