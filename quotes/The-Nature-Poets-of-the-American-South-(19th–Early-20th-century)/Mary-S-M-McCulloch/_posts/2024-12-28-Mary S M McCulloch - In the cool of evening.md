In the cool of evening light,
The whispering winds do sway,
The trees adorned in soft twilight,
Breathe secrets of the day.

A brook, like silver ribbon, flows,
Where sunlight dapples through,
Each pebble, 'neath the current's prose,
Is mirrored in the blue.


- Mary S M McCulloch
