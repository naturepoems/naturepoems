The mountain stands in silence,  
Its head of ancient stone;  
Guarding secrets of the ages,  
In presence all alone.  
 
It watches over rivers,  
And valleys deep and wide;  
As time breathes o'er its surfaces,  
With legend as its guide.

- Rudyard Kipling
