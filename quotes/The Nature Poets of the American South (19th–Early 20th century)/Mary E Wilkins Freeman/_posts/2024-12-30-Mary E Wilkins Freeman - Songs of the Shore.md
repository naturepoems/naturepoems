Upon the shore where soft waves sigh,
And seagulls dance in flight,
I find my spirit drifting high,
In the glow of fading light.

The sands of time, so warm and fine,
Embrace each fleeting moment,
In nature’s arms I will confine,
My heart’s enduring sonnet.

- Mary E Wilkins Freeman
