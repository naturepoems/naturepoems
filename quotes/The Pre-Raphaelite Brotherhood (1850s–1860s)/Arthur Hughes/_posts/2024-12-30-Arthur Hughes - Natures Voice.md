From vale to hill, from hill to sea,
   The voice of nature speaks,
A harmony of wild decree,
   Where every craggy peak
  
Is crowned in mist, a phantom wan,
   And echoes herd the call,
While valleys fold the timid dawn,
   Beneath the shadows tall.


- Arthur Hughes
