Beneath the clouds, the fields lie bare,
Touched gently by the morning air,
Nature's quiet, a soothing balm,
In her stillness, we find calm.

As twilight cloaks the day in blue,
The stars awaken, fresh with dew,
In the embrace of endless night,
Nature cradles hope in her light.
