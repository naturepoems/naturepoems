In the hush of twilight,  
when the sun sinks low,  
the forest whispers softly,  
its secrets to the stream.  
Leaves murmur tales of ages past,  
a symphony of green,  
where time flows like water,  
a dance of moonlight and shadow.

- Aldona Gustas
