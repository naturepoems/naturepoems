Beneath the fruitful branches,
The cool winds stir the land,
Where the afterglow of summer
Still lingers ‘neath the hand.

Come, lie and dream upon the flowers,
The odorous, golden hours,
That linger in the shadows
Of the dearest summer bowers.


- Elizabeth Siddal
