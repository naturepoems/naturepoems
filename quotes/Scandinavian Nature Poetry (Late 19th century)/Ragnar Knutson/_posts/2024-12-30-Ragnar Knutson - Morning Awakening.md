In the hush of morning light,
The dew-kissed fields awake,
A symphony of gentle whispers,
As nature stirs and takes.

The sun breaks through the clouds,
A golden hue unfurls,
The blossoms lean to greet it,
In a dance of swirls and twirls.


- Ragnar Knutson
