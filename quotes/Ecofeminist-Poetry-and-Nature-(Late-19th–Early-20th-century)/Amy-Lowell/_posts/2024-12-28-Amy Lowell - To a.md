To a Butterfly
Wherever you flitter,  
Free as the wind,  
What do you ponder?
Or is it just pleasure,  
Reveling in the sublime?  
You rise and fall in the sun,  
And amidst soft blooms you play,  
As if nature's whisper calls your name,  
Clearing the path for your flit away.

- Amy Lowell
