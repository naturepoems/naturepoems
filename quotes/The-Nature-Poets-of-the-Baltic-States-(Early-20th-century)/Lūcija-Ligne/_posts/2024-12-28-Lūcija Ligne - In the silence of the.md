In the silence of the forest,
Where whispers of the wind abide,
Ferns flutter softly in the twilight,
As secrets of the earth confide.

Mossy boughs draped in emerald,
Kissed by a gentle morning dew,
Each leaf a tale of ages told,
In the lap of nature, ever new.
