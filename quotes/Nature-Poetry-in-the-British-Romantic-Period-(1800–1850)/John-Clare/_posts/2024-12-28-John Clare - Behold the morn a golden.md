Behold the morn, a golden hue,
Awakens with its glance,
The flowers blush, the sky is blue,
Nature leads on the dance.

In every petal, every leaf,
Life sings a vibrant song,
In beauty's arms, we find belief,
And know where we belong.


- John Clare
