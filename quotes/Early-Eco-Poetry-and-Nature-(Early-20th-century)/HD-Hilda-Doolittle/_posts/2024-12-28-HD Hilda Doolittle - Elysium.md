Elysium

The old gods are dead.   
The new gods are born.   
The life of the woods and the waters  
Set my feet on the stones.   
And they beckoned me down  
To sun-drenched shores  
Where flowers left off  
The dull weight of earth  
And the warm salt air rested lightly.







- HD Hilda Doolittle
