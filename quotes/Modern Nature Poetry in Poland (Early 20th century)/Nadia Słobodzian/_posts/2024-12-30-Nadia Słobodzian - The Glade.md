In a glade where silence reigns,  
a carpet of moss hugs the ground,  
ferns unfurl, ancient dancers  
enacting their unending story.  

Time sleeps here, cradled  
in the arms of the wild,  
hidden wisdom beneath old bark,  
a soft sigh of existence.

- Nadia Słobodzian
