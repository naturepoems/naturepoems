As summer’s breath begins to fade,  
The amber leaves in silence wade,  
Each flutter, a farewell kiss,  
In autumn’s arms, a moment of bliss.

And as the world prepares for sleep,  
The rustling whispers of secrets deep,  
Nature cradles every sigh,  
In her embrace, we learn to fly.


- Vivienne Scott
