The autumn leaves, a fiery blaze,  
a farewell dance, a fleeting phase.  
Like memories swept by the wind,  
a tapestry of joy rescind.  
Embrace the change, let colors show,  
the earth’s own celebration of letting go.

- Henry Agard Wendt
