The Garden

   As the aged theme sets us aside,
   —to look at the flowers
   and the golden coins,
   and breathe in calamity  
   is the same as  
   remembering
   the lustrous hour
   when print on the mind
   —like a flower pressed against glass   
   remains so clear.


- HD
