The Symphony of Shadows
Branches stretch like fingers,
Grasping at the breath of night,
An orchestra of rustling leaves,
Plays a requiem for the sun.

Crickets serenade the stars,
In a twilight dance of silence,
While the darkness blooms with secrets,
Eclipsing the day’s weary tales.


- Dadas Rallying Poets
