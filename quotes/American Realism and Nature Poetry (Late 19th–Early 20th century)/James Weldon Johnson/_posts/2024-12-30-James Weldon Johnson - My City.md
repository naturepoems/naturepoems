My City

My city is a wilderness,  
   A world of life and shadow,  
   Tall buildings rise like trees  
   Under a sprawling starlit canopy.

I wander through the parks,  
   Once fields of green and bliss,  
   Now splashed with hues of concrete  
   In a dance of nature's kiss.

- James Weldon Johnson
