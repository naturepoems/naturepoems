A summer's eve, the world aglow,  
Fields of wildflowers, a vibrant show.  
Bees hum softly as they roam,  
In this fragrant patch, I feel at home.

Sunsets melt with fiery grace,  
Colors merging, a warm embrace.  
Every grain of sand on shore,  
Whispers tales of days before.

- Sigrid Undset
