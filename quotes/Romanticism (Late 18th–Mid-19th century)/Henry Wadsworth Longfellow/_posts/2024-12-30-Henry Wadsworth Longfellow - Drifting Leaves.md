Drifting Leaves

The leaves are falling, falling,  
Drifting to the ground;  
Nature’s gentle call, calling  
In whispers all around.

Golden, crimson, and brown,  
They carpet all the earth;  
A tapestry of colors  
Foretelling winter's birth.

- Henry Wadsworth Longfellow
