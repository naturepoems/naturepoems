As the clouds drift,
they carry my thoughts,
a canvas of dreams,
untangling the threads
of yesterday into tomorrow.
A painted sky,
a prayer offered with every breath,
reminds me,
we are all part of the same fabric,
a delicate existence thread by thread.


- Alice Walker
