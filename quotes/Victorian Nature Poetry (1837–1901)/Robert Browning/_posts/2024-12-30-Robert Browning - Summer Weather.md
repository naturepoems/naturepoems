Summer Weather  
I.  
The lark is in the sky,  
For summer’s air is sweet;  
Forget all dreariness,  
And shake the sluggish feet!  

II.  
For at the sun's high noon  
Just dust the chains away,  
And let the bright sun’s bloom  
Bring out new life today!

- Robert Browning
