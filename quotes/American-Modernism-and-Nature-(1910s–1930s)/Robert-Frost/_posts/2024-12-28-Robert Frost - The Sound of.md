The Sound of Trees
I wonder about the trees:
Why do we wish to bear
Forever abandoned trees?
I think I know.

I speak of trees to sweet the heart,
A wishing breed of lonely sighs:
The trees are beautiful
Thinking leaves are guarded by the sun.
