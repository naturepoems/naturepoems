Clouds
Gentle clouds, you roam,
Through the canvas of the blue,
In your soft embrace,
The skies find a place to dream.

Each drop you hold,
Is a promise waiting to fall,
In the arms of the soil,
Life is born, again, anew.


- Léopold Sédar Senghor
