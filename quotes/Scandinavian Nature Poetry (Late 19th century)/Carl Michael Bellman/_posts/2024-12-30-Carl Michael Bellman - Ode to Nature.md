From the green forest's peaceful glade,
Where flows the water clear,
To thee, O Nature, I have laid
My heart without a fear.

When sunbeams through the oak trees shine,
And daisies deck the lea,
I wander in thy paths divine,
And feel thy joy in me.


- Carl Michael Bellman
