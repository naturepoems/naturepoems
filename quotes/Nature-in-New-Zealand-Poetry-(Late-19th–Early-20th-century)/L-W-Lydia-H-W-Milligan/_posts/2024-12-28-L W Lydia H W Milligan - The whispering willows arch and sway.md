The whispering willows arch and sway,  
As breezes gently sigh,  
They weave a tale of days gone past,  
Beneath the open sky.  

With roots dug deep in soil so rich,  
And branches reaching wide,  
They stand as watchers of the earth,  
In nature’s arms abide.

- L W Lydia H W Milligan
