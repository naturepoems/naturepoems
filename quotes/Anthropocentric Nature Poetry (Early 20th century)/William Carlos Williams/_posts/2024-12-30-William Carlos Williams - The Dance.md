The Dance

Applause – applause

The naked girl

in the bright light

standing on the

roaring sea, all

the light,

and the pulsing

of the clouds

resounding to her

every step

Be free – be

free.

O soundless night

there is no

shadow deeper

than this silence.

- William Carlos Williams
