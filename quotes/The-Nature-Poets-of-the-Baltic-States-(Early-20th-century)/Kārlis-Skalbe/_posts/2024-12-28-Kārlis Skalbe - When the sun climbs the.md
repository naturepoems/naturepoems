When the sun climbs the heights,
And the silent forest breathes,
The day awakens, and with it,
The heart of the earth unfolds.

In the glimmer of dew,
On the green blades’ tips,
Nature murmurs softly,
In a language older than words.

- Kārlis Skalbe
