The brook goes on, a distant sound,
As if at play,
While all around, the trees, profound,
Stand still till night and day.

As night approaches, softly still,
The light begins to fade,
The brook reflects the moon's first thrill
And in the woods, let shade.

- Henry Wadsworth Longfellow
