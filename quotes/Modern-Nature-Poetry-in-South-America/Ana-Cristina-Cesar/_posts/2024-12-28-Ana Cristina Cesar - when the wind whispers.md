when the wind whispers 
in thick layers of leaves,  
birch and cedar twist 
beneath the weight of 
a rainbow,  
in shiny colors 
the sun struggles 
to sprout through,  
as if it could illuminate 
every whisper, every pulse, 
that reinvents silence.

- Ana Cristina Cesar
