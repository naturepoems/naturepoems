The golden-rod is yellow—
The corn is green and brown;
The bramble in the berry thicket,
Is blossomed all around;  
And over the hills and valleys  
The sun looks forth with smiles;  
Where once was only Winter,  
The spring reclaims the miles.

- Robert Browning
