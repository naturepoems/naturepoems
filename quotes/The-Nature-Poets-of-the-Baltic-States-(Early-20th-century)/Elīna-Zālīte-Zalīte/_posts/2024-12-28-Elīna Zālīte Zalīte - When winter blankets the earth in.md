When winter blankets the earth in white,
And trees wear coats of frost,
In the silence, a world of light,
Amidst the beauty, all is lost.

Snowflakes dance with gentle care,
Kissing the sleeping ground,
In nature's hush, the world lays bare,
A magic so profound.

- Elīna Zālīte Zalīte
