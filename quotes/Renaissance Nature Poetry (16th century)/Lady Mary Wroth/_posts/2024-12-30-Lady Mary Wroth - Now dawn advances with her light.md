Now dawn advances with her light,
And colors fill the skies,
As night gives way to morning's height,
And life in beauty flies.

The birds awake and sing their tunes,
As petals stretch and gleam,
And Nature wraps her arms around,
To sooth each heart's lost dream.

- Lady Mary Wroth
