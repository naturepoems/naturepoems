The sun, a bold, fiery ember,
Sinks low in the evening sky,
Casting shadows on the river,
Where the whispered breezes sigh.

The trees in stillness glow and fade,
A gentle rustle in the night;
The stars, like lanterns softly laid,
Awake in their celestial flight.

- Marjorie Barnard
