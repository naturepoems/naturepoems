Each leaf is holding stories  
Of every drop of dew,  
A chronicle of seasons,  
In every verdant hue.  

They whisper tales of sunlight,  
And the rains that have kissed their skin,  
A legacy of life, renewed,  
As nature breathes within.

- Selma Lagerlöf
