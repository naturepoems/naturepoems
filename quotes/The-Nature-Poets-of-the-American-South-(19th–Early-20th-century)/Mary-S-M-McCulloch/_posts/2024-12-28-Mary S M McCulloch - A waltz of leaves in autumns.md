A waltz of leaves in autumn's hand,
The golden hues descend,
They dance and flutter, bright and grand,
As time begins to bend.

The whisper of the crickets' song,
The harvest moon's bright glow,
In nature's symphony, we belong,
Where love and beauty flow.


- Mary S M McCulloch
