Ode to Melancholy

No, no! go not to Lethe, neither twist
   Wolf's-bane, tight-rooted, for its poisonous wine;
Nor suffer thy pale lips to tell me that
   The lilies are faded, gods in twine:
Dissolving in a world unmatched define
   Think instead of the quiet foliage thine.

       John Keats

- John Keats
