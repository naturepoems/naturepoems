As rivers carve through rugged stone,
Nature's hand has gently sewn;
With every current, every tide,
The stories of creation bide.
The mountains whisper ancient lore,
Of battles waged and peace once more;
In every drop, in every gust,
The pulse of life, in love, we trust.

- Robert Southwell
