In the Meadow
In the lush green meadow,  
Where wildflowers sway,  
Life whispers through zephyrs,  
In a blissful ballet.

The grass dances with laughter,  
The butterflies play chase,  
While the sun drapes its blessings,  
Over every living face.

- Émile Verhaeren
