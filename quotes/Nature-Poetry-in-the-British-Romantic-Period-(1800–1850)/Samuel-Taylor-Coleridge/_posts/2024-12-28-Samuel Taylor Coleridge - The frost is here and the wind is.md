The frost is here, and the wind is rude;  
I can do nothing but sit and brood;  
The stars are shrouded all in mist,  
And the spirit of the morning kissed  
The jewels of the autumn’s shower,  
Upon the earth in a wondrous bower.

- Samuel Taylor Coleridge
