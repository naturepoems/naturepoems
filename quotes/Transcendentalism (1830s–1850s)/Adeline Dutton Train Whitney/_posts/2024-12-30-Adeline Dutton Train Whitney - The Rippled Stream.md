The Rippled Stream
The rippled stream does softly flow,
Under the shade of arching trees,
Whispering secrets to the stones,
Murmuring gently in the breeze.
It carries dreams from yonder hills,
To cast upon the sunny shores,
A sparkling path of light and life,
Where beauty blooms and nature soars.

— Adeline Dutton Train Whitney
