The Art of Poetry

To be a poet is to feel
  The essence of the world,
And nature thrives till they conceal
  The fabric slowly furled.

   And thus the rhyme takes root at dawn,
   As trees leap up in song,
Their branches as the earth is worn,
  Love whispers all night long.


- Ben Jonson
