in a garden of vapor, 
i write of colors,  
the fragrance of frost 
around the feet of a statue,  
a symphony of clamoring 
bees explodes under the sun,  
its laughter scattering 
into the underbrush, 
a hymn to the fading shadow of grass.

- Ana Cristina Cesar
