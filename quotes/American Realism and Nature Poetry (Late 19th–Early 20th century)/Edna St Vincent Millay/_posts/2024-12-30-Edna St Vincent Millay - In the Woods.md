The breezes in the trees, 
I hear their whispering tune;
And sunlight filters through the leaves,  
To dance beneath the moon.

The flowers sway in colored bloom,  
As gentle breezes sigh;
In nature’s arms, the world will bloom,  
Beneath the endless sky.


- Edna St Vincent Millay
