In the stillness of the woods,
Where the light filters through,
Nature whispers ancient truths,
Every leaf, a voice anew.

The river sings a soft refrain,
Among the rocks and fallen leaves,
Mirroring the sky's expanse,
With secrets that the forest weaves.

- Margaret Atkinson
