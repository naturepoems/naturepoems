Adagia

The storm; the still.
The hot; the cold.
The earth; the break.

The later wave of night.
The receding charm of the morning,
The cracked stain, fruitless,
In the waking boughs, thin in their state,
As they reach restful teems,  
Of more light.

- Wallace Stevens
