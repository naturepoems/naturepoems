The Fisherman

Aye! I can preach that life is sweet;  
  But they who pull the oar shall find  
The taste of fish—the wild retreat  
  Of timeless nature's mind.  
In every stream, in every sea,  
  There are hidden shores we cannot see.

- George Meredith
