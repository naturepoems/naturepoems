The gentle rains, they cleanse the earth,
Awakening life with each rebirth.
A symphony of freshness, worth,
In every drop, a prayer of mirth.

Meadows lush with morning dew,
The sky adorned in cobalt blue.
In nature's arms, our hearts are true,
A sacred bond between us two.

- Sigrid Unset
