Whispers through the pine trees,  
  A song upon the breeze,  
  The golden sun is falling,  
  The world is at its ease.

  Shadows wrap the valleys,  
  While crickets start to play,  
  The magic of the twilight,  
  As night claims back the day.

- Marian McCulloch
