Who hath not seen thee oft amid thy store?  
Sometimes, whoever seeks abroad may find  
In the long winter night, light out of door,  
How much the sun hath shone upon thy kind.  
For when the moon is new, the stars are bright,  
The daffodils awaken with delight.

- Christina Rossetti
