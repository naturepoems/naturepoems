Tell me not in mournful numbers,
Life is but an empty dream!
For the woods and fields and rivers,
Whisper stories, reign supreme.

Nature’s voice calls high and clear,
In her arms, there's no more fear,
Every leaf and every stone,
Speaks of life, not lived alone.
