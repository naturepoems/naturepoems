Shadows on the Hills  
Beneath the stroke of twilight  
   where silence drapes the hills,  
idling shadows sprout from earth,  
   standing tall against horizons,  
waiting like promises  
   that dissolve into dusk,  
clothed in the dreams  
   of fading light.

- Louise Bogan
