Inversnaid
This darksome burn, horseback brown,
His rollrock highroad roaring down,
In silver, is the blithe stream’s don.
Cygnet by a ricking black-cath’ed line,
Divided glows, where wildflowers kneel.

--  Gerard Manley Hopkins
