In morning’s light, the mist unfurls,
A gentle shroud on waking trees,
As nature’s breath, a dance of pearls,
Embraces all upon the breeze.

The world awakens slow and sweet,
In harmony with day’s first light;
With every heartbeat, life’s retreat,
Prepares for what the day ignites.

- Marjorie Barnard
