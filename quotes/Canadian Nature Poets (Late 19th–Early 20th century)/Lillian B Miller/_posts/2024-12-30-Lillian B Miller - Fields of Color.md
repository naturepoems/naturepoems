A gentle breeze strokes rippling fields,
As clouds drift soft and free,
The dance of life unfolds;
In every petal, see.

Colors burst like laughter,
In the fading twilight glow,
Nature’s vibrant tapestry,
In every gust, it grows.

- Lillian B Miller
