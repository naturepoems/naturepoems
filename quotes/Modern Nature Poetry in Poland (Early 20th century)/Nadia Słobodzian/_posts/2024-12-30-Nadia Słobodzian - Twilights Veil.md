Twilight descends like a veil,  
the sky painted in hues of fire,  
a brief farewell to the sun,  
retreating behind the hills.  

Stars awaken, shyly peeking,  
as the world holds its breath  
in the magic of the dusk,  
a promise of the night.

- Nadia Słobodzian
