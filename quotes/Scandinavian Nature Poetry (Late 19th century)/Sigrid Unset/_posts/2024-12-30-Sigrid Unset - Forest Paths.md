Through forests dense, the pathways wind,
Each turn reveals what we may find.
A world of wonder, intertwined,
In every creature, every kind.

Wildflowers bloom in vibrant hues,
Their fragrant scents the heart renews.
Nature's palette, fresh and true,
A canvas vast for me and you.

- Sigrid Unset
