I stand in awe upon the hill,
Where sweeping vistas greet the eye,
The clouds drift by, the seasons change,
While daisies grow and eagles fly.

The quiet giant woods enfold,
Their history etched in bark and leaf,
Each moment there—a tale untold,
A whisper of nature’s belief.

- Henry David Thoreau
