The fog unfolds, 
I search for solitude
between the valleys.
Mountains rise, stooped
under the weight
of a sleeping sun.
I breathe deeply,
the fragrance of the pines,
as if each breath
opens a door
to the unknown.

- Giuseppe Ungaretti
