Through the whispering pine,
In shadows soft and deep,
The forest holds its secrets,
In silence, strong and steep.

A brook sings soft and low,
Its tale of stone and leaf,
While sunlight flickers through,
In a waltz of joy and grief.


- Ragnar Knutson
