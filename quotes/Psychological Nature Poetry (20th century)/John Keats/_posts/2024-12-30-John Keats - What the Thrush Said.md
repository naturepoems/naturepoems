What the Thrush Said

O Fool! I heard his call, too late,
And brought what darkness I could create;
With blessings thin in Solstice stay,
   Leaves and fruit to guard through day:
Let the thicket envy in its strife
For running water is a well of life!

       John Keats

- John Keats
