To a Snail

  A snail is a garden,
  is humble magic;  
  the noise of air
tries painting architecture  
    and view to cease.
    You stir with a light
  under your bronze car.  
  Such rendering is unforced
   and delicate upon.
  ----

- Marianne Moore
