In the quiet of the forest,  
Amidst the rustling leaves,  
The gentle whispers beckon me,  
To the heart where nature weaves.  

Soft the moss beneath my feet,  
A carpet green and small,  
Where shadows dance with dappled light,  
Nature's quiet call.

- L W Lydia H W Milligan
