Oh, green trees,
spirits of the wood,
with your arms
outstretched in prayer,
you whisper the secrets
of the universe
to the wandering winds.
In your shade,
i find peace,
the rustling of leaves
sings melodies
to my soul,
soothing the burdens
of my restless heart.

- Corrado Govoni
