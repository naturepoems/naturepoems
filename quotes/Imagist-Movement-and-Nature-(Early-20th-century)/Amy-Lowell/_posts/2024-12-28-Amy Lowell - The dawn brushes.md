The dawn brushes gently,
The waking sky,
With ribbons of light,
That unfurl slowly,
A dusky blue,
Turns into gold,
As the sun rises,
Over the silhouettes,
Of the hills,
The soft sighing,
Of the day’s first breath,
A promise of warmth,
And endless possibilities.

— Amy Lowell
