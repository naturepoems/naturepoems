A Kiss

A bird sings  
the way
that a kiss

is a kind
of prayer
that you keep.

The sun loves
all and quiet,
drip-drip in window.

Summer days
hold the paper
and smile,
switching mouths
in evergreen.

I remember
your breath's sound,
a diamond let down
to the ground.

Philip Whalen

- Philip Whalen
