The autumn leaves in whispered sighs  
Upon the pathway softly fall,  
With every curl and every rise,  
They beckon as the shadows call.  

In russet tones and golden crown,  
They twirl and dance in breezes mild,  
A fleeting beauty, fragile gown,  
The earth wears with a tender smile.

- Afanasy Fet
