In the morning, fresh dew—  
drops of silver sun—  
glisten on the grass.  
The birds stretch their wings wide,  
fluttering in joyous dance  
upon branches of emerald green,  
where the wind whispers  
sweet secrets of life,  
carrying forth colors  
of spring ever bright.


- Aldo Palazzeschi
