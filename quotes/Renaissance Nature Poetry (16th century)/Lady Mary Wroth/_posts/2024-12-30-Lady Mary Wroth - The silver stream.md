The silver stream that sparkles bright,
In quiet waters flow,
Reflects the stars that light the night,
And all that's pure and low.

Like whispers soft, the zephyrs breeze,
They carry tales of yore,
While in that calm, my heart finds ease,
On Nature's tranquil shore.

- Lady Mary Wroth
