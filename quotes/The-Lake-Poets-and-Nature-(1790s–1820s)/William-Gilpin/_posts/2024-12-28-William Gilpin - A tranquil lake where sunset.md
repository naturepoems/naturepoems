A tranquil lake, where sunset glows,
In ripples kissed by gentle breeze;
And in the quiet distance shows,
The whisper of the trees.
Here time stands still; the heart is calm,
The spirit freely roams,
In Nature’s arms, a healing balm,
That welcomes us back home.

- William Gilpin
