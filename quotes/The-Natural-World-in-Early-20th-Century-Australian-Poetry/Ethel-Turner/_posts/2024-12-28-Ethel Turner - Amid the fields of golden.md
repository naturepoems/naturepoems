Amid the fields of golden grain,
With whispers in the wheat,
A song of earth, a sweet refrain,
Where sky and meadow meet.

There every flower and blade of grass,
A harmony does play,
In nature's grand and wondrous mass,
Where life blooms day by day.

- Ethel Turner
