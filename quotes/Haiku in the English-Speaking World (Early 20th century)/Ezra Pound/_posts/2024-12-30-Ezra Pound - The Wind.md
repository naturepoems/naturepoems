The Wind
When the wind blows
Over stubbled fields,
I see the world awaken.
Each seed takes life anew,
Where roots hold fast below.

The scurrying of leaves,
A soft rustle and flight,
The memories are dancing,
Where nature's heart beats right.


- Ezra Pound
