The softest hush of twilight,  
Falls gently o’er the glade,  
While shadows stretch and linger,  
In serenity displayed.  

As day gives way to nighttime,  
The stars they start to peek,  
A silent serenade of peace,  
In the stillness, nature speaks.

- Selma Lagerlöf
