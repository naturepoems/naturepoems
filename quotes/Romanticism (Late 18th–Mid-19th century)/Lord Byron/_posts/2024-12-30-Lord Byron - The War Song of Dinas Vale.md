The willow, enchantress!  
In quiet repose, whispers her song—  
In solitude thus to dwell the days long,  
Where peace takes root, and nature’s bosom sways!  
The air is soft, votive calm, new born,  
And dew-laden, fresh appeals tomorrow’s dawns.
