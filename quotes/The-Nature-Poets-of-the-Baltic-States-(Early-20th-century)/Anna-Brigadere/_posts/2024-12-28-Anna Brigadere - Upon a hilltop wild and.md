Upon a hilltop, wild and wide,
The world unfolds in nature’s pride,
With every sunrise, dreams take flight,
A canvas kissed by morning light.

The rustling leaves, the river’s flow,
Bring life to every seed we sow,
In harmony, our spirits soar,
In nature’s grace, forevermore.

- Anna Brigadere
