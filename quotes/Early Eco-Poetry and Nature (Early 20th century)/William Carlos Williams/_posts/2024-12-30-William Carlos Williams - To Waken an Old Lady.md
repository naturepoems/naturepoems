To Waken an Old Lady

Old age is

a surprise,

not a fable

of wanderers

among the winds.

how wonderful

it is to be

around—as

trees that shake

yellow buds

for the day

I may rejoice

as they do—.

No, bring me

the storm now,

the branches
..

- William Carlos Williams
