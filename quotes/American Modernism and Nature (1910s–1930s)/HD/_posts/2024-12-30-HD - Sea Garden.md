Sea Garden

We saw you drown
In the West’s fabled sea,
All night we watched
The bobbing lanterns,
And the swift moons,
You followed, through the 
Play of lilies, through the
Play of shadows, and you were
Lost, lost, lost, with this
Flood of dreams
     —H.D.
