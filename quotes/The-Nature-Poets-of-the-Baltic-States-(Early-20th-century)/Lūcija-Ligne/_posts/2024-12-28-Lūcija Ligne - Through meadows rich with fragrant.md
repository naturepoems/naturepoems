Through meadows rich with fragrant bloom,
The breeze carries sweet perfume,
A symphony of life, awake,
In every turn, the earth's heart aches.

Golden fields beneath the sun,
Each grain a testament undone,
A cycle wrought in toil and grace,
In nature's arms, we find our place.


- Lūcija Ligne
