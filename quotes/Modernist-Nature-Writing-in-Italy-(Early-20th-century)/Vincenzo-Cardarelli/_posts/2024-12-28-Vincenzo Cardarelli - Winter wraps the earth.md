Winter wraps the earth tightly,
ah, the cold breath of stillness,
as the world waits,
hidden under a quilt of snow.

Yet beneath this serene cover,
lies the pulse of life,
ready to awaken
with the kiss of spring.

- Vincenzo Cardarelli
