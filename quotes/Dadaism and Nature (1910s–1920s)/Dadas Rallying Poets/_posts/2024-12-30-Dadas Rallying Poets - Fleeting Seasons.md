Fleeting Seasons
Winter hushes the world in white,
Spring tickles the earth to rise,
Summer laughs in waves of heat,
Fall paints a canvas of goodbyes.

Each season a fleeting moment,
Like breaths drawn in silent prayer,
Nature’s cycle spins and dances,
Through the vivid seasons we share.


- Dadas Rallying Poets
