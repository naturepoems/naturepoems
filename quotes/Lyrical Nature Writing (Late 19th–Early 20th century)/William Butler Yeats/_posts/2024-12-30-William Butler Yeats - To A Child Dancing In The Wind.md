To A Child Dancing In The Wind
O sweet elemental wind,
That I could be
The pool, where you would drop
Your tiny golden feet.

But while the drum lets flee
Your footfall to my breast,
And I shall wait, till day is done,
To dance with you, child, and love you best.

- William Butler Yeats
