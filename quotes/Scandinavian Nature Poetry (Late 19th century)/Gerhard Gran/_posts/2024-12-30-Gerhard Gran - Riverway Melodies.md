A river flows through ancient stones,
With waters clear as summer's sky,
Each bend a melody of tones,
Where life emerges, swift and sly.

In eddies soft, my thoughts shall float,
Along the banks of time and grace,
In nature's arms, I find my note,
A harmony, a warm embrace.

- Gerhard Gran
