The Night

The night is deep and still,
Held close by the moon’s caress,
The stars twinkle in whispers,
A cover of velvet silk,
Where the world breathes easy,
Bathed in tranquility,
As dreams weave through the dark,
In tender harmony.

- Amy Lowell
