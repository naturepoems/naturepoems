In the rain, the grass
  that lifts,
  unerringly,
  like new blades,
  the true wind
  shaping the edges,
  makes me wonder again,
  can anything,
  even a shadow, 
  belong to nature? 

  It clings to its source,
  unable to let go of the light.

- William Carlos Williams
