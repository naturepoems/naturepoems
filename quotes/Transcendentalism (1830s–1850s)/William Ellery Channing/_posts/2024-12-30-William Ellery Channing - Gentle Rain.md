Oh, gentle rain, your tears cascade,
Upon the parched, dry earth,
With every drop, new life is made,
In every sound, new birth.

You kiss the ground, a lover's touch,
And wash away the pain,
In your embrace, I long for much,
To dance beneath your rain.
