A Forest Path
A winding path through verdant wood,
   Where silent shadows blend,
The mossy rocks in solitude,
   In quietude descend.

Through leafy routs of emerald hue,
   The sunlight softly streams,
It whispers secrets old and new,
   That linger in our dreams.


- James Russell Lowell
