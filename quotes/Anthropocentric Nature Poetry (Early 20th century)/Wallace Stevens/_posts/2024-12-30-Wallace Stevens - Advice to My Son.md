Advice to My Son

Get the feel of your first palm,take the feel
Of the first summer too, take the feel
Of the first mountain peak with its heights.

Only that is real.
The rest is unworthy.
Silence will keep you alive in these days
Of choice white.

--- Wallace Stevens

- Wallace Stevens
