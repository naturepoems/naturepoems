I Am  

I am — yet what I am none cares or knows;  
My friends forsake me like a memory lost;  
I am the self-consumer of my woes —  
They rise and vanish in oblivion’s host,  
Like shadows in love’s frenzied stoush;  
I am — yet what I am none cares or knows.
