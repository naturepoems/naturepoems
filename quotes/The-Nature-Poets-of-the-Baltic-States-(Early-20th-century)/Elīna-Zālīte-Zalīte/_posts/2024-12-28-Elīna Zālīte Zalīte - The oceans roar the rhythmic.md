The ocean's roar, the rhythmic tide,
Calls forth a timeless song,
With every wave that ebbs and glides,
I know where I belong.

The shore whispers tales of old,
Of sailors brave and true,
In its depths, the mysteries unfold,
As I breathe in the blue.

- Elīna Zālīte Zalīte
