The silence of the evening sky,
As stars awaken, one by one,
In stillness deep, I look and sigh,
The universe, my thoughts have spun.

In every twinkle, dreams unfold,
In cosmic dance, the heart must trust,
Within this vastness, stories told,
Connecting all, in earth and dust.

- Hermann Hesse
