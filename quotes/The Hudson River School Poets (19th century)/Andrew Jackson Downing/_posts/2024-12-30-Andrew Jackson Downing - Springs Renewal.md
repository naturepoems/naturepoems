Spring's Renewal
With the dawn of spring, life awakens,
In every hue and shape it appears,
The whispers of life in the breeze forsaken,
As nature smiles, dispelling our fears.
The crocus breaks through the frost's cold clasp,
And burst forth with joy, a vibrant clasp.

- Andrew Jackson Downing
