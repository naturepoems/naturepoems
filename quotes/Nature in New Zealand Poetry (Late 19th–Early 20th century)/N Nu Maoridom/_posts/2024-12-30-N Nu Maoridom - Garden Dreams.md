Garden Dreams

In every bloom, a story speaks,
Of sunlit days and raindrop weeks,
In colors bright, the garden breathes,
In tendrils soft, the heart believes.

With tender hands, I tend my plot,
In every sprout, a dream forgot,
To nurture life, create, inspire,
In nature's arms, my soul's desire.

- N Nu Maoridom
