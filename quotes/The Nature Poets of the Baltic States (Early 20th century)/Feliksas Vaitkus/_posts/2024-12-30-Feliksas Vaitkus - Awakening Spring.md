Spring awakens with a gentle hand,
Bringing forth life to the warming land,
The flowers bloom, colors ablaze,
Each petal a promise, a playful gaze.

The birds return with songs anew,
A symphony of joy breaks through,
In every bud, in every song,
Nature sings where I belong.


- Feliksas Vaitkus
