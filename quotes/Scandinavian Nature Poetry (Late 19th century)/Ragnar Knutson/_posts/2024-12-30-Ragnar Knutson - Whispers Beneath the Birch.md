Beneath the birch, I find my peace,
In dappled light and shade,
The world outside begins to cease,
In stillness, we’ve made.

Nature’s breath, a soothing balm,
That heals all wounds of time,
In quiet moments, there’s a calm,
A rhythm, soft as rhyme.


- Ragnar Knutson
