A gentle breeze, the trees do sway,
In rhythm soft and free,
While nature sings a sweet ballet,
For every heart to see.

The flowers bloom, the birds take flight,
In ceaseless, joyous throng,
In every heart, they sing goodnight,
To end the day with song.

- Ethel Turner
