Upon yon hill where shadows play,
The meadow is so green,
With every fragrant flower that grows,
In gardens bright and keen.

The sun sets low, its golden rays,
Begin to softly fade,
Yet in the air their beauty stays,
In twilight's soft parade.

- Lady Mary Wroth
