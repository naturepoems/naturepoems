The winter’s breath is icy,
Yet beauty still holds sway,
In the hues of frosted branches,
Where the silence claims the day.

Each crystal like a whisper,
A secret cold and bright,
In this world of still perfection,
Beneath the pale starlight.

- Catherine J W Munro
