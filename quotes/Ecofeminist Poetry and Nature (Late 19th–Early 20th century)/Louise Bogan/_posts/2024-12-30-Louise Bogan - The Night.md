The Night  
In gentle murmurations,  
   night draws near  
amidst the creeping shadows,  
  burying what was bright,  
as it gathers all the stray colors,  
   hand in hand,  
doors of thought unlock,  
   releasing the stars  
like softened murmurs of days gone by.

- Louise Bogan
