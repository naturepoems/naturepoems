In fields of golden grain,  
Underneath the sky so vast,  
I hear the whispers of the earth,  
Echoes from the past.  

The winds that weave through every stalk,  
Tell stories young and old,  
Of harvests shared and seasons changed,  
In whispers soft and bold.

- Selma Lagerlöf
