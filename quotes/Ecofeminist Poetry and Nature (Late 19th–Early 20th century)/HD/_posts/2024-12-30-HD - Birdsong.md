Oh, to be a bird!  
To soar through the currents  
Carrying songs of the sea  
Into the dawn eternal—  
Light as a feather,  
Pulsing with the heart  
Of the earth’s breath,  
Reveling in the liberty  
To dance with wind and sky.

- HD
