In the stillness of the evening,   
As the sun sinks low,   
The whisper of nature's breathing,   
Soft as falling snow.  
Clouds embracing innocence,   
Savings full and fair;   
The evening star a guidance,   
To the world so rare.

- William Cullen Bryant
