Let the woods envelop my heart,
As the sunbeams filter through,
In the hush between the branches,
Nature sings a song so true.

With every step upon the path,
I shed my worries, light as air,
For in the presence of the trees,
I find a peace beyond compare.

- Laura Riding Jackson
