The stars blink softly in velvet night,  
a hush falls gently, a sacred light.  
cradled in darkness, dreams take flight,  
and crickets serenade the tranquil sight.  
Under the cosmic dance, we find,  
the echoes of the universe, intertwined.

- Aldona Gustas
