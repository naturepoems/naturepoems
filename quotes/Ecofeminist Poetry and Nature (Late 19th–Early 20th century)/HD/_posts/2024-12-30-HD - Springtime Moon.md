I stood in the green light  
Of the springtime moon;  
It flashed  
From the silver sea  
That crowned the high cliffs  
Where the wild flowers  
Wove their colors through  
Every shadowed space.  
The night wind surged  
Like a gentle wave,  
Till it melted  
Into a waiting dawn.

- HD
