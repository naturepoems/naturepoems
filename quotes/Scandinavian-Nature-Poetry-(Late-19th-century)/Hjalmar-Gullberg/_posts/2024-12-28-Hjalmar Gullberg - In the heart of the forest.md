In the heart of the forest,  
Where wild things roam free,  
The calls of the ancients  
Are sung by the trees.  

Each rustle of branches,  
Each sigh of the pine,  
Is a welcome reminder  
Of the beauty divine.

- Hjalmar Gullberg
