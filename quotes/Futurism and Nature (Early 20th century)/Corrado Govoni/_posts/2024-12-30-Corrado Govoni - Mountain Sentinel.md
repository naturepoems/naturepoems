The mountain stands tall,
a sentinel of time,
a guardian of ancient whispers,
it cradles the clouds
and kisses the sun.
In its embrace,
I find strength;
each stone holds
millennia of secrets,
tales of storms and peace,
a tribute to endurance.

- Corrado Govoni
