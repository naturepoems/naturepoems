Below the horizon, far away,  
The swirling leaves beckon my attention;  
In every moment of drifting autumn,  
I join them in their dance,  
In every crook of the chilled air,  
I feel their mellow savor,  
And in every twisting vein,  
I embrace their flight.

- Walt Whitman
