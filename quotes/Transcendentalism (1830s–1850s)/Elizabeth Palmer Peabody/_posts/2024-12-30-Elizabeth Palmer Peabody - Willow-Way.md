Willow-Way

On the willow-way, I stroll,
With the flowers that bloom in the air,
They dance in grace, that whirl,
In the stilling, perfect care.

The shades of night, so sweet,
Open wide their arms,
As the stories of Life
Are whispered through charms.

- Elizabeth Palmer Peabody
