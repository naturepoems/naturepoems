One must give flowers their due —  
a sunlit passion  
of roses intertwined   
in heights unfigured.   

Among the petals lies the heart,  
that wild pulse   
of living --   
share this bright wonder,   
these sacred moments,   
this breathtaking essence,   
a blooming in the heart.

- Muriel Rukeyser

- Muriel Rukeyser
