The dawn of the close of the day,  
Where shadows of lilies bend low,  
A whispering wind sails away,  
And petals begin to show.  
They open to gather the sun,  
And laugh at the daft, dreary night;  
With joy, every dawn is begun,  
In this transient play of light.

- Gwendolyn Brooks
