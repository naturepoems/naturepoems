The flower that smiles to-day
To-morrow dies; 
All that we wish to stay
Tempts, and then flies.

What is this world’s delight?
Lighted on death; 
Shadows of morning, bright,
Shadows of night.

And we, that now could see,
To-morrow die; 
How may we ever be
Like them that fly?

--- John Clare, "The Flower that Smiles Today"
